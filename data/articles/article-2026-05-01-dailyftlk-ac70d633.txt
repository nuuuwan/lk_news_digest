Gnanasara Thero indicted over alleged anti-Islam remarks; released on bail

Indictments have been served yesterday before the Colombo High Court against Galagodaaththe Gnanasara Thero, General Secretary of Bodu Bala Sena, over an allegedly derogatory statement made on Islam.

The court ordered that Ven. Gnanasara Thero be released on bail following the serving of indictments.
