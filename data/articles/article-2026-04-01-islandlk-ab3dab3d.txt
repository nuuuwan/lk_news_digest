More value, more choice, and more lifestyle benefits with every cedit card transaction

NDB Bank announces the launch of NDB Rewards, an exciting new rewards programme designed to elevate the everyday spending experience of its credit cardholders. Effective 1st March 2026, NDB Rewards brings greater value, flexibility, and lifestyle privilege to customers through an easy-to-use, fully digital points-earning ecosystem.

Built around modern consumer lifestyles, NDB Rewards allows eligible Cardholders to earn Reward Points on day-to-day personal transactions and redeem them for a wide array of experiences and merchant offers. With transparent earning rules and a growing network of partners, the programme enables customers to enjoy more with every swipe, tap, or online purchase.

Earn More with Every Transaction -Through NDB Rewards, everyday purchases instantly become more rewarding. Customers can earn Points on eligible local and international credit card spends, with enhanced earning opportunities available across popular lifestyle categories such as dining , lodging & travel Depending on the card type, customers enjoy bonus multipliers—up to 5X on international spend and up to 2X on domestic lifestyle categories—allowing them to earn Points significantly faster where they spend most frequently.All Reward Points carry a clear and transparent value of LKR 1 per Point, making it simple for customers to understand the benefits they accumulate with each purchase.

Automatic Enrollment for Eligible Customers – Eligible Platinum and above NDB Credit Cardholders who maintain an active savings or current account with NDB will be automatically enrolled into NDB Rewards. No additional registration or activation is required, ensuring a seamless start to the programme from day one.All purchases made on Supplementary Cards will also contribute to the Primary Cardholder’s Rewards balance, helping families accumulate Points faster.

Redeem Points Across 180+ Lifestyle Partners – NDB Rewards brings together convenience, choice, and lifestyle value. Cardholders can redeem Points across a rapidly expanding network of over 180 clothing, dining, supermarkets, travel, lodging, and leisure partners through Thyaga.lk, NDB’s official rewards partner platform.
