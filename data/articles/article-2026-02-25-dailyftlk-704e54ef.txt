C.M.S. Jayawickrama appointed MD of Aitken Spence Hotel Holdings

Aitken Spence Hotel Holdings PLC has appointed C.M.S. Jayawickrama as Managing Director with effect from 1 April 2026. He is currently on the Board as an Executive Director.

Jayawickrama has had a career spanning over three decades with Aitken Spence and is the Managing Director of Aitken Spence Hotel Managements Ltd., which manages all hotels of the group. He serves on the Aitken Spence Group Management Council and on the Boards of most hotel companies in the group including that of Aitken Spence Hotel Holdings PLC.

He has more than three decades of experience in senior management positions in the group’s hotel sector and the tourism industry in Sri Lanka and overseas. Jayawickrama is a past Vice President of the Tourist Hotels Association of Sri Lanka (THASL).
