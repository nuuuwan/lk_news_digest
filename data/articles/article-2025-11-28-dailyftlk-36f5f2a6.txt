Sri Lanka Institute of Architects presents “A Christmas Wish” by The Old Joes’ Choir

Sri Lanka Institute of Architects (SLIA), which has been established by an Act of Parliament to govern the education and the practice of Architecture in Sri Lanka, the nation’s leading professional body governing architecture and the advancement of built environment of Sri Lanka, presents, “A Christmas Wish” in collaboration with The Old Joes’ Choir. This award-winning Sri Lankan group is celebrated for its rich harmonies and musical versatility. The concert promises an unforgettable evening of harmony, warmth, and festive cheer. It will be held today (28 November) at The Stables, Park Street Mews, at 7:30 p.m. setting the stage for a night that marks the start of the Christmas season.

As a choir with a longstanding tradition of musical excellence, their concert will stay true to itself, bringing to life its hallmark versatility and dynamic approach across varied genres. This Christmas, The OJC is set to take audiences on a musical journey that blends cherished traditions with the spirit of the season.

Guests can look forward to a relaxed, festive atmosphere, with an evening that aims to set the tone for the holidays. For tickets, please visit https://slia.lk/ticket_booking/ or call Sri Lanka Institute of Architects on 0112-689777 or 0112-689888 from 9.30 a.m. – 5.30 p.m. on weekdays.
