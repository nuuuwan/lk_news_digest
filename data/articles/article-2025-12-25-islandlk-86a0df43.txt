Flemingo Duty Free partners with global initiative to support Cancer Hospital

Flemingo Duty Free has pledged support to the National Cancer Hospital (Apeksha Hospital) in Maharagama through a partnership with The Moodie Davitt Report’s global KickCancerThon initiative. The commitment was announced at the ICA International Marathon in Colombo on December 14, 2025.

The donation was formally handed over by Deputy High Commissioner of India, Dr. Satyanjal Pandey, to Dr. Randima Nanayakkara, Consultant Oncosurgeon. The funds are designated for the hospital’s Surgical Department to aid patient care.

“This initiative represents a meaningful step in supporting local communities and advancing cancer awareness,” stated P.K. Thimmayya, Director of Flemingo Duty Free.
