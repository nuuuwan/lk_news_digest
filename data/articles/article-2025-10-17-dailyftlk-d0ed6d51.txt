Harini, Jaishankar discuss education, capacity building cooperation between SL, India

Indian External Affairs Minister Dr. S. Jaishankar yesterday met with Prime Minister Dr. Harini Amarasuriya in New Delhi, reaffirming India’s continued support to Sri Lanka and commitment to strengthening bilateral cooperation.

In a post on ‘X,’ Dr. Jaishankar said he was “pleased to meet” the Sri Lankan Prime Minister. “Discussed India’s continued support to Sri Lanka and strengthening our cooperation in education & capacity building,” he added.
