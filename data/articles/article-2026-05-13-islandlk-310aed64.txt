Hi! Got a few more easy-to-do beauty tips for my lovely readers.

Grind 01 tablespoon oats into a soft powder. Add 01 teaspoon of honey to it and mix in a small amount of water, or milk, until it forms a paste.

Wash your face with lukewarm water and apply the scrub, gently using circular motions.

Massage for about 01 minute, leave it on for another 05 minutes and then rinse with cool water and pat dry.

Benefits: Gently removes dead skin cells, helps soften rough skin, reduces dryness caused by sun exposure; honey helps keep skin moisturised, and oats soothe irritated skin.

Wash a fresh ripe tomato well and then cut and mash it into pulp.

Clean your face and then spread the tomato pulp evenly on sun-damaged areas.

Leave it on for 10 minutes, rinse with cool water, and then apply a light moisturiser.

Benefits: Helps refresh dull skin, gives skin a fresh, bright look and helps cool overheated skin.

Avoid If: Your skin is very sensitive, or have cuts, peeling, or irritation.

Brew a green tea bag, or 01 teaspoon green tea in a cup of hot water for 05 minutes.

Allow it to cool completely and then soak a cotton pad, or soft cloth, in the tea, and place the pads gently on your face or affected areas.

Leave for about 10 minutes, then remove the cotton pad and let skin air dry naturally.

Benefits: Helps calm redness, refreshes tired skin, provides antioxidants for skin recovery, can reduce heat and irritation after sun exposure, and helps hydrate and soothe skin.

How Often to Use: Daily or whenever your skin feels irritated
