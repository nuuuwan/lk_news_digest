China donates 85 MT of flood relief supplies

China yesterday delivered a major humanitarian relief consignment to Sri Lanka to support recovery efforts following the devastation caused by Cyclone Ditwah, which swept across the island recently.

A total of 85 tonnes of flood relief material, including life jackets, tents, blankets and sheets, arrived in Colombo this morning and was officially handed over by the Ambassador of China to Sri Lanka Qi Zhenhong.
