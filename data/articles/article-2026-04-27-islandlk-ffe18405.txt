Third Japanese Management Training Programme in May

RMD Consultants (Pvt) Ltd, a leading HR consultancy specializing in human resources management, has announced its 3rd public Japanese Management Training Programme (MTP), starting 6th May 2026. This programme also marks the 60th MTP batch conducted in Sri Lanka, reinforcing RMD’s reputation in management development and organizational excellence.

The six-week programme, held at a leading four-star hotel in Colombo, spans 64 hours of blended learning over six consecutive Wednesdays. It combines interactive workshops, guided assignments, and 17 video-based case studies to ensure practical application. Key topics include management principles, problem-solving and creativity, improvement & management of work, building trust-based relationships, leadership, and human behaviour. The programme will be led by Rohan Pandithakorralage, Managing Director of RMD Consultants and former CHRO at Aitken Spence PLC, who has successfully trained more than 5,000 managers to obtain the license to manage across Southeast Asia, many of whom now hold prominent leadership positions in local and international organizations. As a founder fellow member, Rohan Pandithakorralage has also played an active role in establishing the Association of HR Professionals (AHRP) in Sri Lanka.

“RMD Consultants specializes in delivering data-driven, practical, and results-oriented HR solutions,” said Rohan Pandithakorralage. “Our goal is to help organizations prevent costly HR mistakes, strengthen their people systems, and build productive, engaged, and future-ready workplaces.”
