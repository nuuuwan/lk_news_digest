Deen Brothers bags double Gold at FCCISL Entrepreneur of the Year 2025

Deen Brothers Imports (Pvt) Ltd, one of Sri Lanka’s most trusted importers of agricultural, industrial, mechanical, technical tools and electric appliances, emerged as a double Gold winner at the prestigious Entrepreneur of the Year Awards 2025, organized by the Federation of Chambers of Commerce and Industry of Sri Lanka (FCCISL). At this year’s ceremony, DBL achieved two of the highest accolades: Southern Province – Extra Large Gold Award and National Level – Extra Large Gold Award.

This remarkable recognition further strengthens Deen Brothers’ reputation as an industry leader with a robust distribution network covering Sri Lanka and the Maldives. The company continues to represent globally acclaimed brands including Oregon, Dongcheng, Shimge, Briggs & Stratton, Toptul, Golden Bridge, and Norton. Under the trusted DBL brand, the company markets agricultural, industrial, carpentry and construction tools, power tools, generators, hardware, spare parts, and household electrical appliances, all backed with comprehensive warranties and exceptional after-sales service. Reflecting on this success, Dr. M. N. M. Najeeb Deen, Managing Director of Deen Brothers Imports (Pvt) Ltd, stated: “Our philosophy has always been to provide the right tools with responsibility and deliver the best quality equipment to our customers. Receiving these two Gold awards at national and provincial levels in 2025 is a moment of immense pride for all of us at Deen Brothers.”
