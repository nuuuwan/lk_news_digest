Anunine Holdings donates Rs. 25 m to ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund

Daily contributions continue to be made to the Government’s ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund established to provide relief to those affected by Cyclone Ditwah.

Anunine Holdings has made a financial donation of Rs. 25 million to the fund.

The relevant cheque was handed over recently by Anunine Holdings Chairman Anurath Aberathna. A group including the company’s managing directors was present at the occasion.
