Groundworth launches ‘Legado’ land development in Homagama

Groundworth (Pvt) Ltd has unveiled “Legado by Groundworth,” an exclusive land development in Homagama, a key suburb under the Western Megapolis initiative. The project offers just eight premium plots of 10 perches each, with prices starting at LKR 1.2 million per perch.

Located 4.5 km from the Kottawa Interchange and 3 km from Homagama Junction, Legado provides excellent connectivity to Colombo via the Southern Expressway. Nearby amenities include hospitals, top schools, and shopping outlets.

Kasun Andrahennadi, Co-Founder of Groundworth, said the development reflects a long-term vision for sustainable growth. Buyers receive 1st Class Bimsawiya titles for added security. Site visits are now open.
