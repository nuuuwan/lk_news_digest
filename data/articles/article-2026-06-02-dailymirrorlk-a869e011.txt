Bakthi Gee at Gangaramaya

A Bakthi Gee programme was held yesterday at the Gangaramaya Temple in view of the Buddha Rashmi National Vesak Festival. Renowned Sri Lankan musician, singer, composer and occasional actor Edward Jayakody, along with Charitha Priyadarshani Peiris, performed at the event. Pix by Nimalsiri Edirisinghe
