Full’r Burgers opens 11th outlet in Malabe

Sri Lanka’s fast-growing burger brand, Full’r Burgers, has expanded its footprint with the opening of its 11th outlet, located at 757/C, Kaduwela Road, North Thalangama, Malabe. The new outlet strengthens the brand’s presence in one of Colombo’s rapidly developing suburban hubs.

Known for its bold flavours, indulgent burger creations, and vibrant brand personality, Full’r Burgers has quickly built a loyal following among burger lovers across the city. The Malabe outlet aims to bring the brand’s signature range of burgers and loaded sides closer to the area’s growing residential and student communities.

Full’r Burgers is a brand under Giga Foods (Pvt) Ltd, a homegrown food and beverage company that continues to expand its portfolio of innovative dining concepts across Sri Lanka.

Commenting on the expansion, Chief Commercial Officer Revan Gunewardene stated,

“The opening of our Malabe outlet represents another important milestone for Full’r Burgers as we continue to expand our presence and make the brand more accessible to customers. The strong response we have received across our outlets encourages us to keep growing while staying true to the bold flavours and energetic spirit that define Full’r.”

With multiple outlets already operating across Colombo and its suburbs, the addition of the Malabe location further reinforces Full’r Burgers’ position as one of Sri Lanka’s rapidly growing quick-service restaurant brands.
