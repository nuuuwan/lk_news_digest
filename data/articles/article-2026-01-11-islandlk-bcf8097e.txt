Pasad Weerasekera appointed to the Board of AgriOne Solutions

AgriOne Solutions (Pvt) Ltd, a subsidiary of Asiya Siyaka Commodities PLC, has announced the appointment of Pasad Weerasekera to its Board of Directors.

A seasoned professional with over 33 years in the agri-input sector, Weerasekera brings a wealth of expertise in finance, marketing, operations, and strategic leadership. In his new role, he will collaborate closely with the AgriOne leadership team to strengthen strategic direction, enhance operational execution, and drive long-term value creation.

Commenting on his appointment, Weerasekera stated, “AgriOne is committed to delivering value to all stakeholders by empowering farming communities with greater resilience and productivity. We will focus on strengthening partnerships across the agricultural value chain and ensuring that our growth is underpinned by responsible and sustainable practices.”

Weerasekera’s distinguished career began at the Ceylon Tobacco Company’s fertiliser division. In 1993, he was pivotal in establishing CIC Agri Business, serving as its Finance Head and contributing significantly to its ascent as a market leader. He later played a foundational role in launching AgStar PLC in 2002 as Finance Director, advancing to Chief Operating Officer and ultimately to Managing Director, a position he held for more than twelve years.
