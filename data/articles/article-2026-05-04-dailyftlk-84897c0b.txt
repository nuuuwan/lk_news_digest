St. Servatius’ College Matara, Colombo OBA AGM

The 49th Annual General Meeting (AGM) of the St. Servatius’ College, Matara Old Boys’ Association (OBA – Colombo branch) will be held at the Mercantile Cricket Association, Colombo, on 7 and 8 May from 6 p.m. onwards.

For further details, please contact the Joint Secretaries Sajeewa Sapukotana (071 077 9779) or Maj. Vibeeshan Perera (071 436 3673).
