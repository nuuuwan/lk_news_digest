SLFPA unveils 23rd edition of Profood Propack & Agbiz Knowledge Hub

The highly anticipated event on Sri Lanka’s food industry calendar, Profood Propack & Agbiz Knowledge Hub 2026, organised by the Sri Lanka Food Processors Association (SLFPA) in partnership with Lanka Exhibition and Conference Services (LECS), is scheduled to take place from 21 to 23 August 2026 at BMICH, Colombo.

The announcement was made during the award ceremony of the 22nd edition, held on 10 March at Cinnamon Grand Colombo, where industry leaders and key stakeholders convened to celebrate excellence within the sector. The event provided an ideal platform for SLFPA to outline its vision for the future, unveiling plans for a more expansive and impactful edition in the year ahead.

Endorsed by the Industries Ministry, the National Agribusiness Council, and the Institute of Food Science and Technology Sri Lanka (IFSTSL), Profood Propack 2026 has served as SLFPA’s flagship platform for over 25 years, advancing Sri Lanka’s food sector and supporting wider economic growth. The exhibition unites leaders from agriculture, food processing, packaging, and allied industries, fostering innovation, knowledge exchange, and strategic collaboration.

Speaking at the event, SLFPA President Thusith Wijesinghe said: “Profood Propack & Agbiz Knowledge Hub 2026 continues to set the benchmark for Sri Lanka’s food industry. By bringing together key local and international stakeholders, the exhibition fosters innovation, collaboration, and growth across the sector.”

Profood Propack connects distributors, manufacturers, suppliers, and industry professionals, showcasing the latest advancements in food and beverage production, processing, packaging machinery, and hospitality sector. The exhibition also highlights operational efficiency, smart manufacturing, and sustainable practices to meet the evolving needs of the modern food industry.

Profood Propack 2026 Organising Committee Co-Chairmen Nadishan Guruge and Sameera Jayathilake stated: “This year, our emphasis is on empowering SMEs and entrepreneurs while facilitating strategic partnerships. With enhanced facilities and international participation, the exhibition promises valuable insights and opportunities for all. Under the Knowledge Hub, we will plan industry-related seminars for our members.”

The event offers strong B2B networking, enabling partnerships, trade agreements, and market expansion. It provides a platform for startups, SMEs, and established brands to grow, while international exhibitors bring global expertise and innovation, strengthening Sri Lanka’s position as a regional food and packaging hub.

Knowledge sharing remains a central focus this year. The expanded Knowledge Hub, alongside live demonstrations and expert-led sessions, supports skill development and industry insight, while a dedicated Consumer Assistance Desk guides emerging entrepreneurs in exploring opportunities and navigating the business landscape effectively.

Profood Propack also holds significant economic and national relevance, contributing to the growth of Sri Lanka’s food, beverage, packaging, and agriculture industries while supporting export development, tourism, and employment generation. It aligns closely with national objectives for industrial development and value addition.

Bringing together trade, innovation, and expertise, Profood Propack & Agbiz Knowledge Hub 2026 will strengthen Sri Lanka’s role as a regional hub for the food, beverage, and packaging sectors. Join industry leaders, innovators, entrepreneurs, and stakeholders to position the business at the forefront of the island’s dynamic food and packaging landscape.
