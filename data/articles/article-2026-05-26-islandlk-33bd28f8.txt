CameraLK and Sony unveil the revolutionary Alpha a7R VI

CameraLK, Sri Lanka’s leading camera retail giant and Official Sony Distributor in Sri Lanka, unveiled the highly anticipated Sony Alpha a7R VI and FE 100-400mm F4.5 GM OSS lens at a landmark regional launch event held in Sri Lanka. The event marked a historic milestone for the country’s photography and videography industry, as Sri Lanka was chosen to host the premier regional launch of the groundbreaking new imaging technology, further cementing the nation’s growing reputation within the global creative community.

The exclusive launch event brought together some of Sri Lanka’s leading photographers, videographers, content creators, and visual storytellers for an immersive evening celebrating creativity, innovation, and the future of imaging technology. Organized by CameraLK, the event showcased Sony’s latest advancements in camera engineering while highlighting the rapid evolution and growing potential of Sri Lanka’s creative industry.
