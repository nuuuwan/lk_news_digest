Vehicle importers meet in Kandy

Officials and members of the Vehicle Importers Association of Sri Lanka (VIASL), together with used vehicle importers from the Kandy region, attend an industry gathering at Kandy Myst by Cinnamon on Saturday (13). The meeting was organised to strengthen collaboration within the sector and discuss key challenges related to vehicle imports, taxation, regulatory requirements, market conditions and other industry-related issues. Participants also exchanged views on measures to promote the sustainable growth and future development of Sri Lanka's vehicle import industry.
