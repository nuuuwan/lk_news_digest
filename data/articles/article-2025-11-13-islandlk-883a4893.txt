Curtin University Colombo welcomes Professor Jeff Brooks

Professor Jeff S. Brooks, Head of the School of Education at Curtin University, Australia, recognised as one the world’s leading voices in educational leadership and social justice, visited Colombo sharing a message of collaboration, courage, and change. His discussions with Sri Lankan educators opened new pathways for academic partnership and leadership innovation, reasserting Curtin University’s commitment to global impact through education.

His visit to Sri Lanka provided a platform for knowledge exchange, emphasising the importance of global partnerships to address shared educational challenges.

During his time in Sri Lanka, Professor Jeff Brooks engaged with a wide range of institutions and stakeholders, highlighting the importance of collaboration in advancing education. At Curtin University Colombo, he met with the leadership team and held in‑depth discussions with faculty and staff. These exchanges focused on strengthening academic development and exploring new avenues for institutional partnerships, strengthening the role of Curtin Colombo as a bridge between Sri Lanka and Curtin University in Perth.
