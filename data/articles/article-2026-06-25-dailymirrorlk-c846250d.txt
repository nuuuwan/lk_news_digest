Prime Residencies forges ahead with Mon Vie construction

Prime Lands Residencies PLC announced that construction is actively underway on Mon Vie, a landmark ultra-luxury residential development located at Thalakotuwa Gardens, Colombo 5.

Featuring never been seen developments in Sri Lankan residential architecture, including the country’s first-ever cantilevered viewing deck and its first floating rooftop restaurant, the project’s scope reflects its broader ambition to bring a genuinely new kind of living experience to Colombo.

Conceived under the defining philosophy of ‘The art of family living, perfected’, Mon Vie is designed for discerning modern families who seek both the refinement of contemporary urban living and the warmth of a genuine home.

Rising Ground + 25 floors, with a signature rooftop, Mon Vie will offer an exclusive collection of two-bedroom and three-bedroom luxury apartments, with prices starting from Rs.86 million.

Mon Vie’s crowning glory will be its floating rooftop restaurant, Sri Lanka’s first of its kind, conceived not only as an amenity for residents but as a gourmet destination of its own right, offering panoramic views of Colombo against a backdrop befitting the city’s most exceptional address in Colombo 5.

Positioned within one of Colombo 5’s most sought-after location in Thalakotuwa Gardens, the residents will also enjoy immediate proximity to the city’s leading international schools, hospitals and key commercial destinations, placing everything that matters within instant reach.

Over 40,000 square feet - equivalent to one acre of land - has been dedicated to curated leisure amenities within the project. Every detail of its development, from spatial planning and material selection to communal amenities and surrounding greenery, has been thoughtfully curated to serve the needs of families who place equal value on comfort, aesthetics and connectivity. The piling work for the development is being undertaken by San Piling (Pvt.) Ltd, a team of highly qualified professionals committed to delivering strong foundations and exceptional service.

With completion estimated for April 2030, the project represents a significant milestone in Colombo’s evolving residential landscape. An attractive four-year payment plan is also available for the project, offering greater flexibility and convenience for prospective homeowners and investors alike.

Currently in its pre-launch pricing phase, Mon Vie is not only poised to set a new benchmark for family-centred luxury living in the capital but also break new ground in residential luxury architecture, effectively positioning Prime Lands Residencies among the country’s most esteemed visionaries in real estate development.
