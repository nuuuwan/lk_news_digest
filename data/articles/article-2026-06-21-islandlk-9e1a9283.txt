Sri Lanka’s enterprise leaders chart course for secure AI adoption at Innovation Forum 2026

Aligned with Sri Lanka’s National Digital Economy Strategy 2030, the IBM Innovation Forum Sri Lanka 2026 brought together senior business leaders, technology decision-makers, and government representatives to chart a practical path for enterprise AI adoption. Held on June 5 at The Studio, Cinnamon Life City of Dreams, the flagship event explored how organisations can move beyond experimentation to operationalise AI securely and at scale.

Under the theme “Building Secure, AI-Driven, and Autonomous Enterprises in Sri Lanka,” the forum featured in-depth discussions on cybersecurity, infrastructure modernisation, data protection, operational resilience, and governance. Industry experts emphasised that Sri Lankan enterprises are entering a defining phase where technology investments must translate into measurable business outcomes.

Cem Borhan, CEO – ASEAN & South Asia, Redington, noted that the collaboration with IBM reflects a shared commitment to helping enterprises innovate responsibly and unlock long-term growth. “Through the IBM Innovation Forum, we are bringing together leaders from across government, industry, and technology to exchange insights and enable practical pathways for AI-driven transformation,” he said.
