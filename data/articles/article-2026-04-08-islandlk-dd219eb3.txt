Tomato Face Packs …

Mix a mashed tomato with a squeeze of lemon juice and a teaspoon of honey and apply on face (avoiding eyes). Leave for 15 minutes and then rinse with cool water. (For dry skin, substitute honey with plain yoghurt.)

Mix a mashed tomato with a teaspoon of baking soda (avoiding sensitive areas), and apply and leave for 10 minutes, and then rinse thoroughly.

Mix a mashed tomato with a teaspoon of gram flour, and a touch of honey, and apply this pack and leave for 15 minutes, and then rinse with lukewarm water.

Mix a mashed tomato with a few drops of almond oil, and apply gently under the eyes, and leave for 15 minutes, and then rinse with cool water.

Mash a ripe tomato and mix with a tablespoon of plain yoghurt, and apply, and leave for 15 minutes, and then rinse with cool water.

Mash a ripe tomato and mix with a tablespoon of olive oil or avocado oil, and apply and let it remain for 15 minutes, and then rinse with cool water.

Mash a ripe tomato and mix with a teaspoon of honey and a few drops of lemon juice, and apply and let it remain for 15 minutes, and then rinse with cool water.
