Samsung Sri Lanka launches Galaxy S26 Series with advanced AI

Samsung Sri Lanka has unveiled the Galaxy S26 series, introducing agentic AI designed to proactively assist users throughout their daily lives. Available from March 16, the lineup includes the S26 Ultra, S26+, and S26.

Key AI features include Call Screening, Now Nudge for contextual suggestions, and enhanced Bixby with real-time web search. Privacy is reinforced by Samsung Knox security.

The S26 Ultra features a custom processor with a 39% faster NPU. Launch benefits worth up to Rs. 235,580 include screen replacement coverage and a Dialog data bundle. Flexible financing is available through Samsung Easy Pay.
