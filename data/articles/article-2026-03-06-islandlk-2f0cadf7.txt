Global Consumer Products earns ISO 9001:2015 certification

Reinforcing its position as one of the leading hygienic disposable manufacturers in Sri Lanka and reaffirming its commitment to quality, Global Consumer Products (Pvt) Ltd. (GCP) has received the ISO 9001:2015 certification from the Sri Lanka Standards Institution (SLSI).

Global Consumer Products (Pvt) Ltd. holds the unique distinction of being the only manufacturer in Sri Lanka producing soft tissues, oxo-biodegradable, LDPE and HDPE garbage bags, and food care products certified by Sri Lanka Standards Institution (ISO 9001:2015 QMS Certified by SLSI). The company has set the gold standard in the industry, backed by a remarkable legacy spanning over four decades of excellence and compliance.
