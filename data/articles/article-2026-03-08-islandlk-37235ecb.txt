ComBank and David Pieris Automobiles join forces for attractive leasing packages

Commercial Bank of Ceylon has entered a strategic partnership with David Pieris Automobiles (Pvt) Ltd to offer specially structured leasing packages for GWM and BAIC vehicles, the companies announced.

Under the collaboration, Commercial Bank will provide leasing facilities through its island-wide branch network for selected GWM and BAIC models. Customers benefit from attractive interest rates, concessions on documentation charges, and a complimentary credit card with the first-year annual fee waived. Repayment schedules can be tailored to individual income patterns for more manageable ownership.

David Pieris Automobiles, the four-wheeler sales arm of the David Pieris Group, markets the BAIC X55, BJ30, and BJ40, alongside GWM’s Haval H6 HEV, H6 PHEV, H6 GT PHEV, TANK 300 HEV, and TANK 500 HEV. The company adds value with free registration and two complimentary labour services.

The partnership is underpinned by comprehensive warranties. GWM hybrids feature engine warranties up to six years or 200,000 kilometres and battery warranties extending to eight years or 160,000 kilometres. BAIC models carry three-year or 100,000-kilometre engine warranties.
