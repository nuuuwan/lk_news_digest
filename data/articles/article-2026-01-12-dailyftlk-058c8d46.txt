SMIB contributes Rs. 5 m to ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund

The State Mortgage and Investment Bank (SMIB) has made a financial contribution of Rs. 5 million to the Government’s Rebuilding Sri Lanka Fund. The cheque was formally handed over at the Presidential Secretariat by SMIB Chairman Maheel Kuragama to Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake. SMIB General Manager and Chief Executive Officer Indika Thushara Asuramanne along with A. W. Perera, were present at the occasion.
