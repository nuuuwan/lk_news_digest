ANC and Achievers Lanka enhance academic pathways

ANC Education and Achievers Lanka Business School have partnered to create a seamless academic pathway for professional qualification holders. Students completing CIMA at Achievers Lanka can now progress to a Top-up Degree or an MBA through ANC Education in partnership with the University of West London, UK. This collaboration integrates professional credentials with internationally recognized academic awards to enhance graduates’ global employability.

Leadership from both institutions highlighted the partnership’s strategic value. According to Azad Hye, Chairman of Achievers Lanka, it combines Achievers’ excellence with ANC’s academic standing to shape globally competitive professionals. CEO Lahiru Karunarathna noted the collaboration provides students a clear pathway for global success. ANC Education CEO Dayan Fernando stated the partnership accelerates the transition from professional credentials to globally recognized degrees, reflecting a shared vision for developing future-ready graduates.
