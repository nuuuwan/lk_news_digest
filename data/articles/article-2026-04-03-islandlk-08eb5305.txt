Vector Agents announces digital worker platform

Vector Agents announced its updated Digital Worker Platform, built for what comes next: the age of digital employees, a new category of enterprise software designed to bring AI-powered digital teammates into the core of everyday business operations.

While the platform has already been in use with early customers, this announcement marks Vector Agents’ official public introduction and declaration of its long-term vision: a future where every department has powerful AI digital workers alongside human professionals making it a fundamental layer of how organizations operate, scale, and compete.

Vector Agents builds role-based digital employees that understand business context, follow workflows, and operate inside existing systems. Unlike traditional automation tools or chatbots, they are built to execute multi-step, end-to-end workflows across functions such as sales, finance, customer success, and HR, working continuously and reliably as part of the team. They don’t just assist with work, they carry out operational responsibilities inside real business workflows, acting as a new, dependable workforce layer inside modern organizations.

“We believe every function in every company will have its own AI teammate,” said Jeevan Gnanam, CEO of Vector Agents. The company’s vision, “Digital Workers, Human Outcomes,” reflects a shift in how organizations will evolve: not by treating AI as a side utility, but by embedding digital workers directly into teams and core processes, as a reliable part of how work gets done.”

Vector Agents currently offers four role-based digital workers across core functions: Lilian for Sales, Bradley for Finance, Rhea for Customer Success, and Blake for HR. Each is designed to integrate into existing workflows, learn company-specific processes, and execute work reliably alongside human teams. Amplifying their outputs, productivity and operational capacity.

Lilian and Rhea are Vector Agents’ first publicly available digital employees. Lilian is an AI SDR that defines your ICP using hundreds of data points and intent signals, researches each account to generate deep prospect intelligence, and runs highly personalized outreach campaigns 24/7. Rhea is an always-on Customer Success Agent that understands your products and processes, handles customer requests instantly, and escalates complex cases with full context via text, image and voice across 80+ languages, acting as a reliable front line for support operations.

Vector Agents builds AI-powered digital workers that operate alongside human teams across core business functions. From sales and finance to customer success and HR, Vector’s digital workers help organizations execute work more reliably, consistently, and at scale—delivering Digital Workers, Human Outcomes.
