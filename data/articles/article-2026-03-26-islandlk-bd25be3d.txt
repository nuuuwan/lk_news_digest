Celeste & BNS partner to revolutionize Sri Lanka’s quick commerce, lifestyle, and online shopping experience

Celeste, one of Sri Lanka’s most celebrated lifestyle brands, marked a defining milestone in its journey by announcing a landmark equity partnership with the country’s iconic music duo BNS. The announcement capped an evening that celebrated six years of growth, community, and the authentic connections that have come to define the Celeste brand today.

Designed as a customer-first celebration, the one-of-a-kind event brought together over 1,000 carefully selected guests from Celeste’s base of more than 135,000 customers, recognising those who have consistently engaged with the brand through loyalty, frequency, and long-standing interaction. The evening gave corporate clients and long-standing patrons the opportunity to interact directly with Celeste’s leadership while reflecting on the brand’s journey, the customer experience it continues to build, and its future ambitions.

During the event, Celeste formally announced an equity partnership with the iconic Sri Lankan music duo Bathiya and Santhush, widely known as BNS. Known for their groundbreaking influence on Sri Lankan music and their ability to connect with audiences across generations, Bathiya and Santhush bring both cultural star power as well as strategic business and entrepreneurial insight to the partnership.
