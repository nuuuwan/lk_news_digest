Mintpay unveils no-expiry corporate vouchers

Mintpay has launched a corporate gifting solution allowing companies to issue digital vouchers redeemable across over 2,500 merchants islandwide across 25 categories, including fashion, dining, groceries, and travel.

Unlike traditional vouchers, Mintpay’s vouchers can be converted into Mint Coins (1 Coin = LKR 1) with no expiry.

Over 50 companies, including Union Assurance and Circles Life, have adopted the solution within six months. Mintpay Co-founder and CEO Kukaraj Tharmasegaram said the move reflects shifting employee expectations toward flexibility and choice in rewards.
