Sri Lanka Army calls for applications for Officer Cadet Courses

Colombo, June 22 (Daily Mirror) - The Sri Lanka Army has invited applications for vacancies in the Officer Cadets Short Course (Diploma) and the Lady Officer Cadets Course (Diploma) in the Regular Force.

Eligible candidates interested in pursuing a career as commissioned officers in the Army are encouraged to apply.

Further details, including eligibility criteria, application procedures and course information, are available on the Sri Lanka Army’s official website (https://www.army.lk/officer).
