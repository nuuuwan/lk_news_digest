eMarketingEye donates Rs. 10 m to ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ fund

eMarketingEye Ltd., has made a financial contribution of Rs. 10 million to the ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund, established to restore livelihoods and rebuild the country in the aftermath of the devastation caused by Cyclone Ditwah.

The cheque was handed over by eMarketingEye CEO Rajitha Dahanayake to Senior Additional Secretary to the President Russell Aponsu. eMarketingEye Senior Vice President – Digital Marketing Kusalika Basnayake, Senior Vice President – Web Solutions Suranga Rajakaruna and Vice President – Operations Maheshi Ranasinghe were also present on the occasion.
