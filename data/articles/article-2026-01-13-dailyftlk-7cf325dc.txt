W.R. Rodrigo & Partners announces strategic transition to partnership

W.R. Rodrigo and Co. has announced a formal transition in its professional structure, moving from a sole practitioner firm to a partnership. This evolution coincides with the appointment of Chanie Rodrigo as a Partner, joining Managing Partner Rajitha Rodrigo to lead the newly reconstituted firm, W.R. Rodrigo and Partners, Chartered Accountants. The transition marks a significant milestone in the firm’s forty-year history, reflecting a strategic move to broaden technical leadership and reinforce governance frameworks in an increasingly complex regulatory environment.

The appointment of Chanie Rodrigo reinforces the firm’s technical capabilities in taxation. With over fifteen years of experience in the Sri Lankan tax landscape, her professional background includes tenures at international audit firms, followed by senior group tax roles within the private sector. An Associate Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka (ACA) and a Fellow of ACCA (UK), she also holds the Enrolled Agent (USA) designation and a Bachelor of Science degree in Applied Accounting from Oxford Brookes University. Her focus remains on the intersection of statutory compliance and practical tax advisory, ensuring the firm’s technical approach remains aligned with evolving Sri Lankan tax laws and regulatory requirements.

Managing Partner Rajitha Rodrigo brings a wealth of international and local expertise to the partnership. As an ACA, ACMA (UK), and CGMA member, Rajitha’s career spans nearly a decade, including nearly a decade with an international audit firm in both Sri Lanka and Singapore. His experience in assurance and advisory services across various sectors provides the foundation for the firm’s focus on professional stewardship and managing organisational responses to shifting political, economic, and technological landscapes.

Commenting on the transition, Rajitha Rodrigo said: “The evolution of the firm into a partnership reflects our commitment to institutionalising the professional values established by our founder, Ranjith Rodrigo, four decades ago. In an era defined by transparency and rapid regulatory shifts, this transition ensures a robust governance framework that supports the long-term stability and resilience of the enterprises we serve through dedicated professional stewardship.”

Sharing her outlook on joining the leadership team, Chanie Rodrigo said: “Professional integrity and a steadfast adherence to regulatory requirements are the cornerstones of our practice. As the regulatory environment becomes increasingly sophisticated, my focus is on applying a detail-oriented, technical discipline to ensure that transparency and ethical practice remain at the core of our service delivery at W.R. Rodrigo and Partners.”

Under its new structure, W.R. Rodrigo and Partners remains committed to providing assurance, tax compliance, and business advisory services with excellence. The firm continues to support a diverse pool of clients across sectors, leveraging four decades of regional acquaintance to deliver professional excellence.
