Website owners invited to submit sites to SL’s only website competition

BestWeb.LK, Sri Lanka’s premium website competition and the benchmark for digital excellence in Sri Lanka, is officially calling for applications and invites all .lk website owners to submit their websites for the opportunity to gain national recognition.

Organized by the LK Domain Registry and now in its 16th edition, BestWeb.LK recognizes outstanding .lk websites across 24 industry categories. The competition features 126 awards, including Gold, Silver, and Bronze accolades, along with special recognitions such as Best Mobile-Optimized Website, Most Popular Website, Best Web Developer, and Overall Awards.
