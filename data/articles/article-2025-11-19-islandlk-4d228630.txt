EduViz International unveils innovative platform to navigate study abroad opportunities

EduViz International officially opened its doors in Colombo 05 , marking an exciting new chapter for Sri Lanka’s study abroad and higher education landscape. The company introduces a student-first approach to overseas education services that combines expertise, transparency, and personalized guidance, helping students, professionals, and parents navigate their global education journey with confidence. All services, from university applications to visa processing, will be provided to students completely free of charge.

As part of its launch, EduViz International announced the signing of several key Memorandums of Understanding (MOUs) with leading financial institutions to introduce dedicated student-loan schemes, enabling students to access tailored education loan facilities. These strategic partnerships aim to make studying abroad more accessible by ensuring that financial constraints no longer stand in the way of academic ambition. The opening ceremony was officiated by Mr. Naleen Edirisinghe, Director/Chief Executive Officer of Pan Asia Bank, together with Mr. Kasun Kaluarachchi, Founder and Chief Executive Officer of EduViz International.

Behind EduViz is Kasun Kaluarachchi, a seasoned professional with over 17 years of experience across banking, marketing, and international education. His journey from being one of Sri Lanka’s youngest bank branch managers to becoming a respected education consultant has shaped the core values of EduViz: trust, credibility, and service excellence. Kasun is a PIER-Qualified Education Agent Counsellor (QEAC – Australia), a British Council Certified UK Counsellor, and a Member of CIPM Sri Lanka. He also holds an MBA from Anglia Ruskin University (United Kingdom) and an Executive MSc in Strategic Marketing from Malaysia.
