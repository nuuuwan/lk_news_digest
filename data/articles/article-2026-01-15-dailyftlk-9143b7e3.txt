Nations Trust Bank partners KOKO

Nations Trust Bank PLC (NTB), the issuer and sole acquirer of American Express Cards in Sri Lanka, has announced a new partnership with KOKO, one of the country’s leading Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL) solution providers.

Through this collaboration, American Express Cardmembers of Nations Trust Bank can utilize their cards as a payment method on the KOKO app, increasing their convenience and flexibility while shopping across a wider network.

Cardmembers can now make purchases at over 6,000 KOKO-partnered online and in-store merchants thanks to this partnership, and many of these previously did not provide installment plans directly on American Express Cards. In addition to earning Membership Rewards points for eligible KOKO transactions made with an American Express Card issued by Nations Trust Bank, cardmembers will also benefit from special promotions, discounts, and benefits offered throughout KOKO’s partner network.

KOKO Regional CEO Michael Sathasivam said: “We are thrilled to welcome Nations Trust Bank American Express Cardmembers to KOKO. More shoppers can now take advantage of various payment alternatives from thousands of merchants thanks to our partnership. We are eager to improve this relationship and provide our joint customers with greater value.”

Nations Trust Bank Executive Vice President – Consumer Banking Sheahan Daniel said: “We are delighted to collaborate with KOKO to improve our American Express Cardmembers everyday payment experience. Our Cardmembers now have more options, access, and flexibility when they shop throughout KOKO’s vast merchant network thanks to this integration. We remain dedicated to growing significant partnerships that facilitate our customer’s convenience and progression.”
