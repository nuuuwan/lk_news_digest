Housing & Construction Expo at BMICH

Build SL 2026 – Housing and Construction Exhibition, organised by the Construction Industry Chamber of Sri Lanka (CCI), serves as a key platform for industry stakeholders to showcase innovative products, services, emerging technologies and construction capabilities to potential clients and the public. The exhibition also highlights major ongoing and planned development projects across the country, offering insights into the vision of building a new Sri Lanka. The 21st edition of the event will be held on May 22, 23 and 24, 2026 at the BMICH. Pics by Kithsiri De Mel
