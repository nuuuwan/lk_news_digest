Gaemi Pata art exhibitionin Colombo on May 16-17

Randhini Fernando will hold a solo art exhibition Gæmi Pāta at the Lionel Wendt Art Gallery on May 16 and 17 from 9.00 am to 7.30 pm.

The exhibition will showcase close to 40 paintings. They are in the mediums of oil, acrylic and soft pastel. Randhini’s paintings depict landscapes and scenery of places around the island nation.

Randhini has been an artist from a young age. She is a self taught artist, inspired by her father Dr. Jayaindra Fernando, also an artist, and encouraged by her mother Thivanshi.

She held her first solo exhibition at the age of 17 in 2010. This was followed by a joint exhibition with her father in 2017. Both were held at the Lionel Wendt Art Gallery.

While Art remains a professional hobby, Randhini is an Attorney-at-Law by profession and also a Cellist in the Symphony Orchestra of Sri Lanka.

The exhibition comprises paintings which draw inspiration from travels over the years done with her family, and now with her husband Nuran Gomez and her daughter.

Randhini hopes to inspire viewers by her paintings, to the beauty and vibrance of this beautiful country we call home.
