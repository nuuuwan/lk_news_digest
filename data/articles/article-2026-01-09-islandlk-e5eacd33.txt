Magnanimity by Washington-based Lankans

Sri Lankans residing in Washington, United States, under the banner of the “Sri Lanka Ranga Kala Kavaya”, have donated US dollars 6,500 to the “Re-Building Sri Lanka” fund this week.

The donation was made with the objective of supporting the restoration of livelihoods of people affected by the Dittwa cyclone and contributing to the rebuilding of areas damaged by the disaster, the organisation’s Chairman, Anura Ratnayake, said. He noted that the initiative reflects the commitment of overseas Sri Lankans to stand with the motherland during times of crisis.

The cheque pertaining to the donation was formally handed over by Chairman Anura Ratnayake to Secretary to the President, Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake, at a brief ceremony held this week.

Officials expressed appreciation for the gesture, stating that such contributions from the Sri Lankan diaspora play a vital role in strengthening national recovery and reconstruction efforts following natural disasters.
