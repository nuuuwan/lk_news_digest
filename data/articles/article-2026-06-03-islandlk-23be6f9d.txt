Three anti-ageing fixes …

Ageing gracefully doesn’t need a 10-step routine. Sometimes the best beauty secrets are already in your pantry.

These three simple, skin-loving combos hydrate, firm, and brighten mature skin, gently.

No harsh chemicals, just kitchen wisdom passed down for generations.

You will need 03 tablespoon of mashed ripe papaya, 01 teaspoon honey, and ½ teaspoon olive oil.

Mix them together and apply and let it remain for 15 minutes, and then rinse with lukewarm water.

Papaya gently exfoliates, honey locks moisture, and olive oil smooths fine lines.

You will need ½ ripe banana and 02 tablespoons plain yoghurt.

Mix and mash well and then apply and let the mixture remain for around 15-20 minutes, and then rinse off.

Banana is plump with potassium, while yoghurt’s lactic acid gives a mild glow.

You will need ½ cup strong green tea and ½ cup aloe vera gel.

Mix the green tea and aloe vera gel, and freeze the mixture in an ice tray. Glide 01 cube over face in the morning.

Cold tightens, while antioxidants from green tea fight free radicals.

By the way, this is important: Always patch test on your wrist first. Also use fresh ingredients and follow with a good moisturiser, and daily sunscreen, for best results.
