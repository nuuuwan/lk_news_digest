Opposition proceeds with no-confidence motion against Energy Minister

Members of Parliament have started endorsing a no-confidence motion against Power and Energy Minister Kumara Jayakody, according to sources from the Opposition Leader’s office.

Opposition Leader Sajith Premadasa, along with several Opposition MPs, has already signed the motion. Among those who have added their signatures are SLPP MP D.V. Chanaka, Ravi Karunanayake, and Ramanathan Archuna.
