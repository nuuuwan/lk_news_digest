NSBM Green University welcomes 2026 newest intake

NSBM GreenUniversity, Sri Lanka’s premier degree-awarding institute, ceremoniously welcomed its newest undergraduate intake for 2026 with an inspiring inauguration ceremony held on 23rd February at its iconic green campus.

This special interim intake marked another significant milestone in NSBM’s journey, as new students embarked on their higher education pathways across a wide spectrum of undergraduate and foundation programmes offered by the Faculties of Business, Computing, Engineering, and Science.

The ceremony was graced by distinguished keynote speakers: Prof. Kapila Perera,Secretary – Ministry of Transport, Highways, and Urban Development; Prof. Darshana Sedera, Executive Dean – Faculty of Business, Law and Arts at Southern CrossUniversity, Australia; and Dr. Anil Jasinghe, Secretary – Ministry of Health and Mass Media. The keynote speakers shared words of wisdom and inspiration, encouraging students to embrace academic excellence, leadership, and innovation throughout their university journey.

This new intake opens doors to over 50 degree programmes, including NSBM’s own degree offerings and internationally recognised foreign degree qualifications delivered in partnership with top-ranked global universities.

To date, NSBM Green University has produced over 23,000 graduates and currently hosts more than 13,000 undergraduates.
