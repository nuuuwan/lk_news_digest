Public Notice by SEC

The Securities and Exchange Commission of Sri Lanka (SEC) wishes to inform the general public that “Samagi Capital (Private) Limited” is not licensed by the SEC to operate as a Stock Broker or Stock Dealer in Sri Lanka.

A certificate currently being circulated on social media, which purports to grant a Broker Dealer License to the above mentioned company (as shown in the image), is fraudulent and has not been issued by the SEC. This certificate is fake and misleading.

The SEC strongly advises investors and the general public as follows:

❌ Investors are advised not to deposit any funds into accounts associated with Samagi Capital (Private) Limited for the purpose of trading on the Colombo Stock Exchange (CSE).

1️⃣ The Securities and Exchange Commission of Sri Lanka (SEC)

☝️Investor protection is of utmost importance. Exercise due diligence and verify before you invest.
