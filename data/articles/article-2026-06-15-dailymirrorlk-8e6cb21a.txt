People’s Bank opens Jaffna Teaching Hospital and UDA ATMs

People’s Bank recently opened two new automated teller machines (ATMs) on the premises of Jaffna Teaching Hospital and Urban Development Authority (UDA) in Jaffna.

The newly installed ATMs provide the customers with convenient access to banking services, enabling cash withdrawals 24 hours a day, seven days a week, throughout the year.

The opening ceremonies were attended by Jaffna Teaching Hospital Director T. Sathiyamoorthy, Urban Development Authority Director D.B.S.K. Dissanayake, People’s Bank Regional Manager Jaffna M. Mathiyalagan, Assistant Regional Managers Y. Sureshkumar and T. Bharathramana, J/Kannathiddy Branch Manager P. Partheepan, J/Main Street Branch Manager N. Thanesh and the staff members of  People’s Bank.
