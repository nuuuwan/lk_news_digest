EKHO Ella ranked in top 1% of hotels worldwide on TripAdvisor

EKHO Ella, a boutique hotel in Sri Lanka’s hill country, has been honored with the prestigious TripAdvisor Travellers’ Choice Best of the Best Award for 2025. This accolade places it among the top one percent of hotels globally, based on consistently outstanding guest reviews.

The award recognizes the hotel’s unique railway-inspired concept, warm hospitality, and its commitment to blending local heritage with modern comfort. To celebrate this achievement, EKHO Ella held an Employee Felicitation Ceremony, honoring the dedicated team whose service is central to its success.

“This recognition shows that guests value our blend of cultural storytelling and refined service. Our railway-themed concept pays tribute to Sri Lanka’s hill country train journey, creating a truly distinctive experience.” said Adrian Jansz, Vice President of Sales and Marketing for CHC Hotels.”

Perched above the iconic Ella Gap, the property offers easy access to major landmarks. This award highlights a growing traveler preference for smaller hotels that offer character, cultural depth, and a strong sense of place.
