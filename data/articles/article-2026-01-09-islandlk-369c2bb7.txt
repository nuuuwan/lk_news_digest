Parenthood Global sets the stage for Family-Friendly Workplaces in Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka marked a significant milestone in the evolution of inclusive employment practices with the successful hosting of the Parent-Inclusive Workplaces Summit 2025. The national-level event brought together senior corporate leaders, human resource professionals, policymakers, wellbeing practitioners, and members of the parenthood community to examine how workplaces can better support working parents while strengthening organizational performance.

The summit was organized and hosted by Parenthood Global in partnership with Great Place to Work Sri Lanka. The full-day programme positioned parent inclusion as a strategic workforce priority rather than a discretionary benefit, emphasizing its role in driving employee engagement, retention, and long-term business sustainability.
