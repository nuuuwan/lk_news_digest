Hayleys Chief Mohan Pandithage recognised as Businessman for Year 2025

LMD magazine has named Mohan Pandithage, Chairman and Chief Executive of Hayleys PLC, as its Businessman for the Year 2025.

Pandithage joined Hayleys in 1969 as a management trainee and has since risen through the ranks to lead one of Sri Lanka’s most diversified conglomerates. He became a board member in 1998 and assumed the role of Chief Executive in 2009.

Under his leadership, Hayleys has maintained its position as a leading player in the private sector. The group has been recognised as Sri Lanka’s top listed company on seven occasions, including the past two years. Its operations now span 16 sectors and over 80 global markets, covering industries from agriculture and manufacturing to logistics, leisure and renewable energy.

Beyond his corporate responsibilities, Pandithage has played a significant role in the logistics and transport sectors. He is a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Logistics and Transport (CILT) and serves on the Advisory Council of the Ceylon Association of Shipping Agents and the Council of the Employers’ Federation of Ceylon.

His contributions have been recognised with several industry accolades, including the Pinnacle Lifetime Award by CILT, the Best Shipping Personality Award from the Institute of Chartered Shipbrokers, and induction into the Sri Lanka Aviation Hall of Fame.

Pandithage also serves as the Honorary Consul of Mexico to Sri Lanka.

The Businessman for the Year award is presented by LMD to acknowledge leadership and impact within Sri Lanka’s corporate sector.
