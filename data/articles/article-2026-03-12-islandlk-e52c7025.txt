Web3Interactive and Immersive Labs unveil advanced real estate visualization

Web3Interactive LLC, a UAE-based immersive technology company headquartered in Dubai, is introducing advanced real estate visualization and immersive sales technologies to Sri Lanka through its innovation subsidiary, Immersive Labs.

Sri Lanka’s real estate sector is demonstrating steady recovery in 2025, boosted by macroeconomic stabilisation, lower policy rates and renewed investor confidence. Overseas and Diaspora interest is increasing with foreign buyers currently accounting for a significant share of online property searches, drawn by comparatively attractive USD pricing and improved economic stability.

Headquartered in Dubai and co-founded by prominent Sri Lankan industry figures Omar Sahib and Safraz Razeek, Web3Interactive designs and delivers advanced immersive technology solutions, particularly within the real estate sector. While the company manages its expanding regional operations from Dubai, its core research and engineering capabilities are anchored in Sri Lanka through Immersive Labs, led by CEO Sameera Piyadigamage. Immersive Labs serves as the group’s dedicated R&D and engineering hub, specializing in Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and high-fidelity architectural visualizations. The two companies combine Sri Lankan engineering capability with Middle Eastern market standards to deliver globally competitive immersive solutions. By centralizing advanced technical development in Sri Lanka and aligning with Middle Eastern market expectations, the group delivers immersive solutions that meet global standards. With direct exposure to the fast-evolving MENA real estate ecosystem, Web3Interactive and Immersive Labs are now enabling Sri Lankan developers to adopt immersive sales acceleration strategies that have proven successful in Dubai and across the region.
