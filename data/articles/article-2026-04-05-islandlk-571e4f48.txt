Sarvodaya Development Finance appoints Sashi Schaffter to board

Sarvodaya Development Finance PLC has appointed Sashi Adele Schaffter as a Non-Executive, Non-Independent Director, effective 2 April 2026. She will represent the company’s shareholder, Janashakthi Ltd (JXG).

Schaffter, a UK-qualified Barrister called to the Bar of England and Wales at the Honourable Society of the Inner Temple with Distinction, brings a mix of legal, financial, and strategic experience to the board. Her background spans corporate finance, advisory work, strategy, and legal research.

She currently serves as Vice President – Corporate Finance at First Capital Holdings PLC, where she is part of the First Capital Advisory Services team, focusing on complex corporate transactions and strategic mandates. She is also an advocate for financial empowerment and mental health and wellbeing.

Prior to First Capital, Schaffter was Apprentice Director at JXG, leading group-level strategic planning initiatives and supporting long-term organisational development.

Her academic qualifications include an LLB (Hons) from SOAS, University of London, and an LLM (Human Rights) from Queen Mary University of London.

Sarvodaya Development Finance said her appointment strengthens the board’s capacity to support the company’s strategic direction and governance priorities as the firm addresses the evolving demands of Sri Lanka’s financial services sector.
