Menaka Athukorala joins Alpha Fire as new Chairman

Alpha Fire Services PLC has appointed Menaka Athukorala to its Board as Non-Executive Chairman.

The company said he is a dynamic and results-driven executive with over 30 years of experience in plantation management, renewable energy, and corporate leadership with proven expertise in strategic planning, operational management, and driving sustainable business growth.

He holds an MBA and a Higher National Diploma in Plantation Management and Agriculture.
