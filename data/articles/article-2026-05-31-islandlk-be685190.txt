BWIO International Awards celebrates excellence across multiple industries

The Business World International Organization INC-USA successfully hosted the Business World International Awards 2026 on 16 May at Mount Lavinia Hotel, bringing together leading business personalities, diplomats, entrepreneurs, and distinguished guests from Sri Lanka and overseas. Recognised as one of the most prestigious business award festivals in the Asia-Pacific region, the annual event celebrated excellence, leadership, innovation, and outstanding achievements across multiple industries while creating a platform to recognise individuals and organisations contributing towards economic and business development.

The event was attended by a large gathering of corporate leaders, professionals, and celebrities, reflecting the growing international stature of the awards programme. The Chief Guest at the ceremony was Badli Hisham Adam, who currently serves as the High Commissioner of Malaysia to Sri Lanka and Ambassador of Malaysia to the Maldives.
