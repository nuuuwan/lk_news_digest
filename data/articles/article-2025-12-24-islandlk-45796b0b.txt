Chinese Dragon Café opens flagship outlet in Galle

Chinese Dragon Café (CDC) has opened its 10th Sri Lankan outlet in Galle, located at 45 Colombo Rd, Kaluwella. Founded in 1942, the renowned restaurant brand has grown from a local eatery into a national household name, with an international branch in Dubai.

The Galle launch underscores CDC’s expansion commitment, bringing its signature dishes like Hot Butter Cuttlefish to new communities. The brand also offers delivery, catering, and online ordering.

CDC CEO Gayan Fernando stated the move responds to loyal southern province customers, aiming to become a familiar name in the area. The opening reinforces CDC’s mission to share its authentic Sri Lankan Chinese flavours nationwide.
