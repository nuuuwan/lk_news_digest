Bribery Commission arrests Rakitha Rajapaksa, SJB organizer

Colombo, June 25 (Daily Mirror) - Attorney-at-Law Rakitha Rajapaksa, son of former Justice Minister Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, and Charith Abeysinghe, the Samagi Jana Balawegaya (SJB) organizer for Horana, have been arrested by the Commission to Investigate Allegations of Bribery or Corruption (CIABOC).
