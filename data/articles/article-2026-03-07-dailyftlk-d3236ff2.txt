Sri Lankans condemn  US-Israel attacks on Iran

Protesters gathered in Colombo yesterday at a demonstration organised by the Friends of a Free Palestine organisation under the theme “No Blood for Oil,” condemning recent United States and Israeli military attacks on Iran. Iranian Ambassador Dr. Alireza Delkhosh (centre) and Samagi Jana Balawegaya MP Mujibur Rahman were among those present — Pic by Pradeep Pathirana
