N. U. Jayawardena Memorial Conference Room inaugurated

A memorial conference room honouring N. U. Jayawardena was opened at the MMBL–Pathfinder Headquarters in Peliyagoda, marking the 24th death anniversary of Sri Lanka’s first Central Bank Governor and noted financier.Named in his memory, this media-enabled conference room will be used for local and international meetings.

MMBL–Pathfinder Founder Milinda Moragoda, family members Jennifer Moragoda, Menaka Balendra, and Renuka Deraniyagala, Group Director/CEO K. Balasunderam, and Pathfinder Foundation Chairman Bernard Goonetilleke, together with other staff members of the Group, attended the occasion.
