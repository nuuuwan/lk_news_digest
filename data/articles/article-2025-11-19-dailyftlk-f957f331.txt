JF Packaging commences trading as “JFP-N-0000” today

Shares of JF Packaging Ltd., recently offered to the public through its oversubscribed IPO, will commence trading today on the Colombo Stock Exchange’s Main Board under the ticker symbol “JFP-N-0000”.

The IPO, which opened on 30 October, offered 51,724,144 Ordinary Voting Shares at an Issue Price of Rs. 11.60 each, raising Rs. 600 million in capital. The Issue was oversubscribed 6.3 times within hours of opening.

Proceeds from the IPO will be used to settle selected term loans and partially repay revolving import loans, strengthening the Company’s balance sheet and reducing finance costs. JF Packaging serves leading local and international brands and is recognised as a leader in introducing eco-friendly packaging solutions to the Sri Lankan market.
