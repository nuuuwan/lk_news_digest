Wimal Liyanagama  appointed Sri Lanka  Customs Director General

The Cabinet of Ministers on Monday approved appointing Wimal S.K. Liyanagama as Director General of Sri Lanka Customs with effect from today (6).

Liyanagama is a Special Grade Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service (SLAS) served as the Director General of the Department of Management Services of the Treasury.

Sri Lanka Customs Director General Seevali Arukgoda retired on 5 May.
