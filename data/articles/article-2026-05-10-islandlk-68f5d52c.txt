Public seminar on Annual Economic Review 2025

The Centre for Banking Studies (CBS) of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka (CBSL) has organised a public seminar to present and discuss the Annual Economic Review 2025, a flagship publication that offers a comprehensive account of the nation’s economic performance over the past year.

Scheduled for the morning of 13 May 2026, the seminar will be held at the Atrium of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka on Janathipathi Mawatha, Colombo 01, from 10.00 a.m. to 12.00 p.m. The event will be conducted in English, ensuring accessibility to a broad audience of professionals, academics, students, and members of the general public with an interest in the country’s economic trajectory.

Dr. L. R. C. Pathberiya, the Director of the Economic Research Department at CBSL. Dr. Pathberiya, a seasoned economist with deep expertise in macroeconomic analysis and policy formulation, is expected to deliver a nuanced interpretation of the data and trends captured in the Annual Economic Review 2025.

His presentation will likely cover key indicators such as GDP growth, inflation dynamics, fiscal performance, external sector stability, and monetary policy outcomes.

Those wishing to attend are requested to confirm their participation by 4.00 p.m. on Tuesday, 12 May 2026.
