Adamjee Lukmanjee holds Legacy of Trust – Customer Forum 2026

Adamjee Lukmanjee Group of Companies, a trusted name in Sri Lanka’s agricultural and industrial supply sector, with a proud legacy of 160 years, successfully hosted Legacy of Trust - Customer Forum 2026 recently at Cinnamon Life, Colombo.

The event was organised to appreciate and strengthen the long-standing relationships built with the company’s valued customers and stakeholders over the years.

With a strong presence in the Sri Lankan market over the past 160 years, Adamjee Lukmanjee Group of Companies has continuously contributed to the country’s agricultural, livestock and construction industries through its range of high-quality products including animal feed ingredients, Saviya cement and N-Joy coconut oil. The company has earned a reputation for reliability, consistency and customer-centric service, becoming a trusted partner for businesses across the island.

The event brought together customers, industry leaders, business partners and corporate representatives for a day of recognition, knowledge sharing and networking built on a foundation of trust. The forum highlighted the company’s commitment to strengthening trust-driven, sustainable partnerships and creating long-term value for its customers.

The keynote speakers for the event included renowned entrepreneur Araliya Group Chairman Dudley Sirisena and economic policy analyst Dhananath Fernando from Advocata.

Addressing the gathering, Sirisena shared valuable insights on entrepreneurship, resilience and the importance of empowering local industries. Fernando spoke on the current economic landscape of Sri Lanka and the role of private sector collaboration in driving national growth and competitiveness.

During the forum, 20 customers were recognised and awarded across two categories: 10 customers were honoured as longstanding customers for their enduring trust and loyalty, while 11 customers were recognised as top-performing customers across four key categories for their outstanding contribution to the company’s growth and success.

Legacy of Trust - Customer Forum 2026 reflected Adamjee Lukmanjee Group of Companies’ dedication to honouring the trust placed in the company by its customers while reinforcing its vision for future growth and innovation. The event concluded on a high note with appreciation extended to all customers and partners who have contributed to the company’s journey and success over the years.
