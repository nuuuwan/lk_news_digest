U.S. Pacific Air Forces Commander arrives in Sri Lanka

Colombo, June 21 (Daily Mirror) - General Kevin Schneider, Commander of the U.S. Pacific Air Forces (PACAF), arrived in Sri Lanka today for a three-day official visit aimed at further strengthening the growing defense partnership between the United States and Sri Lanka. As the head of PACAF, General Schneider oversees all U.S. Air Force personnel and assets across the Indo-Pacific region.

During his visit from June 21 to 24, General Schneider is scheduled to hold discussions with senior Sri Lankan government and defense officials, including leaders from the Ministry of Defense and the Sri Lanka Air Force. The meetings will focus on expanding bilateral cooperation in key areas such as air and maritime domain awareness, cybersecurity, disaster response, and regional security.
