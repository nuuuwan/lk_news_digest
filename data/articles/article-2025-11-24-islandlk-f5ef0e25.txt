Elephant House Ice Cream crowned Brand of the Year at SLIM Brand Excellence Awards 2025

Elephant House Ice Cream cemented its position as one of Sri Lanka’s most iconic brands by achieving the highest recognition at the SLIM Brand Excellence Awards 2025, where it was crowned Brand of the Year. The ceremony, held on 13th November, marked a defining moment for the brand as it reaffirmed its category leadership, deep-rooted consumer trust, and enduring legacy in Sri Lankan households.

In addition to securing the top honor of Brand of the Year, Elephant House Ice Cream delivered an exceptional performance across multiple award categories, winning Gold for Product Brand of the Year and Silver for Local Brand of the Year. This triple win underscores the brand’s holistic strength, from product innovation and strategic marketing to consumer engagement and long-term brand equity. It reflects not only the brand’s current market momentum but also the disciplined investments made to elevate quality, expand its product range, and strengthen emotional relevance among consumers nationwide.

With a heritage spanning over a century, Elephant House Ice Cream has remained synonymous with trusted family enjoyment, uncompromising quality, and category-defining innovation. Over the years, the brand has earned numerous accolades that stand as a testament to its timeless appeal, sustained performance, and pioneering role in shaping Sri Lanka’s frozen confectionery landscape. Its latest achievement at this year’s SLIM Brand Excellence Awards further strengthens its position as one of the country’s most respected and admired consumer brands.

Commenting on the win, Daminda Gamlath, President, John Keells Consumer Foods Sector, said, “This recognition is a testament to the steady, consistent, and consumer-focused initiatives we have driven throughout the year at Elephant House Ice Cream. It reflects our commitment to continuous improvement, purposeful innovation, and strengthening the brand across every stage of the consumer journey. Our teams have put in disciplined, thoughtful work to elevate performance and impact, and this achievement acknowledges that collective effort.”
