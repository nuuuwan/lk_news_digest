MAS Bodyline leads third consecutive Sri Pada clean-up initiative

Nearly 1,000 volunteers from MAS Bodyline and across MAS Holdings came together on May 31, at the end of Sri Pada pilgrimage season, to help clean the sacred trail.

For the third consecutive year, they walked the route from the summit to Nallathanniya, clearing plastic, polythene and other non-biodegradable waste left behind by the pilgrims.

Around 5,000 kilogrammes of waste was cleared from the trail - helping to protect a site that holds deep environmental and cultural significance for Sri Lanka.

The effort was strengthened by the support of religious leaders, security forces, healthcare teams, local authorities and media representatives.
