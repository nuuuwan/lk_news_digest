MSI honours top commercial PC partners at Colombo Awards Ceremony

Micro-Star International (MSI) recently hosted its Partner Excellence Awards in Colombo, gathering over 100 industry stakeholders to celebrate the rapid expansion of its commercial network in Sri Lanka.

At the event, MSI presented 15 awards to Gold and Strategic Partners, recognizing their exceptional contributions to the brand’s commercial PC segment. Additionally, more than 33 System Integrators received Authorized Partner Certificates, signaling MSI’s growing island-wide footprint.

Kevin Chung, MSI Regional Manager, highlighted the sector’s swift growth, while Country Manager Sanjaya Adipathy commended partners for securing key government and corporate tenders. Strengthening its after-sales support, MSI officially certified its distributor, Trident Corporation, as the Authorized Service Centre for commercial products in Sri Lanka and the Maldives, operating from a state-of-the-art facility in Colombo 05.
