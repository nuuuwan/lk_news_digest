Malayagam community donates to ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund

The hill country Malayagam community recently made a financial donation of Rs. 1 million to the Government’s ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund to provide relief to those affected by Cyclone Ditwah.

The relevant cheque was presented by Ratnapura Helping Hands Association Vice President S. Dayalan representing the Malayagam community to Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake, at the Presidential Secretariat.

Plantation and Community Infrastructure Deputy Minister Sundaralingam Pradeep, Ratnapura Helping Hands Association Secretary R. Manoharan, Women’s Organiser G. Manoranjidam, and S. Sivanesan were also present at this event.
