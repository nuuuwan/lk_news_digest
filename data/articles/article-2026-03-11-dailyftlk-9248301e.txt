Aliya Beer expands to global markets

From left: DCSL Breweries Lanka Head of Brand and Trade Marketing Tharaka Silva, International Trade Adviser Sunil Jayaweera, IMAGILAND Resort Investment and Management Co. Chairman Zhao Tian, Chief Executive Officer Onida Kulasekera, and Country Manager Mohan Amarasekera

DCSL Breweries Lanka Ltd., has announced the international launch of Aliya Beer, the latest beer brand to emerge from Sri Lanka. Following its successful reception in local markets, Aliya Beer is now set to make its mark in export markets, introducing the world to the true taste of Sri Lanka. Available in both Strong and Lager variants, Aliya has been meticulously crafted to appeal to a wide range of palates globally, while retaining its authentic Sri Lankan identity.

Inspired by the rich history, strength, and unity of Sri Lanka, Aliya Beer provides a smooth yet bold drinking experience. Its unique blend of ingredients and flavours has been designed to appeal not only to Sri Lankan consumers but also to international audiences eager for authentic, culturally resonant beverages. With its launch into the global market, Aliya Beer is positioned as more than just a beer, it is a tribute to the spirit of Sri Lanka, offering a taste of the island’s heritage to the world.

The expansion of Aliya Beer into international markets represents a major milestone for DCSL Breweries Lanka. With a strategic focus on global growth, the company is aiming to raise Aliya Beer’s profile in key international markets, building strong brand awareness and cultivating a loyal consumer base. This move highlights not only the superior quality of Aliya Beer but also the pride and heritage of Sri Lanka.

“Aliya Beer embodies the spirit of Sri Lanka, its rich traditions and unmatched quality,” said DCSL Breweries Lanka Director – Sales and Marketing Kasun Jayawardena. “Expanding our reach into international markets is a testament to the strength of our brewing heritage and our commitment to sharing that legacy with the world.”

DCSL Breweries Lanka’s portfolio already includes prominent international brands like Heineken and Tiger, as well as well-established local names such as Bison and DCSL Beer. The inclusion of Aliya Beer further strengthens the company’s dedication to brewing excellence, ensuring a robust and diverse selection of both internationally recognised and locally crafted brands.
