FINEZ interior design brand partners Royal – Thomian cricket encounter for third consecutive year

FINEZ – Sri Lanka’s leading interior designer brand has partnered with the historic Royal – Thomian Cricket Encounter as the “Official Furniture and Interior Design Partner” for the third consecutive year. The partnership was formally announced at the official press conference for the 147th edition of the iconic encounter, reaffirming FINEZ’s commitment to supporting one of the most celebrated traditions in Sri Lankan school sports. Dhanushka Fernando, Founder and CEO of FINEZ, said: “As an Old Thomian, the Royal Thomian is more than just a cricket match to me. It represents tradition, brotherhood, and a legacy that has been passed down through generations. Supporting this historic encounter through FINEZ is both a personal honour and a meaningful way for us as a company to give back to the institution that helped shape who I am today. We look forward to continuing our support for this remarkable event.”
