HNB FINANCE secures two prestigious awards at BMPC Awards 2026

HNB FINANCE PLC, one of the Sri Lanka’s leading integrated financial services providers, won its inaugural Best Management Practices Company (BMPC) Awards on the 27th of March 2026. The BMPC Awards 2026 took place at the Monarch Imperial, Sri Jayewardenepura Kotte. Accordingly, HNB FINANCE PLC became the sector winner for Finance & Leasing (Private Sector) and won the accolade for being recognized as one of the Forty Outstanding Companies at the Best Management Practices Company Awards 2026.

The Best Management Practices Company Awards 2026, organized by the Institute of Chartered Professional Managers of Sri Lanka (CPM Sri Lanka), aims to acknowledge and honor organizations that exemplify outstanding management practices, strategic leadership, and professional excellence.
