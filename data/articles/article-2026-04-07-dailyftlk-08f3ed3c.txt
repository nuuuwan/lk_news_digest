Ritzbury honoured with Sri Lanka’s highest National Quality Award

Ritzbury, of CBL Foods International Ltd., has secured the Sri Lanka National Quality Award (SLNQA) 2023 in the Manufacturing – Large Scale category, winning it for the second time. The achievement reflects Ritzbury’s continued commitment to world-class quality standards, strong governance, and the delivery of safe, reliable, and high-quality products, while contributing to Sri Lanka’s industrial growth.

The award was presented at the Sri Lanka National Quality Awards ceremony organised by the Sri Lanka Standards Institution, following a rigorous, multi-stage evaluation process that benchmarks organisations against internationally recognised quality and performance excellence frameworks.

The Sri Lanka National Quality Award is regarded as the country’s highest recognition for organisational excellence, modelled on globally accepted assessment systems including the Malcolm Baldrige framework. Participating organisations are evaluated across critical dimensions including leadership, strategy, customer focus, workforce engagement, process management, and measurable business results.

CBL Foods International Director/CEO Kamal Geeganage said the award represents far more than external recognition. “Winning the Sri Lanka National Quality Award is a strong validation of the systems, standards, and governance structures we have built over many years. It reflects our unwavering commitment to quality at every level of the organisation, from leadership and strategy to day-to-day operations.”

Having been awarded certification under SLS 1509:2015, Ritzbury’s achievement further reinforces the company’s accreditation from other internationally recognised certifications, including ISO 9001:2015, ISO 22000:2018, FSSC 22000 Version 6, and BRCGS. The combined endorsements underscore the strength of Ritzbury’s management systems, disciplined adherence to internationally aligned food safety and quality standards, and its robust culture of continuous improvement across its manufacturing operations.

Building on its legacy of excellence, Ritzbury continues to set new benchmarks in innovation and sustainable growth in the local manufacturing industry. This achievement reaffirms the company’s commitment to sustainability and global competitiveness, highlighting its dedication to fostering a future grounded in trailblazing excellence.
