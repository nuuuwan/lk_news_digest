Belluna marks 20 years of Granbell Hotels & Resorts

Belluna Co. Ltd, Japan, a company listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange, continues to expand its hospitality and real estate presence across South Asia through Belluna Lanka, which serves as the group’s regional anchor and plays a key role in driving its growth initiatives in the region.

The 20th anniversary of Granbell Hotels & Resorts in 2026 marks a significant milestone for Belluna’s international hospitality journey, reflecting the continued evolution of its international portfolio rooted in Japan.

The first property under the brand, Shibuya Granbell Hotel, opened in Tokyo with 105 rooms, laying the foundation for what has grown into a hospitality portfolio spanning multiple destinations. Over the years, the brand has expanded its presence through its ethos of enriching lives, design-led spaces and long-term operational consistency. Today, Belluna operates 32 properties across Japan, while also maintaining one of the country’s largest hospitality inventories in Hokkaido, with more than 2,600 rooms across the region.

Through Granbell Hotel Colombo and Le Grand Galle, Belluna has established a growing hospitality presence in Sri Lanka. Granbell Hotel Colombo also marked the brand’s first property outside Japan, developed with a minimalist architectural approach shaped by Japanese design principles. Additionally, the company is expanding its presence within Sri Lanka’s tourism sector through the upcoming Granbell Hotel development in Ella.

Through its investments in Sri Lanka, Belluna continues to support the growth of the country’s tourism sector. With interests spanning hospitality, real estate and tourism-related developments, the group continues to strengthen its long-term presence in the country through investments designed to create value for guests, communities and the wider economy.

In celebration of this milestone, Belluna will launch a series of guest-focused initiatives at participating properties globally, including Sri Lanka. These initiatives will include dining experiences, hospitality experiences and digital engagement efforts designed to celebrate the milestone while strengthening connections with partners, guests and local communities.
