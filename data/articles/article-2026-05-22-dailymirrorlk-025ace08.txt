Pakistani Lifestyle Expo 2026 opens

Experience the Pakistani Lifestyle Expo in Colombo 2026, held in celebration of Hajj, from May 22 to 24 at BMICH. The expo features a vibrant showcase of Pakistani fashion, handmade textiles, traditional jewellery, home décor, food and more, bringing together leading designers, artisans and brands. The event highlights the richness of Pakistani lifestyle and cultural heritage while blending traditional craftsmanship with modern trends. Pics by Kithsiri De Mel
