A changing of the guard – George Steuart Insurance Brokers shuffles its Board

In a move aimed at strengthening its corporate structure and long-term plans for the future, George Steuart Insurance Brokers (Pvt) Ltd. (GSIB), leading corporate insurance and reinsurance broker in Sri Lanka, appointed T. S. Arulanandan (Arul) as its Managing Director and Principal Officer effective from 1st April 2026.

Arul counts more than 30 years of management experience in insurance underwriting and brokering. Prior to his appointment as a Director at GSIB, he held the positions of Director-Insurance at Finlay Insurance Brokers (Pvt) Ltd and Managing Director of Finlays Maldives (Pvt) Ltd, in the Republic of Maldives which were subsidiaries of Finlays Colombo PLC.

Arul, an alumnus of INSEAD Singapore / France, brings in a wealth of experience and strength to the GSIB team. He takes over the reins from Joachim Caspersz (Joey) who continues as an Executive Director of the Company. The changes have regulatory approval.

Arul said that he was honoured to be leading GSIB into its next phase of growth. He pointed out that growing the relevance of insurance brokering in Sri Lanka was imperative for the local industry, whilst also stating GSIB’s aspirations of spreading its footprint regionally.
