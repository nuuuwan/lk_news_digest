CID probes suspected cyber breach of Public Security Ministry website

The Criminal Investigation Department has launched an inquiry into a suspected cyberattack on the official website of the Public Security Ministry, following the detection of unauthorised access.

Police said multiple incidents of abnormal activity were identified on the site, raising concerns that it may have been compromised. The Police Media Division said investigations are under way to determine the source and extent of the intrusion.

Sri Lanka CERT and the Information and Communication Technology Agency have taken steps to restore the website and reinforce its security framework.

Authorities said irregularities were also noticed in the display of the national emblem on the website during the period in question.
