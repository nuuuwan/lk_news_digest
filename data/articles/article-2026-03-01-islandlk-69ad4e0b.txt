O2 Imports Exports wins top industry recognition at Pinnacle Sri Lanka 2025

O2 Imports Exports and Distributors has been named the Most Outstanding Café Equipment & Beverage Manufacturing Company of the Year at the Pinnacle Sri Lanka 2025 Awards.

Held at the Shangri-La Hotel, Colombo, the People’s Pinnacle award recognises the company’s significant impact on the local coffee and tea vending industry through its flagship brand, O2 CAFE.

The company manufactures premium non-flavored instant premixes, including Milk Tea, Milk Coffee, and Hot Chocolate. Committed to natural ingredients, the products contain no artificial flavors, colors, or preservatives, making them a popular choice for corporate clients and health-conscious consumers.

In addition to premix manufacturing, O2 Imports supplies commercial-grade vending machines designed for high traffic environments like offices, hotels, and factories.

Accepting the award, company representatives dedicated the honour to their customers and team, stating it reinforces their mission to deliver quality-driven innovation and maintain market leadership in Sri Lanka’s corporate beverage sector.
