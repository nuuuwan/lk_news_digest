Peterson Solutions Sri Lanka opens Orion City corporate office

Embassy of the Netherlands in Sri Lanka Deputy Head of Mission and Chargé d’Affaires Iwan Rutjens cuts the ribbon to inaugurate the new Peterson Solutions corporate office at Orion City in the presence of Peterson Solutions Asia Pacific Regional Manager Dr. Rukshan Gunatilaka

Peterson Solutions Sri Lanka officially opened its new corporate office at Orion City, Colombo, recently, marking an important milestone in the company’s growth journey and long-term commitment to supporting the businesses across Sri Lanka and the wider region.

The opening ceremony was attended by distinguished guests from the diplomatic and development community, including Iwan Rutjens, Deputy Head of Mission and Chargé d’Affaires a.i. of the Embassy of the Kingdom of the Netherlands in Sri Lanka, who officiated the ribbon-cutting ceremony.

The event also brought together representatives from the European Union, development organisations including the United Nations Industrial Development Organisation and Market Development Facility Australia, industry partners, clients and other key stakeholders.

Peterson Solutions is the consultancy and advisory arm of the globally recognised Peterson and Control Union Group, a multinational organisation with roots dating back to 1920.

Over the past century, the group has grown into a trusted international partner, operating in more than 80 countries, undertaking projects in over 120 countries and serving a global client base of more than 40,000 through a network of over 6,500 professionals.

Built on a legacy of integrity, technical expertise and practical business solutions, Peterson Solutions supports organisations in navigating increasingly complex sustainability, compliance, sourcing, certification and technology-related challenges.

Today, the company delivers a diverse portfolio of services including certification support services, sustainable sourcing solutions, sustainability and ESG Services, technology and digital solutions, training and capacity building through Peterson Academy and international crop consultancy services through Delphy, a Peterson Solutions company.
