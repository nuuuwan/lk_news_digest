Rocell takes home Gold Award at Sri Lanka National Quality Awards

Royal Ceramics Lanka PLC (Rocell), a forerunner in surface coverings and sanitaryware in Sri Lanka, was recently honoured with the Sri Lanka National Quality Award 2025 in the Manufacturing (Large Scale) category. The award is a definitive recognition of the company’s commitment to world-class manufacturing excellence.

The prestigious award was presented to Rocell by the Sri Lanka Standards Institution (SLSI), the National Standards Body under the Ministry of Technology. It stands as a third-party validation of the company’s unparalleled dedication to superior quality, robust management systems, and flawless execution in manufacturing. The SLSI, which represents the country at the International Organisation for Standardisation (ISO), sets benchmarks for its awards based on rigorous performance excellence criteria. This aligns with the globally recognised Malcolm Baldrige Quality Award framework, which is utilised in over 80 countries around the world.

Winning the Gold Award underscores the strength of Rocell’s organisational processes and demonstrates the effectiveness of its improvement strategies, reinforcing its values as a replicable model of excellence in the industry. Utilising meticulous attention to detail, state-of-the-art manufacturing techniques, and an unwavering focus on quality, the company continuously sets standards for Sri Lanka’s flooring and sanitaryware sectors.

This win is a standalone testament to Rocell’s commitment to delivering high-quality, durable, and meticulously crafted products to homes and businesses across the country. It directly translates into guaranteed service, unmatched product quality, and impeccable design for each customer, reaffirming the company’s role in elevating spaces in Sri Lanka. The award both honours present standards and sets a benchmark for the future, inspiring the entire industry to strive for greater heights in manufacturing excellence.

Rocell’s continuous pursuit of perfection ensures that every single product it manufactures truly reflects world-class quality and design, making the company the preferred choice for discerning customers across the country.
