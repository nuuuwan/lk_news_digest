FTZMA contributes Rs. 5 m to ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund

The Free Trade Zone Manufacturers Association (FTZMA) has made a financial contribution of Rs. 5 million to the Government’s ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund, established to provide relief to those affected by the cyclone Ditwah. The cheque was presented on Thursday at the Presidential Secretariat by the FTZMA Chairman Dhammika Fernando, to the Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake. Also in attendance were the FTZMA Honorary Secretary M. H. Z. M. Marzook, and Executive Committee Members Chandani Ekanayake and Lalith Kahatapitiya
