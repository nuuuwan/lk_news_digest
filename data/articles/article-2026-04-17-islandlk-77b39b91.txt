Ambassador of Nepal visits Pathfinder Foundation

The Pathfinder Foundation hosted Dr Purna Bahadur Nepali, Ambassador of Nepal to Sri Lanka, for a discussion on strengthening bilateral institutional cooperation.The meeting was attended by Ambassador (Retd.) Bernard Goonetilleke, Chairman of the Pathfinder Foundation and team.

Both sides reaffirmed the importance of enhancing engagement between Sri Lankan and Nepali think tanks. Focus was placed on promoting South–South cooperation and fostering sustained intellectual exchange on shared interests.

The meeting further explored opportunities for collaboration in areas such as economic relations, public policy, regional security, and the challenges small states face. The significance of fostering academic partnerships, joint research initiatives, policy dialogues, and cultural links—especially those grounded in shared Buddhist heritage—was also highlighted.

Both sides also exchanged views on recent political and economic developments in their respective countries, including the role of youth in shaping governance and reform processes.

The Pathfinder Foundation and the Embassy of Nepal expressed their shared interest in maintaining close engagement and in identifying practical avenues for future collaboration, including exploring potential opportunities to support joint initiatives.
