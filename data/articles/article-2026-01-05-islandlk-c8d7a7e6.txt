‘Singlish’ mashup

Even though the season here was somewhat shaken up, due to Cyclone Ditwah, Rajiv Sebastian says he couldn’t just keep away from the seasonal feeling.

With his band, The Clan, Rajiv was quick to translate and record the popular Christmas hit, ‘Rudolph The Red Nosed Reindeer,’ as a ‘Singlish’ mashup (a blend of Sinhala and English lyrics).

In addition, he got the assistance of his all-time Choreographer Counterpart, Gayani Karunaratna, of Gayani Dance Studio kids, to be a part of this glamorous music video.

The song ‘Rudolph Namathi Muwa Powwa’ is now available on YouTube.

Rajiv also did mention that he and his band members were in no mood to celebrate their 35 years in showbiz with a concert that was schedule for 21st December.
