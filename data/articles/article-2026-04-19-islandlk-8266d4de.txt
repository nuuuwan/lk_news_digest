Ninewells Hospital to redefine surgical care in Sri Lanka

Ninewells Hospital has marked a major milestone with the groundbreaking of its newest building expansion, signaling a bold step forward in advancing healthcare in Sri Lanka.

The ceremony, attended by key stakeholders, partners, and management, celebrated the start of a transformative phase for the hospital. This development reflects Ninewells’ evolution beyond its strong foundation in women’s and children’s healthcare into a more comprehensive, future-ready institution.

The new facility will introduce expanded surgical capabilities, enhanced patient care environments, and state-of-the-art medical infrastructure to meet growing healthcare needs. Designed with advanced technology and patient comfort in mind, it aims to elevate both clinical excellence and patient experience.

Speaking at the event, Dr. Vibash Wijeratne, Director and CEO of Ninewells Hospital said, “This groundbreaking represents our vision for the future of healthcare. As we expand into surgical specialties, our focus remains on delivering compassionate, patient-centered care with the highest standards.”
