SL entrepreneur Sammoon recognised among Global Influential Women in Paris

Entrepreneur Sarrah Sammoon has been recognised in Paris as one of the ‘Influential Women of the World’ for her impactful work in global mobility. The award, judged on measurable societal contribution, was presented at a ceremony held at the Ritz Paris.

Reflecting on the honour, Sammoon connected it directly to her life’s mission. “My work is about bridging the Global North and South, building dignified pathways for people and capital to move responsibly,” she stated.

With three decades of experience, she champions a philosophy that ‘opportunity without meaning is incomplete,’ guiding clients to pursue prosperity and purpose across borders.
