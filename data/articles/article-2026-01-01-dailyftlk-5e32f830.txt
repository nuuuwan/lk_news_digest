Macksons Tiles Lanka records first ever tile export to Australia

Macksons Tiles Lanka Ltd., (Macktiles) has achieved a significant milestone with its first export of tiles to Australia, marking an important step in the company’s international expansion and Sri Lanka’s growing presence in value-added manufacturing.

Manufactured using premium Sri Lankan raw materials and produced with advanced Italian technology, Macktiles products reflect a commitment to precision engineering, durability, and global quality standards. This inaugural shipment underscores the company’s capability to deliver products that meet the expectations of highly competitive international markets.

The successful entry into the Australian market highlights Macktiles, focus on innovation, consistency, and excellence across its production processes. It also demonstrates the strength of Sri Lanka’s manufacturing sector in combining local resources with world-class technology to create products of international relevance.

This export represents more than a commercial transaction; it is a reflection of the confidence placed in Sri Lankan craftsmanship and industrial expertise. As Macktiles expands its footprint beyond national borders, the company remains committed to driving sustainable growth, advancing manufacturing excellence, and contributing to Sri Lanka’s economic development through globally competitive products.
