Sysco LABS wins double honours at Presidential Export Awards

Sysco LABS secured two top accolades at Sri Lanka’s Presidential Export Awards 2024/25. It was named ‘ICT Exporter of the Year’ for a fourth consecutive year and also won ‘Innovative Export Service of the Year’ for SAGE, its enterprise AI platform built in Colombo.

Presented by the Prime Minister, these prestigious awards recognize exceptional contributions to national exports. The wins underscore the company’s leadership in technology exports and its innovative capabilities.

Managing Director Thushera Kawdawatta stated the awards validate the team’s economic impact through foreign revenue and high-value employment, highlighting world-class ingenuity from Sri Lankan talent.

As the Global Innovation Centre for Sysco, the world’s leading foodservice company, Sysco LABS develops critical digital systems supporting its global operations.
