Launch of The J.R. I Disliked

The launch of the book ‘Maa Akamathi J.R.’ (The J.R. I Disliked) based on the J.R. Jayewardene Memorial Lecture delivered by Imthiaz Bakeer Markar, along with its Tamil and English translations, was held yesterday at the J.R. Jayewardene Centre, Colombo 7.
