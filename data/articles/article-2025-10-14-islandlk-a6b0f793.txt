Cabinet nod to issue Dollar Bonds

The Cabinet of Ministers granted approval to the proposal furnished by the President in his office as the Minister of

Finance, Plan Implementation and Economic Development to initiate action to issue  dollar bonds by the Ministry of Finance, Plan Implementation and Economic Development

within the legal framework existing at present as a short – term liquidity tool.
