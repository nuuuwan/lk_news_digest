Imperial Advisors of the WIS Group opens new office

Imperial Advisors, part of the WIS Group, a Sri Lankan company that provides Financial Solutions in the United Kingdom, has officially opened its Conveyancing office in Kohuwela.

The new office was ceremonially declared opened on the 2nd of February 2026 by WIS Group Co-Founder Ifthikar Mohamed to advance a unified Mortgage and Conveyancing approach for its growing client base. The office is designed to streamline and modernise the Conveyancing process by integrating technology-led solutions that reduce delays, minimise administrative exchanges, and deliver a clearer, faster, and more consistent experience for Advisors and clients. By aligning technology, expertise, and service delivery, Imperial Advisors aims to raise the standard of Conveyancing while supporting Advisors with a more efficient and transparent process.

The office is a key part of the wider WIS Group of companies, a one-stop Financial Services ecosystem that includes WIS Accountancy, WIS Mortgages and Insurance, and WIS Wealth. Together, the group offers a seamless approach to Financial, Mortgage, and Advisory Services across the UK. WIS Accountancy, a leading provider of Digital Accountancy, Bookkeeping, Tax, and Business Advisory services in the UK, is currently expanding its operations in both the UK and Sri Lanka. The launch of Imperial Advisors further strengthens the group’s commitment to delivering integrated, technology-driven solutions that support clients throughout their financial and property journeys. The expansion is also expected to naturally drive job creation, presenting significant employment opportunities for prospective candidates, notably Sri Lankan law graduates.

Co-Founder and Director of WIS Group; Suneth Silva stated, “This office opening is a small but important milestone for us. Today marks the beginning of our Conveyancing office, Imperial Advisors, in Kohuwala. This is more than just a new office. It represents the first step towards building a tighter, more connected mortgage and Conveyancing journey, while also recognising the work done to build the technology that underpins it – reducing delays, cutting down back-and-forth, and enabling a faster, clearer, and more consistent Conveyancing process, all with a strong focus on delivering a superior experience for Advisors and clients alike. One ecosystem. One journey. Built the right way.”
