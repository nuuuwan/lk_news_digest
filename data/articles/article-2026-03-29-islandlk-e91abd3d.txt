EWIS launches AI platform to tackle legacy system risk

Sri Lankan technology firm EWIS has unveiled EWIS AEGIS, an AI-powered platform designed to de-risk the modernisation of mission-critical legacy systems.

Targeting sectors such as banking, healthcare, and government, the platform addresses the high costs and operational disruptions traditionally associated with updating core infrastructure. EWIS AEGIS employs a simulation-first approach, utilising AI-driven code intelligence and digital twin technology to replicate and validate system behaviour before any changes are implemented.

This proprietary system, for which a patent is pending, allows organisations to predict outcomes and identify risks in advance. EWIS Chairman Sanjeewa Wickramanayake stated the platform enables businesses to “move from uncertainty to control.”

Engineered in Sri Lanka for global markets, EWIS AEGIS aims to deliver greater cost predictability and reduced downtime for enterprises undertaking large-scale digital transformation.
