Link4 acquires Unifiedpost to strengthen Asia Pacific e-invoicing leadership

Link4 has announced the acquisition of 100% of the shares of Unifiedpost Pte Ltd., from Banqup Group SA. The transaction includes all assets and customer relationships of the Singapore-based e-Invoicing provider.

The acquisition reinforces Link4’s position as a growing regional leader in e-Invoicing across Asia-Pacific and strengthens its presence within Singapore’s InvoiceNow network.

Unifiedpost Pte Ltd., has operated as an accredited provider within Singapore’s InvoiceNow framework. Link4 will continue to support all existing customers and ensure a smooth transition of services. As part of the integration, the Access Point accreditation of Unifiedpost Group and the IRSP accreditation of Unifiedpost Pte Ltd., with IMDA will terminate effective 31 March 2026, with customers continuing under Link4’s established accreditation.

Link4 CEO and Co-Founder Robin Sands said: “Singapore is a strategic digital trade hub in Asia. This acquisition reflects our confidence in the long-term growth of e-Invoicing across the region. Governments throughout Asia-Pacific are accelerating digital mandate programs, and Link4 is building the infrastructure to support businesses in every market adopting these frameworks.”

Founded in 2016, Link4 has expanded from Australia into multiple Asia-Pacific jurisdictions and now supports thousands of organisations across the region. The company focuses exclusively on compliant, scalable e-Invoicing solutions integrated with leading ERP systems.

The acquisition strengthens Link4’s: Singapore market position; Recurring revenue base; Regional customer footprint and Long-term mandate-driven growth strategy.

Link4 has formally welcomed Unifiedpost customers and confirmed continuity of service. GST-registered businesses may continue accessing InvoiceNow services free of charge in line with IMDA support, while non-GST-registered businesses are being offered transitional options to ensure flexibility and choice.

With e-Invoicing mandates expanding across Asia-Pacific, Link4 remains focused on sustainable ARR growth, regional scale, and delivering compliant e-Invoicing and compliance solutions that enable businesses to transact seamlessly across borders.

Link4 is a leading e-Invoicing network provider delivering secure, compliant digital invoicing solutions across the Asia-Pacific region. Founded in Australia in 2016, Link4 enables businesses, accounting firms and software providers to exchange invoices seamlessly through government-mandated e-Invoicing frameworks such as Peppol. The Link4 platform integrates with major ERP and accounting systems, helping organisations automate accounts payable and receivable processes, improve cash flow visibility and meet regulatory compliance requirements. With a rapidly expanding customer base across multiple markets, Link4 continues to support businesses transitioning to the global digital economy.
