AAC Official Sponsor of Colombo Motor Show 2025

A media conference for the forthcoming Colombo Motor Show 2025 to be held from 21st to 23rd November 2025 was held at Galadari Ballroom on September 30, organized by Asia Exhibition & Convention (Pvt) Ltd.

Colombo Motor show 2025 is being conducted for the 19th consecutive year.

The Automobile Association of Ceylon (AAC), oldest and largest motoring organization in Sri Lanka, was recognized as the Official Sponsor and will be the Official Motoring Club. The Association had been associated with the Motor Show from the inception.

The Automobile Association of Ceylon initiated the investment and Devapriya Hettiarachchi – Secretary, AAC handed over a cheque for Rs. 500,000.00 at the media conference to the organizers.

The organizers gave a brief account on the arrangements for the event and the prospective participants and stakeholders who would display their merchandise and motor vehicles.
