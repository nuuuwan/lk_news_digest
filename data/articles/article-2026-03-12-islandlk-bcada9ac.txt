Hayleys Mobility and Kaiyi Auto introduce affordable and fully electric Kaiyi eVibe to Sri Lanka

Hayleys Mobility Limited, the mobility arm of Hayleys Fentons Limited, has entered a strategic partnership with Kaiyi Auto, a rapidly expanding Chinese state-backed automotive manufacturer. This collaboration marks a significant expansion of Hayleys Mobility’s New Energy Vehicle (NEV) portfolio and positions the company at the forefront of delivering sustainable, technology-driven mobility solutions to the Sri Lankan market.

Through this partnership, Hayleys Mobility introduces the Kaiyi eVibe, a contemporary sub-compact electric hatchback. Originally known as the Kaiyi E-Qute 04, the model has been rebranded as Kaiyi eVibe specifically to establish a unique local identity.

Priced at an introductory Rs. 7.9 million, the eVibe comes with a limited launch benefit where the first 100 customers will receive a complimentary 3kW solar system, enabling owners to further reduce energy costs while supporting cleaner, more sustainable mobility. The eVibe delivers a 300 km CLTC-certified range, making it a practical and cost-efficient solution for daily city and suburban travel. As a fully electric, zero-emission vehicle, the eVibe offers a compelling option for families and corporate entities eager to reduce their carbon footprint and align with the global environmental movement.

Reflecting Kaiyi’s emphasis on intelligent engineering and user-centric design, the eVibe is equipped with a 360-degree surround-view camera to enhance driver confidence and a 30-minute fast-charging capability for maximum convenience. A premium six-speaker audio system featuring seamless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto connectivity integrates the driver’s digital lifestyle with an intuitive infotainment interface. Despite its accessible price point, Kaiyi Auto maintains a rigorous focus on innovation through partnerships with global leaders like CATL for advanced battery solutions, ensuring high standards of safety, reliability, and performance.
