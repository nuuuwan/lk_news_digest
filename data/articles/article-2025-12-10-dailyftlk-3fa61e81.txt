Yasantha Kodagoda named Acting Chief Justice

Yasantha Kodagoda takes the oath as Acting Chief Justice before President Anura Kumara Dissanayake (right)

President Anura Kumara Dissanayake has appointed Supreme Court Justice and President’s Counsel Yasantha Kodagoda as Acting Chief Justice.

The appointment was made at the Presidential Secretariat and follows Chief Justice Preethi Padman Surasena’s departure on official overseas travel, the President’s Media Division said.
