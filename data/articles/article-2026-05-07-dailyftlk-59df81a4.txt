GCF-SLEA public lecture on  ‘Sri Lanka’s Current Macroeconomic Policy Directions in the Context of Global Volatility’ tomorrow

The Gamani Corea Foundation (GCF) and Sri Lanka Economic Association (SLEA) will hold a public lecture on ‘Sri Lanka’s Current Macroeconomic Policy Directions in the Context of Global Volatility’ delivered by Central Bank of Sri Lanka (CBSL) Deputy Governor Dr. Chandranath Amarasekara on tomorrow (8), from 4 p.m. at the Lighthouse Auditorium, No. 24, Horton Place, Colombo 7.

This will be followed by a panel discussion involving University of Peradeniya Senior Professor in Economics Prof. O.G. Dayaratne Banda; GCF Honorary Board Director Manjula de Silva, and The Ceylon Chamber of Commerce Economist Arani Rodrigo. It will be moderated by Daily FT Editor/CEO Nisthar Cassim.

The Q&A session will be coordinated by GCF Honorary Chairperson Dr. Harsha Aturupane.  SLEA President and GCF Honorary Deputy Chairperson Prof. Sirimevan Colombage and SLEA General Secretary Nihal Rodrigo will also address event.
