Sri Lanka Insurance Corporation General strengthens national health protection as Official Insurance Partner of Medicare 2026

Sri Lanka Insurance Corporation General Ltd. (SLICGL), the nation’s trusted insurer, continuing to champion the wellbeing of Sri Lankans, through accessible health protection, was the Official Insurance Partner of the Medicare 2026 Health Exhibition, held from 6 to 8 March 2026 at the BMICH, Colombo.

Recognised as Sri Lanka’s largest and most credible healthcare and wellness exhibition, Medicare 2026 attracted over 10,000 visitors, including healthcare professionals, policymakers, industry leaders, and the general public.

SLICGL’s participation at Medicare 2026 was driven by a clear purpose of demonstrating affordable, reliable protection is within reach. Furthermore, for SLICGL, the partnership was a meaningful way to encourage people to care about their health and the health of their families. It was also part of the company’s long-term commitment to the health, security, and peace of mind of every Sri Lankan family, today and in the years ahead.

SLICGL understands illness, injury, and unexpected medical expenses can disrupt even the most carefully planned lives, and for many families across Sri Lanka, the financial burden of a hospitalisation can be as devastating. SLICGL has long championed prevention and preparedness are as important as treatment and having the right cover in place transforms a health crisis from a catastrophe into something manageable.
