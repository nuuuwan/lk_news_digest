Groundworth secures special recognition for land investments at Asia Property Guru Awards 2025

Groundworth (PVT) Ltd has been honored with Special Recognition for Land Investments at the prestigious Asia Property Guru Awards 2025, reaffirming its position as a trusted leader in Sri Lanka’s real estate sector. This accolade celebrates Groundworth’s commitment to delivering strategically located lands with exceptional growth potential, ensuring long-term value for investors.

Kasun Andrahennadi, Co-Founder of Groundworth, stated: “This award is a testament to our vision of creating more than just land—it’s about building a foundation for prosperity. Every project we undertake is designed to maximize investment potential.”

Hans-Christian Gut, Co-Founder of Groundworth, added: “Our promise has always been to provide clients with secure, future-ready investments. Winning this award validates our approach and inspires us to keep innovating and setting new benchmarks in the real estate sector.”

This recognition reinforces Groundworth’s brand philosophy: “Grounds for Growth”, a promise to shape landscapes that nurture financial success and community well-being.
