Russel’s leverages its large-scale event expertise to secure premier sporting partnerships

Russel’s (Pvt) Ltd, a leading player in Sri Lanka’s food and beverage sector, is demonstrating the significant commercial value of its large-scale event catering capabilities, having recently completed a major engagement at one of the country’s most high-profile annual gatherings.

The company served as the official catering partner for the 92nd edition of the ‘Battle of the Saints’ cricket encounter, a three-day event held from March 19 to 21 at the Sinhalese Sports Club (SSC). The engagement saw Russel’s manage the complex logistical challenge of delivering high-quality food and beverage services to thousands of attendees, including students, alumni, and other stakeholders, over the course of the event.

For Russel’s, the partnership underscored the company’s strategic focus on the lucrative and demanding large-scale event sector. With a business model built on extensive experience in high-volume catering, the company deployed its operational framework to ensure a seamless dining experience. Key operational priorities included maintaining rigorous standards of quality and hygiene, managing supply chain efficiency, and delivering a diverse menu capable of catering to a broad spectrum of consumer preferences under high-pressure conditions.
