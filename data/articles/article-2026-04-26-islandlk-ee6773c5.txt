Lanka Hospitals posts record revenue of Rs. 14.47 billion in 2025

Lanka Hospitals Corporation PLC achieved the highest revenue since its inception in 2025, reporting Rs. 14.47 billion, driven by clinical excellence and operational efficiency. The state-owned enterprise (SOE) also delivered record profitability and its largest-ever total dividend payout, marking a milestone for Sri Lanka’s SOE sector.

The hospital’s financial performance is closely tied to clinical volumes. In 2025, Lanka Hospitals performed 802 cardiothoracic surgeries – the most by any private hospital in the country – and over 390 bariatric surgeries with zero mortality, a world-class benchmark. Diagnostic volumes also set records, with more than 10,000 MRI scans and 8,000 CT scans.

As a premier SOE, Lanka Hospitals contributed over Rs. 1.4 billion to the state treasury through taxes and dividends, reinforcing a model that balances public service with commercial viability.

“Achieving a revenue of Rs. 14.47 billion while maintaining zero mortality in complex surgical lines demonstrates that our growth is driven by trust and responsible practices,” said Chaaminda Kumarasiri, Group Managing Director.
