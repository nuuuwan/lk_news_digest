Metalabs wins Enterprise category award for mAudit at 2025 ICSDB Excellence Awards

The Metalabs team accepting the Silver Award in the Client Implementations (Enterprise) category for their mAudit platform at the ICSDB Excellence Awards 2025

Metalabs has been honoured with the Silver Award in the ‘Client Implementations (Enterprise) category’ at the 2025 ICSDB Excellence Awards for its flagship compliance platform, mAudit.

The prestigious recognition highlights Metalabs’ growing impact in the global enterprise technology landscape and its commitment to delivering enterprise-grade digital solutions that enable organisations to manage compliance, risk, and audit processes with greater confidence, efficiency, and transparency. Held as part of the ICSDB Excellence Awards 2025, the ceremony brought together leading technology providers and innovators from across the region. mAudit stood out for its innovative approach to automating audit and compliance workflows and for delivering measurable, real-world value to enterprise clients operating in complex regulatory environments.

mAudit is a comprehensive compliance and audit automation platform designed to support organisations of all sizes, with a particular focus on the needs of large and complex enterprises. The platform helps businesses streamline internal controls and regulatory processes while accelerating readiness for critical global frameworks such as SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, and other industry-specific standards. Through customisable workflows, intelligent risk mapping, and AI-assisted insights, the platform reduces the manual burden traditionally associated with audits and compliance management.

Its unified architecture bridges audit, risk, compliance, and ESG reporting within a single platform, enabling organisations to capture evidence efficiently, continuously monitor control effectiveness, and generate comprehensive reports with improved accuracy and reduced effort. The platform’s intuitive interface and enterprise-grade security features make it particularly suited for organisations managing distributed teams, multiple regulatory obligations, and evolving governance requirements. By improving visibility and accountability across compliance programmes, mAudit empowers leadership teams to make informed decisions while strengthening organisational trust and resilience.

Winning the Silver Award in the ‘Enterprise category’ represents a significant milestone for Metalabs and serves as strong industry validation of mAudit’s practical impact. The recognition underscores the company’s ability to deliver scalable, secure, and future-ready solutions that address the growing compliance and governance challenges faced by enterprises worldwide

Metalabs noted that the award reflects the dedication and expertise of its team, as well as its continued focus on building technology that solves real business problems. The achievement further reinforces Metalabs’ position as a trusted partner for organisations seeking to modernise their audit and compliance functions. As regulatory expectations and digital transformation initiatives continue to accelerate globally, Metalabs remains committed to advancing innovation in audit, risk, and governance technologies, further establishing mAudit as a solution of choice for enterprises navigating an increasingly complex and regulated business environment.
