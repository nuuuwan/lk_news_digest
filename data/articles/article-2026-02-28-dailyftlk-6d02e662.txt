Russel’s Wellness at Gulfood 2026

Russel’s Wellness recently showcased its Ayurveda-inspired wellness teas and medicinal formulations at Gulfood 2026, one of the world’s most prestigious food and beverage exhibitions.

The event brought together global buyers, distributors, and industry leaders, creating meaningful conversations around heritage, quality, and innovation. A special highlight of the exhibition was the distinguished visit by Trade, Commerce, Food Security and Cooperative Development Wasantha Samarasinghe, who engaged in a thoughtful discussion with the Founder of Russel’s Wellness at the stall.

The exchange reflected growing international recognition for Sri Lanka’s heritage wellness sector and the role Russel’s plays in elevating it to global markets.

From curated tastings to strategic discussions with overseas partners, the response was both inspiring and promising. The strong interest from global importers reaffirmed that authentic Ceylon wellness, rooted in tradition and crafted to export-ready standards, continues to resonate powerfully across international markets.

Gulfood 2026 marked not just participation, but progress, as Russel’s Wellness advances its mission of taking Sri Lanka’s rich wellness legacy to the world.
