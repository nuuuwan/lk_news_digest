David Pieris Automobiles delivers BAIC X55 fleet to Chevron Lubricants Lanka

David Pieris Automobiles Ltd., (DPA) has marked another significant milestone with the delivery of eight (08) BAIC X55 vehicles to Chevron Lubricants Lanka PLC, which markets the strong Caltex brand in Sri Lanka.

This delivery reinforces David Pieris Automobiles’ continued efforts to strengthen its portfolio of internationally acclaimed brands while supporting corporate customers with robust after-sales service, nationwide support and trusted mobility solutions.

The fleet acquisition was facilitated through financing support from Central Finance Company PLC.  This strategic financial partnership further strengthened the collaboration, ensuring efficient delivery and deployment of the BAIC X55 fleet.

Officials representing David Pieris Automobiles, together with representatives from BAIC principals, were present at the handover event, highlighting the strong partnership between the local distributor and the global automotive manufacturer. Their presence reaffirmed a shared commitment to delivering world-class mobility solutions tailored to the evolving needs of Sri Lankan enterprises. Notably, there is growing confidence amongst leading multinational brands in the BAIC X55 — a premium compact SUV that combines modern design, advanced technology and dependable performance, making it well-suited for corporate and operational mobility requirements.

DPA, a member of the David Pieris Group, serves as the Group’s dedicated four-wheeler sales arm. Committed to bringing globally renowned vehicle brands to Sri Lanka, DPA continues to usher in a new era of innovation, quality and exceptional customer service for Sri Lankan motorists.
