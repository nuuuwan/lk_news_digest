Pan Asia Bank expands digital payment solutions through new strategic partnership

Pan Asia Bank, has taken another step forward in strengthening its digital banking capabilities by signing a strategic agreement with WEBXPAY to provide seamless, secure, and innovative payment solutions to its customers and merchant base.

This partnership is designed to accelerate financial inclusion and provide greater support for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) across the country. Merchants will now have access to an expanded range of online and in-store payment facilities, enabling them to accept card payments, QR codes, installment facilities, and multi-currency transactions with ease.

Speaking on the occasion, Naleen Edirisinghe Director/CEO of Pan Asia Bank said “Our goal is to make digital financial services more inclusive, convenient, and future-ready. By collaborating with a strong fintech partner, we can provide businesses with tools to expand, thrive, and deliver greater value to their customers.”
