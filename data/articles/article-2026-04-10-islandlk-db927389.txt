CEAT Kelani adjudged best-managed company in Sri Lanka

CEAT Kelani Holdings shared the overall Gold award for Best Management Practices and was honoured with four other significant accolades at the 2026 edition of the Best Management Practices Company Awards of the Institute of Chartered Professional Managers of Sri Lanka (CPM Sri Lanka), to be adjudged the country’s best-managed company.

The company also received the Sector Award as Winner in the Manufacturing (Tyre & Rubber) category, was ranked among the ‘Forty Outstanding Companies’ at the awards, and was presented a Best Management Practices Excellence Award. In addition, CEAT Kelani Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer Ravi Dadlani was recognised with the Leadership Excellence award, underscoring the strength of the company’s leadership and strategic direction.

The CPM Best Management Practices Company Awards recognise public and private sector organisations for excellence in management across leadership, policies and strategies, people management, partnerships and resources, processes and performance, based on the year under review.

Commenting on the awards, CEAT Kelani Managing Director & CEO Ravi Dadlani said: “To be named the best-managed company in Sri Lanka is an extraordinary honour as well as a reward for years of hard work in times of challenge. This recognition reflects the company’s sustained focus on embedding best-in-class management practices across its operations with the involvement of employees at every level.”
