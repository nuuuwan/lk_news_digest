AICPA and CIMA CXO Forum 2026 on 8 April

AICPA and CIMA will host their annual CXO Forum 2026 on 8 April 2026 at Cinnamon Life, Colombo, bringing together senior business and finance leaders from across the island to share insights and explore the strategic opportunities shaping Sri Lanka’s next phase of growth.

Held under the theme “From Recovery to Resilience – The World Is Being Reordered”, the forum will focus on how organisations can navigate disruption, strengthen resilience, and position themselves for longterm, sustainable growth.

The event will be opened by John Graham FCMA (UK), CGMA, CIMA President and CoChair of the Association of International Certified Professional Accountants who will share a global perspective on the evolving role of the finance profession in a rapidly changing world.

The forum will also feature a panel discussion moderated by Venkkat Ramanan FCMA (UK), CGMA, Vice President – APAC at the Association of International Certified Professional Accountants focused on how Sri Lanka’s business and finance leaders can respond to change and position their organisations to evolve and thrive in the years ahead.
