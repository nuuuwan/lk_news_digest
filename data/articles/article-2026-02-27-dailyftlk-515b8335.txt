Cabinet approves UL’s passenger and cargo handling contracts worth € 9.63 m

Two international agents have won contracts to provide passenger and ramp handling services and warehouse handling services to national carrier—SriLankan Airlines at the Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport, France for three years.

The Cabinet of Ministers on Monday cleared recommendations by the Standing High-Level Procurement Committee to award the passenger and ramp handling services and warehouse handling services agent’s contracts to Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport, France (CDG) for three years.

The Cabinet approved to award a € 7.73 million contract for passenger and ramp handling services at CDG to Groupe Europe Handling for a period of three years from 1 April 2026 and a € 1.90 million contract for cargo handling services at CDG to France Handling Cargo for a period of three years from 1 February 2026.
