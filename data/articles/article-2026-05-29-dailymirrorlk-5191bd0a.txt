Sri Lanka announce squad for ICC Women’s T20 World Cup 2026

Colombo, May 29 (Daily Mirror) :The Sri Lanka Cricket Selection Panel has named a 15-member squad for the ICC Women’s T20 World Cup 2026, with all-rounder Chamari Athapaththu set to lead the side.

Sri Lanka will depart for England on 3 June 2026 ahead of the tournament, which is scheduled to be held from 12 June to 5 July across England and Wales.
