First Capital appoints Lasith Nanayakkara as CTO

First Capital Holdings PLC, a subsidiary of JXG (Janashakthi Group) and a pioneering full-service investment institution, has announced the appointment of Lasith Nanayakkara as the Chief Transformation Officer.

Lasith, a technology leader with over 35 years of international experience, brings deep expertise in digital transformation, enterprise architecture, and technology modernisation. His career has spanned key markets including Australia, New Zealand, the USA, Japan, Cambodia, the UK, and Sri Lanka, covering sectors such as Banking and Finance, Insurance, Retail, Leisure and Entertainment, Manufacturing and Healthcare. He has led major digital initiatives for multi-million-dollar organisations, integrating business strategy with advanced technological capabilities.
