Macksons Holdings donates  Rs. 15 m to ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’

Macksons Holdings Chairman Milfer Makeen (left) handing over the cheque to Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake

Macksons Holdings made a contribution of Rs. 15 million to the Government’s ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund which was established to provide relief and support to communities affected by Cyclone Ditwah.

Macksons Holdings Chairman Milfer Makeen formally handed over the donation at the Presidential Secretariat to the Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake earlier this week.

This meaningful contribution reflects Macksons Holdings deep empathy and concern for families and communities facing significant challenges due to the disaster. The company emphasises the importance of solidarity and collective responsibility during times of national hardship.

Through this gesture, Macksons Holdings reaffirms its commitment to standing alongside the people of Sri Lanka, supporting the nation’s recovery, and contributing to efforts that help rebuild affected communities with strength, resilience, and unity.
