BestWeb.LK competition calls for submissions

BestWeb.LK, Sri Lanka’s premium website competition and the benchmark for digital excellence in Sri Lanka, is officially calling for applications and invites all .lk website owners to submit their websites for the opportunity to gain national recognition.

Organised by the LK Domain Registry and now in its 16th edition, BestWeb.LK recognises outstanding .lk websites across 24 industry categories.

The competition features 126 awards, including Gold, Silver and Bronze accolades, along with special recognitions such as Best Mobile-Optimised Website, Most Popular Website, Best Web Developer and Overall Awards.

The BestWeb.LK award categories cover almost every industry across the island. Categories include Advertising and Marketing, Automotive, Banking, Corporate, e-Commerce, Education, Fashion and Lifestyle, Finance and Insurance, Food and Beverage, Government, Health and Wellness, Manufacturing and Industrial, Media and Entertainment, Nonprofit, School, Sports, Technology, Travel and Tourism and more.

The websites are evaluated based on design, performance, content quality, technical excellence, security, user experience and overall innovation.

Sending applications in early gives organisations a significant advantage as their website can benefit from insights in the TopWeb.LK report, which highlights areas for improvement in each website. This provides the organisation valuable time to fine-tune the website, boost performance and optimise user experience before judging.

Domain Registrar and LK Domain Registry CEO Prof. Gihan Dias stated, “Over the years, BestWeb.LK has established itself as the national benchmark for recognising outstanding Sri Lankan websites, spanning every major sector of the economy. BestWeb.LK is more than a competition; it’s a platform that celebrates Sri Lanka’s digital progress. It continues to encourage, recognise and reward higher standards, better user experiences and innovation across the local web space. If you own or manage a .lk website, this is your opportunity to gain national recognition, build credibility and stand out in your field.”

The BestWeb.LK selection process is designed to be fair, transparent and focused on continuous improvement, guiding participants through several structured stages. It begins with registration, which ends on June 8, followed by screening and feedback to highlight areas for enhancement.

Next comes the rating and review phase, where the websites receive public input, which will be shared with the owners, leading into expert evaluation and nominee selection by industry professionals.

The journey concludes with the final awards presentation in a grand ceremony, bringing together industry leaders, business professionals and web developers to celebrate digital excellence and recognise the outstanding achievements in web development.

Together, these stages help the participants clearly understand their current standing and identify the ways to further improve their websites.
