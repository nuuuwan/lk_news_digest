Commercial Bank takes state-of-the-art banking to Kaluwanchikudy

Committed to delivering convenient banking solutions for all, the Commercial Bank of Ceylon has expanded its footprint to Kaluwanchikudy, with the opening of its newest branch in this scenic coastal town southeast of Batticaloa in the Eastern Province.

S. Prabagar, Chief Operating Officer of Commercial Bank officiated as the Chief Guest at the opening of this branch, at a ceremony also attended by Hasrath Munasinghe, Deputy General Manager – Corporate Banking, S. Ganeshan, Deputy General Manager – Personal Banking and Mr R. Sivagnanam, Assistant General Manager – Personal Banking.

The new Kaluwanchikudy branch, located at No. 300, Main Street, Kaluwanchikudy.
