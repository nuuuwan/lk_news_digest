IMC Education and Plymouth Marjon University launch BSc in Applied Psychology in Sri Lanka

IMC Education, in partnership with Plymouth Marjon University, United Kingdom, has officially launched the BSc (Hons) in Applied Psychology programme in Sri Lanka, marking a significant expansion of internationally recognised psychological education in the country.

The launch event featured a panel discussion on Teenage Mental Health & Psychological First Aid, addressing one of Sri Lanka’s most pressing public health challenges. Distinguished speakers included Dr. Amila B. Kulathunga (CEO, IMC Education), Prof. Inoka C. Perera (Director of Academics), and Dr. Katheryn Edwards from Plymouth Marjon University, alongside other academic and clinical professionals.

The programme follows British Psychological Society (BPS)-accredited curriculum standards, providing students with a rigorous academic foundation and practical competencies for careers in clinical, educational, counselling, and community psychology settings.
