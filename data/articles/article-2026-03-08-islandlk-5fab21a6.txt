Berendina secures dual recognition at CA Sri Lanka TAGS Awards 2025

Berendina Micro Investments Company Limited (BMIC) and Berendina Development Services (Guarantee) Limited (BDS) have both received Bronze Awards at the CA Sri Lanka TAGS Awards 2025. BMIC, the nation’s first licensed microfinance company, was honored for financial reporting excellence, while BDS was recognized in the Not-for-Profit category for transparent reporting and community development. BMIC serves over 100,000 customers with a LKR 6.8 billion loan portfolio, and BDS supports 25,000 families annually.

Chairman Anura Atapattu stated the awards validate that transparency is core to the organization’s mission, demonstrating that every rupee is accounted for.
