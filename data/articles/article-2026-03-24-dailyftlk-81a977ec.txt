Prym Intimates expands global footprint with new manufacturing facility in Vietnam

Prym Intimates, a division of the Germany-based William Prym Group, has opened its newest manufacturing facility in Hung Yen, Vietnam, in close proximity to Hanoi.

The plant commenced operations on 3 March 2026 and marks the company’s fourth manufacturing location globally.

The William Prym Group, established in 1530, is one of the world’s oldest family-owned industrial companies operating four divisions at present, with Prym Intimates serving the global intimate apparel sector.

Prym Intimates specialises in manufacturing accessories used in intimate apparel, including components for bras and related products and operates manufacturing facilities in Sri Lanka, China and Indonesia.

The new Vietnam facility strengthens Prym Intimates’ global production network, enabling the company to serve intimate apparel brands and vendors across key sourcing regions. With manufacturing spanning four countries, customers can source accessories closer to their garment production locations, helping reduce lead times and increase supply chain flexibility.

Prym Intimates Divisional Head Vasu Wijegoonawardane said in today’s increasingly volatile and fast-moving market environment, speed and agility have become critical for success. Prym Intimates’ expanded manufacturing footprint allows brands and vendors to respond quickly to changing customer demands while benefiting from reliable and efficient supply.

Operating across multiple locations, Prym Intimates ensures consistent product quality and service standards worldwide through standardised manufacturing platforms, processes and technology across all facilities, allowing customers to source from any Prym location with confidence.

Recognised for its innovation, design capabilities and continuous product development, the company focuses on anticipating market changes, adapting quickly, and delivering solutions that create value for its business partners.

Guided by a philosophy of being close to the needle point, Prym Intimates works closely with brands and vendors to collaborate on solutions that are both fast and cost-competitive, reinforcing its role as a trusted partner to the global intimate apparel industry.
