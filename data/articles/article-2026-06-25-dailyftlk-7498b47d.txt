High Commission of Canada relocates

The High Commission of Canada to Sri Lanka and the Maldives has relocated its Chancery from 5th Lane to the renovated premises on Srimath R. G. Senanayake Mawatha in Colombo 7.

The move reflects Canada’s continued commitment to strengthening its engagement with the government and public of Sri Lanka and the Maldives.

Visitors are advised that all appointments and correspondence should be directed to the new address starting from 24 June, 2026. Address: No. 6, Srimath R. G. Senanayake Mawatha, Colombo 07, Sri Lanka.

The High Commission urged visitors to consult its website for information about its programs, contacts and services: https://www.international.gc.ca/country-pays/sri_lanka/colombo.aspx?lang=eng
