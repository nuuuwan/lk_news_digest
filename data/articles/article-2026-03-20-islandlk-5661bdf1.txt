Aberdeen Holdings marks 30th anniversary honouring the people behind three decades of progress

Aberdeen Holdings marks its 30th anniversary, reflecting on a journey shaped not only by growth and diversification but also by the people who have stood behind the organisation for three decades. Established in March 1995, the Group has evolved into a diversified conglomerate operating across seven sectors through 18+ companies, supported by a workforce of over 1,800 employees.

While scale and longevity are notable achievements, Aberdeen’s leadership emphasises that the Group’s real strength lies in continuity – in the individuals whose long-term commitment has provided stability through changing markets and economic cycles.

To honour this legacy, Aberdeen Holdings has introduced Decades of Dedication, an initiative recognising long-serving employees whose careers have been closely intertwined with the Group’s progress. More than 550 employees have completed over five years of service, with many contributing well beyond a decade. Their experience and institutional knowledge continue to play a critical role in operational excellence, leadership development, and organisational resilience.

Chairman Sattar Kassim reflected on the milestone, stating: “Thirty years of sustained growth is never the achievement of a single moment or a single leader. It is built on the consistent dedication of people who believed in the organisation and chose to grow alongside it. Decades of Dedication recognises those individuals whose commitment formed the foundation of Aberdeen Holdings.”

The programme acknowledges employees across tiered service milestones – Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Ruby, Emerald, and Diamond – symbolising careers strengthened over time. Beyond formal recognition, the initiative reinforces a culture that values loyalty, experience, and long-term contribution.

“Our people carry the institutional memory of this Group – its values, discipline, and resilience,” Kassim added. “As Aberdeen enters its fourth decade, this continuity will remain central to how we build the future.”

As Aberdeen Holdings looks ahead, the Group reaffirms its belief that sustainable success is rooted not only in expansion and performance but also in enduring relationships – with people at the heart of every chapter.
