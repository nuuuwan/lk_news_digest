ITC Ratnadipa presents Easter Brunch on April 5 at Indian Ocean Pavilion

This Easter, ITC Ratnadipa invites guests to celebrate the season with a special brunch at the Indian Ocean Pavilion on Sunday, April 5, from 12:30 PM to 3:30 PM.

The Easter brunch offers a festive and relaxing afternoon, complemented by a range of interactive and playful activities for children, alongside a stunning view of the Indian Ocean. Priced at LKR 9,500 nett per person, children dine at half rate.

The brunch features live cuisine theatres, allowing families to enjoy an international buffet celebrating the spirit of Easter with a mix of global spring flavours and local favourites.
