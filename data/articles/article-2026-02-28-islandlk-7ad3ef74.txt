Altair Showrooms: Strategically Chosen by the World’s Leading Automotive Brands

Altair’s prominence on Colombo’s skyline is now mirrored at street level.

A growing number of global automotive brands have strategically chosen to locate their flagship showrooms at Altair, drawn by its iconic architecture, prime positioning by Beira Lake, and reputation as one of the city’s most prestigious addresse.

Leading brands, including BYD, DENZA, TATA, JAECOO, OMODA, and KIA, are establishing a strong presence at Altair. TATA, JAECOO and Omoda showrooms are already operational, while BYD, DENZA, and KIA are set to open shortly — bringing sophisticated and experiential automotive shopping directly to Colombo’s most distinctive development.

This clustering of world renowned brands is no coincidence. “Altair was always envisioned as more than a residential development; it was designed as a landmark destination. In Sri Lanka. Automotive brands are a powerful expression of aspiration, luxury, good design and innovation – values that underpin the Altair residences. As such the presence of showrooms designed to international standards, reinforces Altair’s position as a premium address chosen by global brands, represented by reputed corporates such as John Keells Holdings, Hayleys and DIMO,” says Thilan Wijesinghe.

This concentration of leading automotive brands on the ground floor complemented by food & beverage offerings on the first floor, reinforces Altair as a lifestyle brand anchor and a location chosen for visibility, stature, and alignment with architectural distinction, catering to those with sophisticated tastes.
