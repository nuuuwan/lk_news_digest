APHNH elects new Governing Council for 2025–2027

The Association of Private Hospitals and Nursing Homes (APHNH) has elected a new Governing Council for the 2025–2027 term. Raveen Wickramasinghe, CEO of the Ruhunu Hospital Group, will lead as President.

Outlining his vision, Wickramasinghe emphasized strengthening the private healthcare ecosystem through policy advocacy, quality enhancement, and international collaboration. He reaffirmed APHNH’s role as an essential partner in the national healthcare system.

Founded in 1972, APHNH represents registered private healthcare providers and works to advance ethical, patient-centered care across Sri Lanka. The new council aims to elevate professional standards and support the sector’s strategic growth.
