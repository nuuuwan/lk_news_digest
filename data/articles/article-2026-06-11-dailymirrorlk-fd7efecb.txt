Schools rugby league heats up in week 2

June.11 (Mirror Sports) - The 2026 Schools Rugby League Tournament moves into its second round this weekend, with several blockbuster encounters set to light up the Division ‘A’ competition.

Traditional rivals and title hopefuls will battle for crucial early-season points, with clashes such as St. Joseph’s vs St. Anthony’s, Royal vs Trinity, Isipathana vs St. Peter’s, and Ananda vs S. Thomas’ among the standout fixtures scheduled across June 12–14.
