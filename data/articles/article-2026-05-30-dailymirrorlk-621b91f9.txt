Rooftop solar owners asked to switch off systems

Colombo, May 30 (Daily Mirror)- The National System Operator (Private) Limited (NSO) has requested rooftop solar panel owners across the country to voluntarily switch off their solar power systems until 3:00 p.m. today to help ensure the reliable and stable operation of the national electricity grid during the Vesak festival period.

According to the NSO, the request has been made due to the significantly low electricity demand currently observed in the system, which requires careful management to maintain grid stability.

The operator emphasized that public cooperation is crucial to ensuring a continuous and safe electricity supply for all consumers during the festive period.

Accordingly, rooftop solar panel owners have been urged to comply with the temporary measure as a contribution towards maintaining the stability of the national grid.
