Chamindry Saparamadu appointed to OXFAM Global Assembly

Prominent Sri Lankan development professional, Chamindry Saparamadu, who currently serves as the Executive Director of DevPro has been appointed as a Delegate of the OXFAM Global Assembly with effect from 04th March 2026.

OXFAM Global Assembly, designed to be a multistakeholder body, comprises of Affiliate Delegates and Stakeholder Delegates. It is the highest governance body of the OXFAM Confederation that is responsible for strategic review and approval of OXFAM’s global strategy, establishing the competency framework for the OXFAM International Board, and the appointment of new Board members and setting general principles and frameworks for global policies across the entire Confederation. Delegates in the Global Assembly hold significant governance powers and hold a vote in these key organizational decisions.

Saparamadu is a transformational leader with over 25+ years of experience in policy design and institutional development with a strong academic foundation in economics, law and international development. She currently serves as the Executive Director of DevPro, a legacy organization of OXFAM Sri Lanka working towards inclusive economic development driving systemic change by strengthening markets, enhancing livelihoods, and catalyzing sustainable practices in agriculture, climate resilience, skills and development education, and tourism.
