Mistletoe 2025: A festive night of music with a mission

The Rotary Club of Colombo Centennial proudly ushers in the festive season with Mistletoe 2025, a spectacular Christmas musical extravaganza that promises to be both a celebration of the holidays and a powerful force for good.

The event is set for 5th December 2025, at the beautiful Avenra Garden, Negombo. With a unique blend of festive cheer and high-calibre musical performance, Mistletoe 2025 is anticipated to draw an audience of over 800 fans, making it one of the most vibrant and impactful Rotary-led entertainment nights of the year.

Beyond the stage lights and carols, Mistletoe 2025 is, first and foremost, a vital fundraising initiative. All proceeds are dedicated to fueling the Rotary Club of Colombo Centennial’s impactful community service projects, particularly those focused on empowering local communities in the post-conflict areas. Every ticket supports the Club’s mission to drive resilience, inclusion, and opportunity across Sri Lanka.

Guests can look forward to an evening featuring Bathiya & Santhush, the iconic duo redefining Sri Lankan music. Falan Andrea, celebrated for her soulful artistry. C+ Band led by Chandumal, delivering dynamic rhythms and festive flair.
