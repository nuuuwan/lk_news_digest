Bally’s celebrates 30 years in entertainment sector, honours over 300 employees

Bally’s celebrated three decades as a Sri Lankan entertainment leader at its Grand Awards 2025. Held at ITC Ratnadipa Colombo recently, the ceremony honoured over 300 employees for dedication, loyalty, and exceptional performance across various categories.

Chairman Anuradha Perera attended as Chief Guest. The event, blending traditional and modern themes, reflected Bally’s evolution. Speeches by the CEO and CHRO highlighted the people-centric culture and collective journey.
