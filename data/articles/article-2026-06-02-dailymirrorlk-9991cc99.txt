Buddha Rashmi Vesak Zone concludes in Colombo

Opposition Leader Sajith Premadasa declared open the final day of the Buddha Rashmi Vesak Zone at Colombo’s Gangaramaya Temple on June 2, 2026. The event, jointly organized by the Hunupitiya Gangaramaya Temple, the Presidential Secretariat, and the Prime Minister’s Office, was attended by Ven. Dr. Pallegama Rathanasara Thero of Gangaramaya Temple. Deputy Minister of Digital Economy Eranga Weeraratne and Member of Parliament Harshan Rajakaruna were also present. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
