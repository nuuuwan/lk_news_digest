Bloombird launches Kalubowila apartment project

Bloombird Real Estate has announced its latest residential development, “Westbury Urbanside,” in the popular suburb of Kalubowila. This follows the group’s successful completion of “Westbury Residencies” on Campbell Avenue.

The new project features only 28 exclusive three-bedroom units with a study room, emphasizing spacious layouts, natural light, and privacy. It includes modern amenities such as a swimming pool and gym, targeting families and professionals.

Managing Director Muhez Muzzamil stated the project reflects a commitment to curated, quality living rather than high-density development. Insights from their first project have informed enhanced space optimization and construction quality.

Strategically located, the complex offers easy access to schools, healthcare, and major roads. A model apartment is now open for viewing, allowing potential buyers to inspect finishes and design firsthand. Construction is currently underway.
