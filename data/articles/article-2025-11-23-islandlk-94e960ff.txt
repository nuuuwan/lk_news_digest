Sri Lanka Insurance Life shines at SLIM National Sales Awards 2025

Sri Lanka Insurance Life marked a momentous achievement at the prestigious SLIM National Sales Awards 2025, with its sales team being recognized for their unwavering dedication and excellence in the field.

S A Joshua (SLIC Life Bancassurance – Cluster Manger) received the Silver Award in the Territory Manager Category, while. S J Prashanthi (SLIC Life Bancassurance – Area Manger) was honored with the Silver Award in the Sales Supervisor Category.

These accolades underscore the exceptional commitment, expertise, and leadership of these professionals, reflecting the high standards upheld by Sri Lanka Insurance Life in the competitive insurance industry. The awards further affirm the company’s relentless pursuit of excellence and its pivotal role in shaping innovative and customer-focused solutions.

The SLIM National Sales Awards 2025, held on October 28, 2025, at the Monarch Imperial, celebrated top-performing sales professionals across various industries, making it a significant platform to honor outstanding achievements.
