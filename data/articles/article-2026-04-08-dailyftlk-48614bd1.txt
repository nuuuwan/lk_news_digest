Vogue Jewellers achieves double recognition at BMPC Awards 2026

Vogue Jewellers Ltd. has been honoured with two prestigious accolades at the Best Management Practices Company (BMPC) Awards 2026, organised by the Institute of Chartered Professional Managers of Sri Lanka (CPM Sri Lanka).

The company received a Merit Award in the highly competitive People/HR Governance (ESG) category and was named Winner in the Manufacturing Sector (Diamonds, Gems and Jewellery) for the second consecutive year, in recognition of Vogue Jewellers’ commitment to operational excellence, ethical governance and sustainable business practices.

The Merit Award in People/HR Governance (ESG) highlights the company’s robust human resource frameworks, inclusive workplace culture and alignment with Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles. Through targeted initiatives focused on employee development, ethical practices and responsible people strategies, Vogue Jewellers has strengthened its organisational foundation, fostering a performance-driven yet caring environment that values diversity, equity and long-term talent growth.

Simultaneously, the repeat win in the Manufacturing Sector (Diamonds, Gems and Jewellery) category reaffirms Vogue Jewellers’ leadership position in an intensely competitive industry. This back-to-back success reflects the company’s consistent delivery of innovation, superior quality standards and operational efficiency, while upholding the highest levels of craftsmanship and integrity that have defined its legacy.

“These achievements are a proud reflection of the collective dedication and hard work of our entire team,” said Managing Director Anura Hemachandra. “Our employees’ commitment to excellence has been a significant driving force behind our success. We remain firmly dedicated to continuous improvement, investing in people development and upholding the highest standards across every aspect of our operations.”

The BMPC Awards 2026, held on 27 March 2026 at Monarch Imperial, Sri Jayewardenepura Kotte, celebrate organisations that demonstrate outstanding management practices, strategic leadership and professional excellence. Evaluation covers key areas including leadership, strategy, people management, processes and performance outcomes.

These dual honours position Vogue Jewellers not only as a trusted name in fine jewellery but also as a benchmark for progressive, people-centric corporate culture in Sri Lanka’s manufacturing landscape. The company continues to blend traditional craftsmanship with modern, sustainable business practices to set new industry standards.
