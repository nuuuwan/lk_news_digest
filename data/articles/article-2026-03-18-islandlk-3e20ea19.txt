ICBS celebrates academic and sporting excellence at SQA graduation ceremony and Colours Night 2026

On 18 February 2026, Imperial College of Business Studies hosted the SQA Graduation Ceremony together with Colours Night 2026 at the Centre for Banking Studies, bringing together students, parents, faculty, and distinguished guests for an evening that celebrated both academic excellence and sporting achievement.

The ceremony marked a significant milestone as more than 100 graduates were formally conferred with their qualifications. In addition, over 70 students were recognised for their outstanding performances in sports during the 2024 and 2025 season, reflecting the institution’s strong emphasis on holistic development.

The event was graced by Miss Margaret Curran, International Regional Manager South Asia of the Scottish Qualifications Authority, who attended as the Chief Guest. Her presence reinforced ICBS’ longstanding international partnerships and its commitment to delivering globally recognised qualifications that open doors for students both locally and internationally.

Also in attendance were the Dean of ICBS, Dr. M. Ganeshamoorthy, and Deputy Dean of ICBS, Dr. Royston Cumine. They were joined by Academic Director Imperial College of Business Studies, Pravinth Rajaratnam; Director Strategic Initiatives, Shanmugam Sivabaskaran; and Director Strategy and Operations, Chinthaka Wijerathna. Their collective presence underscored the institution’s leadership commitment to academic distinction and student achievement.

Adding inspiration to the evening was Guest of Honour Dilantha Malagamuwa, who commended student athletes for their dedication and achievements. Students who represented ICBS in Cricket, Rugby, Football, Badminton, Basketball, Netball, Swimming, and Athletics were presented with awards in recognition of their commitment, discipline, and teamwork.
