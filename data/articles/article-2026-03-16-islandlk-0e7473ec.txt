Technomedics marks over three decades of advancing healthcare in Sri Lanka

Technomedics, one of Sri Lanka’s most trusted medical technology companies, marks over 30 years of dedicated service to the nation’s healthcare sector, reaffirming its long-standing commitment to saving lives, improving patient outcomes, and strengthening the country’s healthcare ecosystem. Since its humble beginnings, Technomedics gradually grew into a formidable and respected partner to Sri Lanka’s public and private healthcare institutions. Today, the company is acknowledged not just as a supplier of medical devices and equipment but also as a strategic healthcare partner that is essential to elevating clinical standards, introducing technologies that are ready for the future, and empowering medical professionals across the nation.

Over the last three decades, Technomedics has worked closely with doctors, specialists, nurses, biomedical teams, hospital administrators, and policymakers to deliver world-class medical solutions tailored to local healthcare needs. Its portfolio includes cutting-edge medical innovations that were introduced in collaboration with leading global principals and are supported by extensive knowledge transfer, training, and after-sales service.
