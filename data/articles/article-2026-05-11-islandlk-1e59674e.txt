Ambition … to be a famous singer

His ambition is to be a famous singer, one day, and Kalum Tharanga has the potential, and the experience, to succeed.

He says from 1996 to 2001 he played the octapad and sang with a band, called Scorpions.

He then moved to Dubai and joined the Sahara Flash music band.

Fourteen years later he teamed up Rhythm of Clarence as their main vocalist.

Kalum’s overseas experience also include performances in the Maldives and India, as a vocalist, mainly doing the English hits.

He continues to play the drums and sings, as well, and, what’s more, he has five original songs to his credit.

His one ambition, he says, is to be a famous singer and to do something special for his country.
