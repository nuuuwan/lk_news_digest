G.C.E. O/L examination results released online

Colombo, June 19 (Daily Mirror) - The Department of Examinations has released the results of the General Certificate of Education (Ordinary Level) Examination online. Students can now access their results over the internet.

The 2025 G.C.E. Ordinary Level Examination was conducted earlier this year from February 17 to February 26. According to official records, a total of 451,463 candidates sat for the exam across the island.

Candidates can check their individual results by visiting the official website of the Ministry of Education's Department of Examinations at www.doenets.lk or results.exams.gov.lk.
