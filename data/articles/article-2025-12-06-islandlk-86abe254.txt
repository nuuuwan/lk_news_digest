HNB Assurance backs Kandy Kings for Colombo Golf League

HNB Assurance PLC has partnered with the Kandy Kings as the Official Protection Partner for the upcoming Colombo Golf League, scheduled from 5th to 7th December at the Royal Colombo Golf Club.

Through this partnership, HNB Assurance will provide insurance coverage to the 15-member Kandy Kings squad, ensuring their wellbeing throughout the tournament. As a brand rooted in trust and protection, HNB Assurance is proud to support a team that embodies passion and excellence.

Expressing his thoughts, Executive Director/CEO of HNB Assurance, Lasitha Wimalaratne, said:

“We are truly pleased to partner with the Kandy Kings for this year’s Colombo Golf League. We thank the franchise for choosing HNB Assurance as their protection partner and placing their confidence in us. As the team prepares to take on this exciting challenge, we are proud to safeguard their 15 players and wish them the very best on the greens.”

Sanesh Fernando, Chief Business Officer/GM – Partnerships at HNB Assurance, added:

“Supporting local sporting talent is a privilege for us. We extend our appreciation to the Kandy Kings franchise for this opportunity and look forward to backing the team throughout the league. We wish the players success, strength, and a memorable performance in this year’s tournament.”
