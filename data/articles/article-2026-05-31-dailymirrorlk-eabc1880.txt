Back-to-back Champions! RCB Dominate Gujarat to Retain IPL Title

(May.31 - Mirror Sports) - Royal Challengers Bangalore etched their name deeper into IPL history with a commanding win over Gujarat Titans in the 2026 final to secure a second consecutive title. After lifting their maiden trophy against Punjab Kings in 2025, RCB backed it up in style with another emphatic championship performance.

Virat Kohli hit an unbeaten 75 as Royal Challengers Bengaluru beat Gujarat Titans by five wickets to win their second straight Indian Premier League title on Sunday.

Bengaluru restricted Gujarat to 155-8, a total they chased down with 12 balls to spare after Kohli reached his fifth half-century of the tournament in the final in Ahmedabad.
