Peter Jasinghe elected NCC’s new President

The Annual General meeting of NCC was held on 26 March. At this AGM, Peter Jasinghe was elected as the new President and Neomal Perera the new General Secretary. Peter has previously held the post of Swimming Secretary, General Secretary and was the Vice President, before taking over the position of President.

The followings are the present office bearers elected at 137th AGM headed by Peter Jasinghe. Vice Presidents Lalith Ganlath, Marlon Fernandopulle and Nilesh De Silva. General Secretary Neomal Perera, Assistant General Secretary Riyad Riffai, Treasurer Nilantha Weerasinghe, Assistant Treasurer Upul Thennakoon and other members of the Executive Committee are Rajiv Meewakkala, Krishan Senaratna, Rohan Ratnayake, Anuruddha Hikkaduwage, Thilina K. Siriwardena, Rajkumar Kuvadas, Pinsiri Fernando, Farveez Maharoof, Ruwan Kalpage, Upul Tharanga, Julian Bolling, Praveen Madawala, and the immediate Past Presidents are, Suwan Perera, Marlon Ranasinghe and Lucky Fernandez.
