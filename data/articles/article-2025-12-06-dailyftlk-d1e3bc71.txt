Maldivian business magnate donates $200,000 to Sri Lanka

Sun Siyam Group of Maldives Chairman/Managing Director Dr. Ahmad Siyam Mohammed met with High Commissioner of Sri Lanka to Maldives Rizvi Hassen and extended a donation of $ 200,000. A token cheque of $ 75,000 was handed over to the High Commissioner recently. Sun Siyam Group is the largest operator of upmarket hotels in Maldives with over 5,000 rooms in 5 different resorts. It also operates Sun Siyam Passikudha in Sri Lanka.
