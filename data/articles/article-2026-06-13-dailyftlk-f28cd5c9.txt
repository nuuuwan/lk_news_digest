Assetline Finance – preferred partner at David Pieris Automobiles’ first GWM flagship experience centre

AFIN and the DPA teams with the first customers of the GWM flagship experience centre

Assetline Finance PLC (AFIN), a member of David Pieris Holdings, is pleased to be the preferred finance partner for the launch of Sri Lanka’s first GWM flagship experience centre at Access Towers, Colombo by David Pieris Automobiles (DPA) Ltd.

This state‑of‑the‑art facility marks a significant milestone in the country’s evolving automotive retail landscape. As the first and only vehicle experience centre of its kind, it redefines the traditional showroom concept by offering customers an immersive and interactive journey into modern mobility.

Visitors to the centre will be able to explore GWM’s premium range of SUVs, electric vehicles and Plug-in Hybrids (PHV) in a dynamic, experience‑led environment. Dedicated display and demonstration zones, interactive touchpoints, and test‑drive facilitation will allow customers to engage directly with GWM’s innovation, performance, and design excellence.

As the preferred finance partner, AFIN promises a seamless finance and ownership process for customers visiting the GWM centre. With super‑fast approvals, flexible financing plans, and nationwide support, AFL brings financial convenience directly into the experience centre, making vehicle ownership more accessible and efficient.

“As the Group’s preferred finance partner, AFIN is delighted to support this flagship launch. Our focus is to simplify the customer journey by offering flexible financing solutions, timely support, and a service experience that helps customers make confident decisions,” said AFIN Director/CEO Ashan Nissanka. This commitment highlights the strength of integrated service models within David Pieris Holdings. By combining product access with financial expertise, AFIN contributes to a more connected and efficient customer journey, one that reflects Sri Lanka’s growing demand for smarter, future‑ready mobility solutions.

As mobility needs continue to evolve, AFIN remains committed to delivering financing solutions that are flexible, efficient, and relevant, reinforcing its position as a trusted partner in Sri Lanka’s automotive sector.
