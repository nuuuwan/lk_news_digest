Songs of the ’70s … with Annesley

Songs of the ’70s are still very popular with Sri Lankans here and abroad and the organisers, in Toronto, Canada, are all geared up to give the best of Sinhala Pops of the ’70s to the audience in that part of the world.

‘Rhythm of the ’70s’, to be held on Saturday, 20th June, at The Arena, Pickering Casino, will feature Annesley Malawana, along with Indrani Perera, Corrine Almeida and Pawan Mallawarachchi.

Annesley says this is his third overseas tour for this year, after performing in Australia and London

“I now perform as a solo artiste which gives me the freedom to choose the events I like to be a part of.

Annesley also mentioned that another mega event is being planned for October this year, in Colombo, and details will be released soon.
