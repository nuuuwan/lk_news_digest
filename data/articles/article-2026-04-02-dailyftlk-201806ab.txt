Liberty Lands acquires full ownership of Scope Cinemas in strategic consolidation

Liberty Lands and Developments (LLD) has acquired the remaining shares of Scope Cinemas, securing full ownership of the cinema chain as part of its broader group restructuring and long-term strategic direction.

The acquisition marks a significant step in LLD’s efforts to consolidate its entertainment portfolio and strengthen its commitment to delivering world-class cinema experiences, while enhancing operational alignment. With Scope Cinemas now fully under its ownership, the group is positioned to drive greater cohesion across its businesses while reinforcing its core brand promise of creating memorable experiences that “put a smile on everyone’s face.”

As part of this transition, Thushan Rangana Meemanage will step down from his role as Chief Executive Officer and Director of Scope Cinemas, as well as from his roles across other affiliate companies, effective 31 March. His departure reflects a leadership transition aligned with the group’s evolving strategic priorities.

Naveed Cader has been appointed Interim CEO of Scope Cinemas with immediate effect, while continuing in his capacity as Executive Chairman. His appointment ensures continuity in leadership while supporting the company’s next phase of growth and transformation.

LLD operates a diversified portfolio spanning entertainment, hospitality, real estate, and food and beverage. Its key subsidiaries include Scope Cinemas and Food Studio, while the group also holds strategic stakes in Alhambra Hotels and Cine Digital.

Commenting on the development, Cader said: “This transition is part of a broader group restructuring initiative designed to strengthen alignment across our businesses and position Scope Cinemas for its next phase of growth. Our focus remains on building a resilient, future-ready organisation while continuing to deliver exceptional cinema experiences to our audiences.”

Scope Cinemas continues to play a leading role in shaping Sri Lanka’s cinema landscape, with a consistent focus on elevating audience experiences. The acquisition reflects LLD’s confidence in the long-term potential of the entertainment sector and reinforces its direction towards building a more integrated and forward-looking business.
