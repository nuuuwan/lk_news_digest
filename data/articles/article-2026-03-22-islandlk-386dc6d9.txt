Wycherley International School marks 40 years with coffee table book

Wycherley International School celebrated its 40th anniversary with the launch of a commemorative coffee table book, featuring four decades of educational excellence.Coordinating Principal Dayan Fernando described the project as a personal dream, intended to preserve the legacy of the school’s founders, educators, and alumni for future generations.

The book captures the institution’s journey from humble beginnings to a leading academy with about 4,500 students. Chairman Dr. Jagath Alwis and current Principal Dulka Mendis joined founders and staff at the launch, reaffirming the school’s commitment to holistic education and strong student-teacher relationships.
