Lakbima Rice Mills donates Rs. 100 m to ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund

Lakbima Rice Mills Ltd. has contributed Rs. 100 million to the Government’s ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund, established to provide relief to communities affected by Cyclone Ditwa. The donation cheque was handed over at the Presidential Secretariat to Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake by Araliya Group Chairman Dudley Sirisena.
