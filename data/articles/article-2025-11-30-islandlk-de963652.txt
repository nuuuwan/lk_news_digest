KT Clean Star unveils advanced Nano Coating Solutions in Sri Lanka

KT Clean Star, a member of the KT Group, has launched two advanced Japanese nanotechnology products in Sri Lanka: Nano Shine and Nano Maintenance.

Developed by Japan’s System Brain Corporation and established in over 40 countries, these solutions set new benchmarks for surface protection. Nano Shine provides vehicles with a long-lasting, high-gloss, and scratch-resistant shield that repels water and simplifies cleaning. Nano Maintenance offers durable protection for building exteriors and interiors, ensuring surfaces remain resilient against environmental wear.

Backed by extensive research and testing, these eco-conscious products bring world-class innovation to Sri Lanka’s automotive and property sectors, promising unmatched durability, shine, and ease of maintenance.
