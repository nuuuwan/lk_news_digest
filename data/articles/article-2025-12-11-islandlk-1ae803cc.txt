Sophos launches Authorized Training Center in Colombo in partnership with Sinetcom

New facility aims to strengthen cybersecurity skills and empower IT professionals across Sri Lanka

Sophos, a global leader in innovative security solutions for defeating cyberattacks, announced the launch of its new Authorized Training Center (ATC) in partnership with Sinetcom (Pvt) Ltd., a leading cybersecurity solutions provider in Sri Lanka. The new training facility, located in Colombo, marks Sophos’ continued investment in building local cybersecurity capacity and supporting its partners and customers with hands-on learning opportunities.

The Sophos ATC program is a global initiative that enables select partners, distributors, and accredited organizations to deliver official Sophos training and certification courses. The program helps IT professionals gain advanced skills, enhance partner capabilities, and ensure organizations can confidently deploy and manage Sophos’ cutting-edge cybersecurity technologies.

“With the launch of our ATC in Colombo, we are reinforcing our commitment to strengthening cybersecurity expertise in Sri Lanka and the wider South Asian region”.
