Samson Trading opens TVS showroom in Malabe

Samson Trading Company (Pvt) Ltd., a member of the DSI Samson Group, opened its new TVS showroom in Malabe on May 23, marking the launch of its official dealership partnership with TVS Lanka (Pvt) Ltd.

The new showroom signals a strategic expansion by Samson Trading Company (Pvt) Ltd. into the two-wheeler automotive sector, further strengthening its long-standing presence within the DSI Samson Group, known for reliability and trust in the local market.

The outlet, dedicated exclusively to TVS two-wheelers, offers a full retail range of motorcycles and scooters, including the TVS Ronin, Apache RTR series, Raider, Ntorq 125, Jupiter, Sport, and XL100 models.

The opening ceremony was attended by senior representatives of TVS Lanka (Pvt) Ltd., Samson Trading Company (Pvt) Ltd., and the DSI Samson Group, including Thusitha Rajapaksha, Managing Director of D. Samson and Sons (Pvt) Ltd.; Pradeep Samarathunga, General Manager of D. Samson and Sons (Pvt) Ltd.; Asanka Rajapaksha, Managing Director of Samson Trading Company (Pvt) Ltd.; Geethal Anthony, Chief Executive Officer of TVS Lanka (Pvt) Ltd.; and Malaka Vehalla, Assistant General Manager – Sales of TVS Lanka (Pvt) Ltd.

Speaking at the opening, Asanka Rajapaksha, Managing Director of Samson Trading Company (Pvt) Ltd., said the showroom marked an important milestone in expanding the company’s footprint in the two-wheeler segment. He noted that the 47-year-old company had built a strong foundation of trust and reliability, and that the partnership with TVS Lanka (Pvt) Ltd. would help deliver a modern retail experience backed by strong service standards.

TVS Lanka (Pvt) Ltd. Chief Executive Officer Geethal Anthony said the collaboration would further strengthen the brand’s presence in Sri Lanka by expanding accessibility and improving the ownership experience through a customer-centric dealership network.
