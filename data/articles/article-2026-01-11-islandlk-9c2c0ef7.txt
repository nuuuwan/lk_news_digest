NDB Bank supports national SME recovery initiative

NDB Bank has reinforced its commitment to Sri Lanka’s small and medium enterprises by participating in the ‘Revive Sri Lanka | Rising Stronger’ forum at Kandy City Centre. The event, attended by over 800 entrepreneurs and stakeholders, aimed to restore livelihoods and confidence following the recent DITWA cyclone.

The initiative highlighted crucial public-private collaboration for economic recovery, with the presence of Central Bank Governor Dr. Nandalal Weerasinghe and Central Province Chief Secretary G.H.M.A. Premasinghe. It united leading financial institutions in a show of sector-wide support.

Representing NDB, Sanjaya Perera, Senior Vice President – Personal Banking and Customer Experience, emphasized the bank’s dedication. “The resilience of our nation is built on the strength of its entrepreneurs. Supporting SMEs during disruption is a commitment we take seriously,” he stated.

NDB’s involvement aligns with its mission to foster inclusive growth. Through tailored financial solutions and advisory support, the bank remains dedicated to helping Sri Lankan businesses navigate challenges and achieve sustainable recovery, underscoring the vital role of SMEs in employment and regional development.
