Doola River Edge marks Christmas cake-mixing ceremony 2025

Doola River Edge, Udawalawa, ushered in the festive season with its annual Christmas Cake-Mixing Ceremony recently, a cherished tradition symbolizing unity, joy, and togetherness. The event brought together management, staff, and guests to celebrate the warmth of the season in true hospitality style. The cake-mixing ceremony was led by Directress Indika Senani, Director/Chief Executive Officer A. D. Amaradewa and senior management.

Located just minutes from the iconic Udawalawe National Park, Doola River Edge is a boutique escape surrounded by serene river views and lush greenery. The resort offers modern deluxe rooms and luxury suites designed for comfort and tranquility. Guests can enjoy refreshing dips in the riverside pool, embark on thrilling wildlife safaris, visit the Udawalawe Elephant Transit Home, or experience bird-watching, cycling trails, and river walks.
