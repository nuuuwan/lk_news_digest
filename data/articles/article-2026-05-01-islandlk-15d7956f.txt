CEAT Kelani launches GRIPP X5 and ZOOM X5 premium motorcycle tyres for Sri Lankan roads

CEAT Kelani Holdings has strengthened its leadership in Sri Lanka’s two-wheeler tyre market with the launch of the X5 series of its premium motorcycle and scooter tyre ranges – ‘GRIPP’ and ‘ZOOM’ – which are engineered to deliver higher mileage, enhanced safety and superior performance for modern riding needs.

The new tyres respond to the growing demand among youth and urban commuters for tyres that combine durability, control and confident handling with a bold, premium aesthetic. Designed specifically for Sri Lankan road and weather conditions, GRIPP X5 and ZOOM X5 elevate everyday commuting and leisure riding by offering a refined balance of stability, grip and braking efficiency on both dry and wet surfaces, the company said.

Commenting on the latest products, CEAT Kelani Holdings Chief Operating Officer, Shamal Gunawardene said: “These new tyres reflect our sharp focus on evolving rider expectations in Sri Lanka’s two-wheeler market. As riding patterns change and demand grows for higher performance, safety and premium styling, these tyres deliver world-class design and engineering solutions that are responsive to local road conditions and customer requirements.”

The ZOOM X5 tyre is developed for front-wheel fitment and is optimised to deliver stability and precise handling. Its continuous centre groove enhances straight-line control, while optimised angular grooves improve water dispersion, translating to better handling and traction on wet roads. Additional circumferential grooves at the shoulder, supported by knurling, enhance stability during leaning, giving riders greater confidence when manoeuvring in traffic or negotiating turns. A high-grip tread compound and sipes in the tread pattern further improve traction on dry roads, ensuring dependable performance across varied riding conditions.

The GRIPP X5, designed for rear-wheel applications, focuses on traction, control and durability under load.
