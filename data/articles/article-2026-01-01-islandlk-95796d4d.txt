Hameedia Holidays brings the spirit of the season to life

Hameedia, Sri Lanka’s leading menswear and lifestyle brand, ushers in the festive spirit this December with a vibrant celebration that brings together fashion, gifting, experiences, and meaningful community impact. This season, Hameedia invites customers to enjoy an elevated holiday journey across its outlets, with seasonal collections, festive activations, and delightful surprises crafted to bring joy throughout the month.

Shoppers can explore Hameedia’s Seasonal Collection, featuring festive edits from all six brands — Envoy, FH, Le Bond, Rosevelt, NUHA and Husn. From refined menswear and elegant womenswear to lifestyle essentials, the collection offers versatile styles for celebrations, corporate events and family gatherings.

Adding to the festive cheer, Seasonal Offers run throughout December, delivering exceptional value for holiday shoppers. Customers can also explore the newly launched Gifting Corner, featuring curated hampers, festive treats and premium wrapping services. Selected outlets will host Lucky Dip Activations, while high-value shoppers can look forward to exciting rewards through the Seasonal Raffle Draw, in partnership with Travelco Holidays and Galle Face Hotel.

The festivities continue beyond fashion with the much-anticipated Christmas Market at Envoy Mansion, showcasing artisanal vendors, gourmet treats and festive entertainment, including carols, live music, a Christmas photobooth and surprise gifts for children — creating joyful experiences for the entire family.
