Meats from Delmo now on Uber Eats

Uber Eats announced an exclusive partnership with Delmo, Sri Lanka’s leading poultry and meat producer, to bring fresh chicken and value-added meat products on-demand to consumers across the country.

Launching initially with 10 outlets, the partnership will expand nationwide in the coming months, making Delmo’s range of fresh and value-added chicken products including sausages and ready-to-cook items – available directly on the Uber Eats app.

Uber Eats’ partnership with Delmo, one of Sri Lanka’s most trusted poultry brands with over 140+ outlets nationwide, deepens the grocery and daily essentials selection available on the app, and reiterates its ‘Get Almost, Almost Anything’ promise.
