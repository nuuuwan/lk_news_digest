Senok delivers Foton Tunland G7 fleet to NEM Construction

Senok Trade Combine (Pvt) Ltd has strengthened its partnership with Sri Lanka’s construction and infrastructure sector by handing over a fleet of Foton Tunland G7 Double Cab vehicles to NEM Construction.

The fleet delivery marks another milestone in Senok’s automotive operations, reflecting growing corporate confidence in the reliability, durability and performance of the Foton Tunland G7 for commercial and industrial applications.

The vehicles were assembled locally at the Western Automobile Assembly (WAA) facility in Kurunegala, underscoring Senok’s commitment to supporting local manufacturing while providing Sri Lankan customers with internationally recognised automotive standards.

Senior representatives of both Senok and NEM Construction attended the official handover ceremony.

Company officials highlighted the importance of dependable mobility solutions for the construction industry, noting that the Foton Tunland G7 has emerged as a preferred choice among businesses seeking a combination of rugged performance, modern technology, safety features and value.

Designed to meet the demanding requirements of commercial operations, the Foton Tunland G7 offers powerful performance, enhanced safety, high load-carrying capacity and driving comfort, making it suitable for construction, engineering and infrastructure development projects.

Senok’s extensive after-sales service network was also highlighted as a key factor behind customer confidence in Foton vehicles. Backed by more than 45 years of experience in Sri Lanka’s automotive industry, Senok provides countrywide service support, genuine spare parts, trained technical personnel and comprehensive maintenance solutions to ensure maximum vehicle uptime.

The company’s investment in local vehicle assembly through WAA further demonstrates its contribution to Sri Lanka’s industrial development while delivering global-standard automotive solutions to customers.

Businesses interested in the Foton Tunland G7 Double Cab and other Foton commercial vehicles can visit the Foton showroom at No. 3, R.A. De Mel Mawatha, Colombo 05.
