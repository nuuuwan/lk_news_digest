Esshva recognised at NBQSA 2025

Esshva, a global technology company delivering enterprise software solutions to clients across diverse industries, won two major awards at the National Best Quality ICT Awards (NBQSA) 2025 for its outstanding and innovative technology solutions. This recognition highlights Esshva’s significant contribution to advancing ICT excellence and driving digital transformation in Sri Lanka, further reinforcing its position as a leader in digital innovation.

Leading Workplace, a workplace certification and assessment platform for employee engagement, captured the Gold Award in the Professional Services category. The solution redefines how exceptional companies are recognised by amplifying employee voices through dynamic and real-time workplace assessments. Leading Workplace equips organisations with actionable insights and tools to champion engagement, align people with impact, and differentiate themselves as employers of choice in competitive markets.

Chekku, a comprehensive business management platform, earned the Bronze Award in the Business ICT Solutions category. The solution streamlines operations for entrepreneurs and independent contractors by providing instant access to job opportunities, integrated compliance and governance tools, a centralised back-office library for operational efficiency, and skill training resources for continuous professional development.

“These awards validate our belief that exceptional technology transcends borders,” said Esshva Founder and Chief Executive Officer Sujeev Rajakulendran. “The team has worked tirelessly to build solutions that solve problems for businesses worldwide. This recognition inspires the team to continue raising the bar for what’s possible.”

The NBQSA represents Sri Lanka’s premier recognition program for ICT products, celebrating companies that compete on the global stage while advancing the nation’s digital economy.
