Sri Lanka’s Halal Council recognised for quality as certified exports hit $1.9 billion

The Halal Assessment Council (HAC) received a Merit Award at the prestigious Sri Lanka National Quality Awards 2023.

The award recognizes HAC’s commitment to upholding international certification standards, which help Sri Lankan producers access global markets and build consumer trust. This trusted certification is a major economic driver.

In 2024, Sri Lanka’s exports of Halal-certified products were estimated at USD 1.9 billion. This accounts for 63 percent of all food and beverage exports and 16 percent of the nation’s total exports, underscoring the critical role of certification in the country’s export economy.
