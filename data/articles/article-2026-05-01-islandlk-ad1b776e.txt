RDA joins “Vehicle Testing Can Save Lives” campaign

The “Vehicle Testing Can Save Lives” awareness project, conducted by the Automobile Association of Ceylon under the grant program of the Fédération Internationale de l’Automobile, was successfully completed with the distribution of 72,000 leaflets at highway entrances.

Devapriya Hettiarachchi, Secretary, AA Ceylon handed over the leaflets to T. Paskaran, Chairman of the Road Development Authority. Also present at the launch were Director General

H. H. N. A. Hettiarachchi, Deputy Director General (Expressway Operations) R. A. D. Kahatapitiya, and Deputy Director General K. Chandrakumara.
