LANKATILES opens 60th showroom in Colombo

LANKATILES has strengthened its retail footprint with the opening of its 60th showroom in Sri Lanka, marking a significant milestone in its more than five-decade journey in the building materials sector.

The new outlet, located at No. 85, Dr. N. M. Perera Mawatha in Colombo 08, was unveiled as a modern design destination offering premium wall and floor tile collections, including the company’s signature double-fired tile range.

The showroom was declared open at a ceremony attended by Chief Guest Chartered Architect Russell Dandeniya and LANKATILES Managing Director Priyantha Talwatte, along with representatives from the architecture, construction and business communities.

Positioned in the Borella area—one of Colombo’s most commercially active and well-connected urban corridors—the new showroom is aimed at serving homeowners, architects, designers and developers seeking contemporary surface solutions.

Company officials said the expansion reflects a strategic move to align with rapid urban development in the Western Province while improving access to premium design products and services.

Speaking at the event, Talwatte said the new showroom represents an important step in bringing high-quality tile solutions closer to customers in key urban centres, particularly as Borella evolves into a hub of modern residential and commercial development.

Chief Guest Dandeniya, in his remarks, praised the improved quality and customer engagement, noting that the new concept showroom is a valuable addition to Colombo’s growing design landscape.The company said the milestone reinforces its long-standing commitment to innovation, quality and design excellence in Sri Lanka’s construction and interiors sector.
