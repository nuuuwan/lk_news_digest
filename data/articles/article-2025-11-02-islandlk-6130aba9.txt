Charles Gamage joins LCB Finance Board

Lanka Credit and Business Finance PLC (LCB Finance) has appointed Charles Gamage to its Board of Directors, effective 15th October 2025. This strategic move brings a veteran with over 30 years of experience in the Co-operative sector and NGOs to the company’s leadership.

Gamage’s expertise is anticipated to be instrumental in achieving LCB Finance’s ambitious business expansion, specifically in increasing its business volume with co-operative establishments within its branch network areas. He is widely recognised for his transformative role in developing the Galle Co-operative Hospital from a small dispensary into a fully-fledged institution for medium and low-income groups, overseeing its expansion to include a wide range of specialist medical services and diagnostic facilities.

His distinguished career in finance includes being an initial Director of the Ruhunu Development Bank. He currently holds several key positions, including Director of the Galle District Co-operative Rural Bank and the Sri Lanka Federation of Co-operative Societies. The LCB Finance Board expects his deep-rooted connections and administrative skills to provide invaluable strategic direction for future growth.
