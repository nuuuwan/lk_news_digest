Seylan Bank continues SME sector regional engagement forums in North Central Region

Seylan Bank recently continued its support of the nation’s small and medium enterprises (SME) sector with its second SME Program in the North Central Region. Hosted at Hotel Mahanuge in Polonnaruwa, the initiative is the latest chapter in a series of developmental forums focused on improving financial literacy among small and medium business owners in the area and supporting them with practical knowledge to manage and scale their operations.

Organised and managed by Seylan Bank, the full day session was designed according to the specific needs of the region. The forum covered a seminar on financial and tax management, providing clear guidance on managing business finances, maintaining compliance, and planning for sustainable growth, addressing how entrepreneurs could elevate business growth under their specific circumstances.

Speaking on the initiative, Seylan Bank’s Assistant General Manager, Marketing and Sales, Asiri Abhayaratne stated, “Developing financially informed entrepreneurs is the key to strengthening the SME sector and supporting regional business development. As the Bank with a Heart, we will continue to deliver similar knowledge-based platforms for SMEs across the country.”
