HNB General Insurance continues its winning streak

HNB General Insurance (HNBGI) announces that Rumesh Wijethilaka, Assistant Manager – Business Development at HNBGI, has been recognized at recently concluded SLIM National Sales Awards 2025 organized by the Sri Lanka Institute of Marketing (SLIM) which stand as the highest national recognition for sales excellence, celebrating the best performers in the profession.

Rumesh’s achievement is a testament to his relentless dedication, customer-centric mindset and exceptional sales capability. In his capacity in Business Development, he has consistently exemplified service excellence, resilience and strong relationship-building skills and driving growth while reinforcing customer trust and loyalty.

Sharing his thoughts on this achievement, Sampath Wickramarachchi, Chief Business Officer / General Manager of HNB General Insurance stated “Rumesh’s accomplishment is a true reflection of the passion, discipline, and commitment that define our sales force. As a front-line professional serving our valued customers, he has exemplified how determination, skill, and a positive attitude can lead to outstanding success.

We take immense pride in witnessing our team members excel at a national level and look forward to seeing him continue to grow, inspire others and elevate our standards of excellence in the years ahead.”

This accolade reinforces HNBGI’s commitment to building and empowering a high-performing sales force through continuous development, innovation and a culture that rewards excellence. With passionate individuals taking the lead, HNBGI continues to elevate its industry presence as a trusted, customer-first insurer.
