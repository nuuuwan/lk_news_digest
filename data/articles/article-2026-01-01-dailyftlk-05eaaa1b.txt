SLIIT donates  Rs. 20 m to “Rebuilding Sri Lanka” Fund

The Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology Guarantee Limited (SLIIT) has made a financial donation of Rs. 20 million to the Government’s “Rebuilding Sri Lanka” Fund to provide relief to those affected by Cyclone Ditwah. The relevant cheque was handed over recently at the Presidential Secretariat by SLIIT Chairman/Chancellor Prof. Lakshman Ratnayake and Vice-Chancellor and Managing Director/CEO Prof. Lalith Gamage to Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake.
