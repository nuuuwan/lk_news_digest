Thayalan Bartlett named APAC future leader

Thayalan Bartlett, Executive Chairman of MullenLowe Group Sri Lanka, has been named to Campaign Asia-Pacific’s inaugural 50 Over Fifty 2026 list. He is the only Sri Lankan recognized among 37 senior leaders across 11 APAC markets, celebrated for actively shaping the future of marketing, media, and communications. With over three decades of experience, Bartlett has guided his agency through national crises including the pandemic and Sri Lanka’s debt crisis, while championing creativity, data, and technology. The honour challenges the industry’s focus on youth, highlighting Bartlett’s mentorship, entrepreneurial drive, and long-term institution-building. His inclusion places him alongside regional leaders from Edelman, Omnicom, and Microsoft Advertising, reinforcing the value of experienced leadership in a rapidly evolving communications landscape.
