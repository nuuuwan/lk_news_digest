Foxygen brings in Ozone’s architectural hardware solutions

Foxygen has announced the launch of Ozone’s architectural hardware solutions in Sri Lanka as the exclusive distributor and channel partner, at the Architects Exhibition held at BMICH. Ozone’s products including its existing range of glass hardware, along with glass railing systems, automatic doors, door hardware, furniture fittings, wardrobe fittings, locks, and digital safes will now be just one call away for the Sri Lankan building industry.

With over two decades of industry expertise, Ozone has earned the confidence of architects, builders, planners, and homeowners across 45+ countries, including the USA, Germany, Turkey, Italy, and the UAE. Supported by a portfolio of more than 6,000 products, the company positions itself not only as a solutions provider but also as a creative catalyst and collaborative partner, committed to high-value, customer-centric innovation.
