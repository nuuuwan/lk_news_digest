EU says Israeli attacks on Lebanon causing ‘mass displacement’

EU foreign policy chief Kaja Kallas has called on Israel to cease its attacks on Lebanon, reiterating diplomacy is the best chance to avoid further destabilisation.

“Hezbollah’s decision to attack Israel in support of Iran endangers the entire region and adds a deadly dimension. Israel has the right to self-defence in line with international law,” Kallas said in a statement.

“At the same time, Israel’s response has been heavy-handed. Its retaliation is causing mass displacement and is further destabilising a fragile situation. Lebanon’s sovereignty and territorial integrity must be respected.”
