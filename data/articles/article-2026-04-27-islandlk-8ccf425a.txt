Sri Lanka on the global digital map

Discover Ceylon on Roblox reaches 5 million impressions and 55,000 players in two weeks

Sea Pony Studios Pvt Ltd, an independent game development studio headquartered in Rajagiriya, is making its mark on the global digital landscape through Discover Ceylon — a Roblox-based interactive tourism experience that achieved 5 million impressions and 55,000 players within its first two weeks of a pilot launch.

Founded and led by Anthony Lambert, Sea Pony Studios consists of a core team of 20 Sri Lanka-based professionals, supported by 26 international freelancers. It specialises in Roblox experience development, culturally driven digital content, national branding, and proprietary platform technology. Lambert said that his venture was named in tribute to the family businesses — Sea Horse Enterprises and Sea Horse Marine

Having completed his HND in Software Engineering at the age of 15, Lambert holds several additional technical diplomas across relevant disciplines in addition to studying Technical Computer Science in the Netherlands. Lambert said that he returned to the country a few years ago with aview to explore ways and means of placing Sri Lanka on the global digital stage through interactive media. “I began building games at the age of 17 and now leads a studio of 46 professionals and international collaborators,” Lambert said.
