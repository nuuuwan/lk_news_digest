Rajeev begins second Presidential term at BASL; De Silva new Secy.

Rajeev Amarasuriya yesterday assumed duties for his second term as President of the Bar Association of Sri Lanka (BASL) for 2026/2027, and will be ceremonially inducted at the 52nd Convocation of the BASL scheduled for 10 May.

Former BASL Treasurer Nalin Chandika De Silva also assumed duties as the new Secretary of the BASL for 2026/2027, taking over from outgoing Secretary Chathura Galhena.
