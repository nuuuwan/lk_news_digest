Lion Brewery recognised as Sri Lanka’s Top Beverage Exporter

Lion Brewery (Ceylon) PLC has been awarded ‘Best Exporter – Beverages’ at the 27th Presidential Export Awards. The national accolade recognises its exceptional export performance and role in promoting Sri Lankan brands globally.

CEO Rajiv Meewakkala stated the award endorses the company’s commitment to “taking Sri Lankan excellence to the world.” The brewery’s exports grew by 64% year-on-year, with its products now available in over 19 countries across five continents. Key growth markets include Africa, the UAE, Australia, and Italy.

The company’s international strategy combines local market adaptation with consistent quality. Its flagship beers, Lion Stout and Lion Lager, have also recently received global taste awards, reinforcing the brand’s worldwide reputation.
