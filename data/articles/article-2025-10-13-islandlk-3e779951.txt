JF Packaging announces IPO, investor forum

An acclaimed Sri Lanka’s leading flexible packaging solutions provider, and a multi-award-winning innovator in the industry, JF Packaging Limited intends to officially announce its Initial Public Offering of Ordinary Voting Shares.

The Investor Forum has been scheduled for the 21st October 2025, to provide potential investors with an in-depth understanding of the company’s operations, business model, and future growth plans. Investors from across the globe are invited to join the forum via an online video stream, offering a unique opportunity to engage directly with the company’s leadership. The company encourages interested parties to join the forum and gain valuable insights into this compelling investment opportunity.
