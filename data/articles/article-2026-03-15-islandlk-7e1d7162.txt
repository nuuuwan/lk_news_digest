Fcode Labs launches Forevate to speed up business decisions

Fcode Labs has launched Forevate, a real-time market intelligence platform designed to help businesses respond faster to market changes.

The new solution combines internal company data with external intelligence to track competitor activity, pricing movements, and social trends as they happen. It targets industries where rapid change often outpaces traditional planning, including manufacturing, retail, supply chain, and FMCG.

Co-CEO Buddhishan Manamperi said businesses can no longer rely on outdated planning cycles. “Changes in competitor activity, pricing, and social trends can happen overnight,” he said.

Unlike static analytics tools, Forevate uses a two-phase model: a Design Pod to map where intelligence can have the greatest impact, followed by a Delivery Pod to test and refine insights continuously.
