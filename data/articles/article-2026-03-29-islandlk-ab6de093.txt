KOKO wins Overall Gold at LankaPay Technnovation Awards 2026

KOKO has secured the Overall Gold Award for Excellence in Digital Payments at the LankaPay Technnovation Awards 2026 – a remarkable feat in its first year of participation.

The company also bagged three category Gold Awards: Best Enabler for LankaSign Digital Signatures, Most Popular Digital Payment Solution, and Best Digital Payment App for Retail Payments. It was additionally shortlisted for Best Institution for Financial Inclusivity.

During the year under review, KOKO enabled approximately LKR 4 billion in transactions, recorded 100,000 registered JustPay users, and supported 1,200 merchants – achieving an 846% year-on-year growth in users and merchants.

LankaPay CEO Channa de Silva said KOKO’s achievement reflects progress across Sri Lanka’s digital payments ecosystem, strengthening adoption and customer experience.

Today, KOKO serves roughly 2 million users across approximately 6,500 merchants, positioning itself not only as the BNPL category leader but also as a major force in the country’s broader digital payment landscape.

The awards ceremony was attended by key leaders including Deputy Minister of Digital Economy Eng. Eranga Weeraratne, Central Bank Governor Dr. P. Nandalal Weerasinghe, and Dr. Hans Wijayasuriya, Chief Advisor to the President on Digital Economy.
