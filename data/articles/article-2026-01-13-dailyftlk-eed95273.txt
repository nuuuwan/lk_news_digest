“WAVES OF HOPE” aid concert for flood victims on 8 February

“WAVES OF HOPE”, a charity concert organised by Rotary District 3220 and the Western Musicians Association (WMA), will be held on 8 February 2026, at 7:00 p.m. at the Radisson Blu (Hotel Galadari) Ballroom. The event is dedicated to supporting communities and families affected by Cyclone Ditwah, bringing people together through music and compassion.

The official launch of Waves of Hope was held at Jetwing Seven (physical and Zoom), announcing the upcoming charity concert organised to raise funds and awareness for communities and families affected by Cyclone Ditwah. Half of the 50 available tables were already reserved on the day of the launch, reflecting an enthusiastic response.

The best of Sri Lanka’s western musicians are joining hands for this cause. The legendary band Misty will lead the line-up, joined by celebrated artists including Chitral, Dalreen, Rukshan, Dina, Sohan, with Clifford and Lulu serving as presenters. All performers have generously agreed to appear free of charge, offering the audience a rich variety of music ranging from timeless classics to contemporary favourites. Guests can also look forward to many surprises and special guest appearances, ensuring an unforgettable evening of entertainment and solidarity.

Tickets are priced at Rs. 10,000, inclusive of set menu dinner options, with a BYOB (Bring Your Own Beverage) policy. All proceeds will directly support relief and recovery initiatives for families impacted by Cyclone Ditwah.

Rotary District 3220 and WMA warmly invite corporates, organisations, and community groups to come forward as partners in this initiative. By joining hands, partners can demonstrate their commitment to social responsibility, strengthen community ties, and make a lasting impact on the lives of those affected by Cyclone Ditwah. Partnership opportunities include sponsorships, LED Screen Advertising, contributions and table bookings toward relief efforts.
