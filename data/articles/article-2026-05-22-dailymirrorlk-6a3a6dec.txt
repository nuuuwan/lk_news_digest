Vesak pandals in the making...

Vesak week pandals are being constructed across Colombo and its suburbs in preparation for the upcoming Vesak festival. The illuminated displays will depict significant events from the life of the Buddha and his previous births, with major pandals located in Thotalanga, Borella, Dematagoda, Peliyagoda, Kosgas Junction and Pettah.
