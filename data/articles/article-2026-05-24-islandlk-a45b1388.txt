Tech startup Corzent named ‘Best ICT Startup’ at South Asian Business Excellence Awards

Corzent, a technology firm based in Sri Lanka and Dubai, was crowned the “Best ICT Startup Organization” at the 9th South Asian Business Excellence Awards held in Colombo. Competing against entities across India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Nepal, and Sri Lanka, the regional accolade highlights Corzent’s strong technical foundation, disciplined execution, and world-class engineering team.

Founded in 2021, the boutique firm specializes in providing stable, dedicated engineering teams for global startups and scaleups, heavily anchoring its growth in frameworks like Angular and .NET. Their continuous team stability and retention of institutional knowledge have allowed the firm to counter the local tech “brain drain” and build a highly dependable delivery track record for international clients, particularly across Australia and Scandinavia in fields like Fintech, Greentech, Proptech, and construction technology.
