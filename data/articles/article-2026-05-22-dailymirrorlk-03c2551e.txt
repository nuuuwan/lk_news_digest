The Daily Leaf officially launches in Colombo

Colombo’s vibrant culinary and lifestyle landscape welcomed a new addition with the official launch of The Daily Leaf.

Located in the heart of the city at 14/11 Park Street, Colombo 2, The Daily Leaf is consciously charting a path back to local roots. The brand’s philosophy rests on the conviction that for many, tea is far more than a routine beverage; it serves as an emotional anchor, evoking early childhood memories, the gentle clatter of family kitchens, and the comforting aroma of tea lovingly prepared by one’s parents, relatives, or neighbors.

Spearheading this vision are siblings Managing Director Fahmy Rahman and Director Sumaiya Rahman, who introduced the concept to Colombo’s discerning audience during a private, warmly attended launch event.

“For many of us, tea is more than just a beverage; it is a memory,” said Fahmy Rahman, Managing Director of The Daily Leaf. “It takes us back to those early mornings of our childhood, the gentle clatter of utensils from the kitchen, the comforting aroma filling the air, and the first sip of tea lovingly prepared by our families.

That feeling of warmth, familiarity, and care is exactly what we aim to recreate with every cup we serve at The Daily Leaf. Today, as we officially launch, we invite all of you to join us on a journey rooted in nostalgia, driven by passion, and dedicated to delivering comfort in its simplest, most authentic form.”

The Daily Leaf honours this timeless cultural connection through an expertly curated beverage and food menu designed to balance heritage with contemporary presentation. At the heart of the beverage portfolio is a delightful range of milk teas. Guests can indulge in classic favorites or explore meticulously infused aromatic blends such as Cardamom, Ginger, and the establishment’s highly-anticipated Signature Milk Tea.

To cater to the island’s warmer afternoons, The Daily Leaf also introduces a refreshing suite of premium iced teas. These creations feature unique, locally resonant flavor profiles including Lime, Rose, Coconut, and Honey, each crafted to provide crisp rejuvenation without losing the authentic integrity of the underlying tea leaf.

Recognizing that an extraordinary cup of tea deserves an equally comforting culinary accompaniment, the menu incorporates a selection of hearty, flavor-dense dishes that challenge conventional café fare. By pairing iconic staples such as rich, artisanal crab curry with traditional pol rotti, and perfectly textured dosa with deeply spiced chicken curry, The Daily Leaf seamlessly brings the taste of home-cooked meals into a sophisticated urban setting.
