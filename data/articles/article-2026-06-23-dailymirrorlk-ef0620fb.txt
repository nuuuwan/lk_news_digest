People’s Bank opens Kotawila service centre

People’s Bank recently opened its newest service centre in Kotawila.

The service centre offers the latest digital banking services, including an ATM that enables the customers to withdraw cash 24 hours a day, seven days a week and 365 days a year.

The opening ceremony was attended by People’s Bank Deputy General Manager Channel Management Naleen Pathiranage, Matara Regional Manager A.L.C.P. Abeysinghe, Assistant Regional Managers W.S.S. Rupasiri, P.W. Gunasekara and Renuka Amarasinghe, Senior Law Officer Ajantha Kapugamage, Matara Dharmapala Mawatha Branch Manager E.B.C.M. Bandara and Kotawila Service Centre Manager H.A.N.S. Perera, together with the government officials, bank staff and customers.
