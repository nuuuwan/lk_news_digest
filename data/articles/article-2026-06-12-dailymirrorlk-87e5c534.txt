Bollywood stars shine at BIA

Renowned Bollywood personalities Shilpa Shetty Kundra, Aditya Roy Kapur, and Jacqueline Fernandez arrived at the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) today, adding a touch of glamour to the BIA Silk Route experience.

The celebrities were welcomed at the airport’s exclusive fast-track service, BIA Silk Route, which is designed to provide passengers with enhanced comfort, convenience, and privacy. The service offers seamless assistance from arrival to departure, including priority processing and personalized support.

BIA Silk Route continues to position itself as a premium airport experience, catering to high-profile travelers seeking efficiency and exclusivity.
