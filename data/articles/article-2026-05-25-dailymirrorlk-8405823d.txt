Vesak Maha Perahera...

In line with the 2570 Vesak festival, a Vesak Maha Perahera was held last evening (24) at Narangodapaluwa, Batuwatta. The Perahera was held under the patronage of Ven. Sivuralumulle Sumanarathne Thera, the chief incumbent of the Galahitiyawa Sri Sugatha Subadrarama temple and Narangodapaluwa Rathna Sri Gnanarama Pothgul Temple. Pix by Nimalsiri Edirisinghe
