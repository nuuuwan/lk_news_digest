Doing it with Cabbage …

Mix finely grated cabbage with a teaspoon of honey and apply the mixture to your face. Leave it on for 15-20 minutes and then rinse off with lukewarm water.

Crush some fresh cabbage leaves to obtain the juice and then place the crushed leaves on your face, focusing on areas with fine lines. Leave the compress on for 15-20 minutes and then rinse with water.

Mix a grated cabbage with plain yoghurt to form a paste and apply the paste to your face and neck. Allow it to sit for 15-20 minutes and then rinse off with lukewarm water.

Mix 04 tablespoons of minced cabbage with 01 egg white, 01 teaspoon raw honey, 02 teaspoons gram flour, and a few drops of lemon. Mix everything well until a smooth paste is formed. Apply this mask all over your face and then wash it off with lukewarm water after 20 minutes.

Mix a minced quarter head of small cabbage with 01 egg white and 01 teaspoon of fresh lemon juice. Apply it all over the face and then wash it off after 20 minutes.

Mince 03 tablespoons of cabbage and mix it with 01 egg yolk, 01 tablespoon of olive oil and blend everything until a smooth mixture is formed. Apply this over your face, leave it for 20 minutes, and then wash it off to enjoy a well-hydrated skin.
