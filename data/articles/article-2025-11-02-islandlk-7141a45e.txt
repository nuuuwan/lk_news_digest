WPO hosts 114th Board Meeting in Colombo

The World Packaging Organisation (WPO) hosted its 114th Board Meeting and a week-long series of events in Colombo from October 27 to 31, 2025. Hosted by the local member, the Sri Lanka Institute of Packaging (SLIP), the programme convened 34 WPO delegates from five continents.

The series focused on the future of packaging, innovation, and sustainability. Key events include the Global Packaging Congress and the prestigious Sri Lanka Packaging Awards Ceremony.

WPO President Luciana Pellegrino expressed excitement to return to Sri Lanka, noting the potential and evolution of the local industry. SLIP President Thusith Wijesinghe highlighted the event’s significance, coinciding with SLIP’s 50th anniversary. He added that the convergence of global professionals to explore best practices in sustainability, regulation, and waste management was vital in driving the Sri Lankan packaging industry forward. The event also preceded WPO’s discussions on reducing food loss and waste at COP30.
