Dr. Ilma Osman Sedar honoured with prestigious mental health awards

Dr. S. F. Ilma Osman Sedar has been distinguished with a series of national and international awards in 2025, recognizing her exceptional contributions to mental health care in Sri Lanka.

Her accolades include the Excellence in Psychology & Counseling – Legendary Woman of the Year at the Women Legends Awards, the Outstanding Psychological Advisor Award at the Iconic Awards, and the Excellence in Clinical Psychology award at the Lakwanitha Abhimani Awards. These honors reflect her transformative work in psychological well-being, mental health education, and community rehabilitation. This follows her 2024 Honorary Doctorate in Psychology from Washington Digital University, USA.

An accomplished researcher and founder of the Hope Wellness Center in Wattala, Dr. Sedar is a member of the Global Institute of Counseling Professionals (GIOCP) and a graduate member of the Lanka Association of Psychologists & Counselling Professionals (LAPCP). Her awards collectively celebrate her patient-centred care, influential advisory role, and dedication to empowering women in healthcare leadership.
