Another Rs. 3 m donation to ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ fund

A financial contribution of Rs. 3 million has been made to the ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ fund by Herath Enterprises Ltd. and Intermax Ltd.

Herath Enterprises donated Rs. 2 million and the relevant cheque was recently handed over to the Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake, by Sameera Sanjeewa Rajitha Bandara Herath, H.K. Sachini Maduka, Gayanjali Sandatharaka Herath, and A.A.M.D. Chandra Rohini.

Meanwhile, Intermax Founder and Managing Director Shamshard Marikkar, presented a cheque for Rs. 1 million to the Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake. Ummu Regina and Shehani Dilhani of Intermax Ltd. were also present on the occasion.
