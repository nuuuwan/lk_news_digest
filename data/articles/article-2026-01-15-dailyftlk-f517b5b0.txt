Arava wins Presidential Export Award for excellence in coconut substrate products

Arava Ltd., a leading Sri Lankan exporter of coconut-based products, has been honoured with the Presidential Export Award 2024/25 for Best Exporter – Coconut Substrate Products, one of the highest national recognitions for export excellence in Sri Lanka.

The award was presented under the Presidential Export Awards program organised by the Sri Lanka Export Development Board (EDB), in recognition of Arava’s outstanding contribution to the national export sector. This prestigious accolade reflects Arava’s strong global presence, consistent export performance, and commitment to delivering high-quality, value-added coconut-based products to international markets. Founded in 2013, Arava has grown rapidly into a globally competitive enterprise with three state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities across Sri Lanka, employing over 800 staff and exporting thousands of containers annually to more than 46 countries across all seven continents.

Arava specialises in a diverse portfolio of premium coconut substrate products, including coir fibre bales, cocopeat, grow bags, cut fibre, and husk chips, serving international agricultural and horticultural markets. The company is widely recognised for its focus on sustainability, innovation, and quality assurance. Its manufacturing processes comply with stringent international standards and are certified under ISO 9001:2015, OMRI, and GMP, ensuring products that meet the highest global benchmarks. Further strengthening its reputation for leadership and innovation, Arava was also recognised at the Entrepreneur of the Year Awards 2025, organised by the Federation of Chambers of Commerce and Industry of Sri Lanka (FCCISL). At the national-level awards ceremony Arava Chairman and Founder Asiri Gurusinghe received the Most Innovative Entrepreneur of the Year 2025 – Gold Award in the Extra-Large Category, in recognition of his visionary leadership and entrepreneurial excellence.

Gurusinghe stated that the awards reflect the dedication of the Arava team and the trust placed in the company by global customers. He noted that Arava’s journey from a coir-based exporter to a diversified, value-added global supplier demonstrates Sri Lanka’s potential to compete successfully in international markets.

Arava said it continues to play a vital role in positioning Sri Lanka as a trusted global supplier of sustainable, eco-friendly coconut-based products, while setting new benchmarks in export performance and innovation.
