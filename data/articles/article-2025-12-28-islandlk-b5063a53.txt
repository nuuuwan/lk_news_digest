People’s Bank Holds 47th Annual Christmas Carols

The Christian Guild of People’s Bank recently held its 47th Annual Christmas Carols at the People’s Tower, Colombo 02.

Staff representing branches located in Colombo, Puttalam, Kalutara, and Jaffna regions sang the carols.

The Christmas Carols were held under the patronage of Rt. Rev. Dr J. D. Anthony, Auxiliary Bishop of Colombo.

People’s Bank Chairman Prof. Narada Fernando, Chief Executive Officer/General Manager Clive Fonseka, as well as members of the Corporate and Executive Management, bank staff and their families, and retired employees of People’s Bank, graced the occasion.

At the end of the Christmas Carols, gifts were distributed among children by Santa Claus.
