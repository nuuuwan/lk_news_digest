MTI case features Sri Lankan street food franchise in Serbia, Croatia

The case study presentation to MTI’s Global Strategy Interns in the Balkan countries was delivered by Curry Souls Founder Clement Senaratne—the pioneering Sri Lankan street food franchise in Serbia and Croatia. His outlets are celebrated for their vibrant, funky, and youthful atmosphere, blending authentic Sri Lankan flavours with a modern, urban setting that resonates with the dynamic spirit of the Balkans. Curry Souls has consistently ranked among the top restaurants in Serbia on Trip Advisor.

MTI Consulting, the internationally networked boutique strategy consultancy, recently announced the launch of its first cohort under the Global Strategy Internship Program. This inaugural cohort focuses on the Balkans region, covering Albania, Kosovo, Macedonia, Montenegro, and Slovenia, and forms a key component of MTI’s GROW initiative aimed at rapid global network expansion.

The month-long intensive program is designed to immerse participants in the fundamentals of strategy consulting. Interns gain hands-on experience through practical consulting tasks, direct interactions with MTI’s Senior International Consultants and Subject Matter Experts, and the opportunity to conduct nationally strategic studies relevant to their respective countries. The program culminates in a final graduation presentation showcasing their work.

With nearly three decades of experience and assignments across 51 countries, MTI Consulting continues to expand its global footprint through initiatives like this, fostering the next generation of strategy professionals.
