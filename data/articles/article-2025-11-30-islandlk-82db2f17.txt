SriLankan Airlines issues urgent advisory for flight crew

In light of the current situation in the country and its impact on standard communication networks, SriLankan Airlines has released a special advisory for its pilots and cabin crew.

The airline has identified difficulties in contacting certain crew members through regular channels. To ensure the continued smooth operation of its flight schedule, the management urgently requests all pilots and cabin crew members to make immediate contact.

Crew members are instructed to get in touch with the Airline’s Crew Control Centre (CCC) without delay for essential updates and further instructions.

The airline emphasizes that a prompt response from all crew is crucial for maintaining operational readiness and the continuity of its vital services.
