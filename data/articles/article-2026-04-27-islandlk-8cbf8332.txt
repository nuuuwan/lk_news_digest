More dates for Rajiv … in the UK

Rajiv Sebastian, veteran of Sri Lankan music and one of the island’s most versatile entertainers, is back in the UK, as was reported in  The Island, last Tuesday (21), keeping his fans, in that part of the world, extremely happy and, literally, shouting for more!

Rajiv was originally scheduled to do four events but with his UK fans wanting more, two more dates have been added to the singer’s itinerary.

Saturday, 9th May, Rajiv will move into action at the Sri Lankan Cultural Day 2026, to be held at Southampton.

They say it’s a full day event of family fun, with music, food and culture, including Avurudu games, etc., and I’m sure Rajiv would love such a scene, and, of course, he will be there in a big way, with his Avurudu outfit of the sarama (sarong)!

On his return to Sri Lanka, Rajiv mentioned that he and his band The Clan have two overseas assignments lined up.
