Opposition claims CMC control after Budget defeat

Mayor urges councillors to examine conscience, pledges to continue work despite setback

Opposition MP Mujibur Rahman said the Joint Opposition would move to take control of the Colombo Municipal Council following the defeat of its Budget.

Speaking to the media, Rahman said the mayor would be reduced to a figurehead once the Joint Opposition assumes control of the council. He also claimed the Opposition could have taken over the CMC earlier had the Mayoral election been conducted through an open vote rather than a secret ballot.

Despite the setback, the Mayor said she remained committed to working with CMC members and staff going forward.
