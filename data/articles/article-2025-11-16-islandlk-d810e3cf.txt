D & R Exports chairperson recognised as ‘Legendary Woman of the Year’

Manel Rajkumar, Chairperson of D & R Exports (Pvt) Ltd, has been awarded the ‘Legendary Woman of the Year’ (Large-Scale Category) at the Women Legend Awards 2025.

The company, a established player in Sri Lanka’s food and beverage sector, began its operations in 1993. It expanded into international markets in 2002 and now exports a diverse range of products including canned fish, spices, ready-to-eat meals, and tea to destinations across Asia, Europe, and the Middle East.

All products are manufactured at the company’s own facilities, adhering to stringent local and international quality standards. This award recognises a business built over three decades on a foundation of quality and safety.

“This award is a testament to our team’s dedication and our customers’ trust,” Rajkumar stated after the ceremony.
