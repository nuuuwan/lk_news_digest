Walkers Tours partners IRONMAN 70.3 Colombo

Walkers Tours, Sri Lanka’s leading destination management company with over 57 years of expertise, is proud to announce its role as the Official Travel Partner for IRONMAN 70.3 Colombo, scheduled to take place on 22 February 2026.

This prestigious international endurance triathlon marks the event’s highly anticipated return to Sri Lanka after a break since 2019. The race follows a fast, scenic course featuring a sunrise ocean swim, a flat coastal bike ride, and a run through the heart of Colombo, with Port City Colombo as the host venue. Athletes will also compete for qualification slots to the 2026 IRONMAN 70.3 World Championship.

As the Official Travel Partner, Walkers Tours will deliver seamless, athlete-friendly services, including airport transfers, conveniently located accommodations, and curated pre- and post-race experiences that showcase Sri Lanka’s natural beauty and renowned hospitality. This partnership allows the athletes and their supporters to enjoy a hassle-free race experience, enabling them to focus fully on performance while embracing an exceptional travel journey.

The event represents a key milestone in strengthening Sri Lanka’s presence on the global sports tourism stage.
