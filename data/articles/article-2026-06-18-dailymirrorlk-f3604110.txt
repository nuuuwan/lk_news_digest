Ghost’s children have entered UNP working committee: Ravi K

Colombo, June 18 (Daily Mirror) - Ghost’s children have entered the United National Party (UNP) working committee while seniors have been kept out, MP Ravi Karunanayake said today.

"Both the UNP and Samagi Jana Balawegaya (SJB) should get together so that leaders of both parties and their members would survive politically," he also said.

MP Karunanayake however expressed confidence that both parties will get together under the current leaders and will go from strength to strength.
