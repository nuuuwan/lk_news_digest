People’s Bank launches 125th Cheque Deposit Kiosk

People’s Bank launched its 125th Cheque Deposit Kiosk (CDK) at the Self Banking Unit (SBU) of its Mid City Branch on 22 June, under the patronage of Clive Fonseka, Chief Executive Officer/General Manager of People’s Bank, marking the highest number of Cheque Deposit Kiosks deployed by any commercial bank in Sri Lanka to date.

The Cheque Deposit Kiosk is designed to provide customers with a seamless and user-friendly experience by enabling cheque deposits without the need of filling deposit slips.

The launch of the 125th Cheque Deposit Kiosk further strengthens People’s Bank’s position as a leader in digital banking, reinforcing its commitment to delivering innovative banking solutions that enhance customer convenience while supporting the country’s digital transformation.
