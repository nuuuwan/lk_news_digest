SELVE: AI-powered speaking practice

SELVE, an innovative AI-driven app, delivers real-time support to help you master real-world conversations. Developed by the Selvern Trust, the app tackles the language barriers that stall careers – and its interactive, learner-first design makes all the difference.

Personalised start: An initial assessment maps your current level, then guides your progress.

Native language support: Switch to Sinhala or Tamil when stuck – SELVE responds in English to keep you immersed.

Engaging format: Vibrant videos, animations, interactive characters, and real-life scenarios make learning stick.

Gamified motivation: Earn points, climb leaderboards, and get constructive feedback when you stumble.

With a free trial available in March 2026, SELVE is already trusted by over 200 users who credit its interactive approach for their growing confidence.
