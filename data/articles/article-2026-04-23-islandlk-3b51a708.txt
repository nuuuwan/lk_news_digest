Dialog Enterprise partners Star Garments: Pioneering 5G innovation in Sri Lanka’s apparel industry

Dialog Enterprise, the corporate solutions arm of Dialog Axiata PLC and Sri Lanka’s number one ICT solutions provider, has partnered with Star Garments Pvt Ltd to enable next-generation textile manufacturing through advanced 5G-powered solutions, enhancing operational efficiency, automation and real-time decision-making across its production ecosystem.

Through this collaboration, Dialog Enterprise is equipping Star Garments with cutting-edge 5G connectivity to support the transition toward a fully digital, intelligent, and responsive manufacturing environment. The deployment focuses on integrating Industrial IoT (IIoT), real-time analytics, and smart factory capabilities to modernise existing systems and drive greater production agility.
