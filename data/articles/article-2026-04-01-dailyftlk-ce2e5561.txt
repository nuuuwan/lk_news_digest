OPPO launches Reno 15 Series

OPPO Sri Lanka recently launched its latest smartphone lineup, the Reno 15 Series, at the Abans showroom in Havelock City Mall  marking the official entry of the device range into the local market while announcing plans to further strengthen its investment in Sri Lanka.

The launch event brought together leading influencers, technology key opinion leaders (KOLs), media representatives and industry stakeholders, reflecting OPPO’s growing presence and strategic focus on the Sri Lankan market. The company stated that the initiative is aimed at delivering enhanced consumer experiences, improved product accessibility and long-term brand value.

Company representatives also expressed appreciation for the continued trust and recognition shown by Sri Lankan consumers, noting that OPPO remains committed to continuous improvement and innovation to meet evolving customer needs.

The Reno 15 Series is positioned to cater to modern digital lifestyles, particularly targeting creators, gamers and digital-first users. The event featured strong influencer participation, with real-time content creation and social media engagement amplifying the launch across multiple platforms.

Attendees were given hands-on access to the devices through interactive experience zones, enabling them to explore key features including camera capabilities, gaming performance and overall design. The approach reflects OPPO’s emphasis on consumer-centric engagement and experiential marketing.

In terms of design, the Reno 15 Series introduces a premium aesthetic inspired by the “Dancing Aurora” concept. The device features a light-reflective finish with dynamic colour transitions, offering a sleek and modern appearance while maintaining a comfortable in-hand feel.

A key highlight of the device is its advanced camera system. Equipped with a multi-camera setup including ultra-wide and telephoto capabilities, the Reno 15 Series supports up to 120x zoom, allowing users to capture distant subjects with enhanced clarity. AI-powered features such as AI Flash Photography and AI Portrait Glow further enhance image quality, while built-in editing tools provide additional creative flexibility.

Performance is powered by the Snapdragon 7 Gen 4 Mobile Platform, delivering fast and efficient operation with seamless multitasking. The device also supports up to 120FPS gaming, offering users an immersive and responsive gaming experience with improved visual performance.

The Reno 15 Series is also designed with durability and security in mind. Featuring IP69 water and dust resistance, the device is built to perform reliably across different environments, including support for underwater photography. In addition, advanced security features such as theft protection systems, cloud security and chip-level encryption help ensure user data protection.
