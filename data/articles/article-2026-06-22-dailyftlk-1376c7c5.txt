Veepee Graphics Lanka participates at  Sri Lanka Print and Trade Fair Exhibition 2026

Veepee Graphics Lanka Director Nalani Madhushani, Industries Ministry Secretary Thilaka Jayasundara and Sri Lanka United National Business Alliance Chairman Tania Abeysundara lighting the traditional oil lamp to inaugurate the Veepee stall

Veepee Graphics Lanka, a premier prepress and premedia organisation, showcased itself at the Sri Lanka Print and Trade Fair Exhibition 2026 at the BMICH.

Its stall was ceremoniously inaugurated with a ribbon-cutting ceremony and the lighting of the traditional oil lamp by Veepee Graphics Lanka Director Nalani Madhushani, Industries Ministry Secretary Thilaka Jayasundara, and Sri Lanka United National Business Alliance Chairman Tania Abeysundara. Sri Lanka Association of Printers President Eng. Janaka Rathnakumara also participated in the momentous occasion. The exhibition, recognised as Sri Lanka’s premier and largest printing, packaging, labelling, signage, and graphic communications event, concluded on Sunday serving as a prime platform for the company to showcase its cutting-edge innovations in the flexible packaging sector.

​Operating as a proud extension of Bangalore-based Veepee Graphic Solutions, Veepee Graphics Lanka has established itself as a trusted name synonymous with quality, precision, and technological excellence. The strategic initiative to expand into Sri Lanka was positioned by astute entrepreneur P. Jaichandra. Today, the organisation is further strengthened by its future-ready leadership, with Natasha Jaichandra representing the next generation. As one of the youngest directors in the flexible packaging industry, she brings youthful energy, technical acumen, and a progressive vision that seamlessly blends tradition with modern innovation.

​The organisation is equipped with state-of-the-art machinery, advanced artificial intelligence-driven workflows, and world-renowned automation engines that collectively ensure unmatched accuracy and efficiency. Veepee Graphics Lanka remains at the forefront of colour innovation through its implementation of Extended Colour Gamut technology. This revolutionary screening technology enables superior colour consistency, expanded design possibilities, and enhanced print performance, setting new benchmarks in print quality across the region.

​Demonstrating its relentless pursuit of excellence, the company serves an impressive portfolio of prestigious clientele and has consistently delivered world-class solutions earning recognition across Asia and Europe. This includes honors at the prestigious FPLMA Awards in Australia. The organisation’s success is deeply reflected in the accolades bestowed upon its leadership. Director Nalani Madhushani was recently honoured by PrintWeek Asia in its esteemed Women to Watch segment, receiving the coveted Factory Person of the Year Award as a testament to her visionary leadership and commitment to operational excellence.

​Commenting on the occasion and the company’s continuous commitment to the industry, Director Nalani Madhushani noted the organisation’s dedication to uplifting local standards. “At Veepee, excellence is not merely a goal, it is a tradition, a responsibility, and a promise that continues to shape the future of print,” she stated. Beyond commercial success, Veepee Graphics Lanka takes immense pride in contributing to the economic progress of Sri Lanka by empowering local businesses to grow, compete, and thrive in a dynamic global marketplace.
