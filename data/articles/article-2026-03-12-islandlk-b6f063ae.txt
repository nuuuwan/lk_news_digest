St. Anthony’s Service launches new digital platforms to strengthen customer convenience and service excellence

St. Anthony’s Service, a subsidiary of St. Anthony’s Hardware (Pvt) Ltd, has officially launched its dedicated website and social media platforms, marking a significant milestone in the company’s ongoing digital transformation journey. The website, www.stanthonysservice.lk, was ceremonially launched by Sarath Ganamage, Director / General Manager of St. Anthony’s Hardware (Pvt) Ltd, together with r Rumesh Jayasekara, Senior Marketing Manager, and Sujeewa Herath, Senior Service Manager, reaffirming the Group’s commitment to innovation, accessibility, and customer-centric service delivery.

The website serves as a comprehensive and centralised digital hub, enabling contractors, builders, and homeowners to conveniently explore a wide range of service solutions under one platform. With clearly structured and dedicated pages for each service category, users can seamlessly navigate through hardware-related services, solar hot water and PV system solutions, machinery repairs, water purification services, CCTV solutions, and home appliance maintenance.
