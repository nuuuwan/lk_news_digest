Access Motors launches Range Rover Boutique Residency in Colombo

To deepen its engagement with luxury clients, Access Motors has launched a Range Rover Boutique Residency in Colombo, Sri Lanka. Located at ITC Rathnadeepa Colombo One Workplaces, the immersive space embodies the brand’s modern luxury ethos through minimalist architecture and curated design. Unlike a traditional showroom, the boutique offers an intimate, highly personalized client journey. Trained specialists guide one-to-one consultations, showcasing the latest Range Rover models, bespoke configurations, and lifestyle selections.

The residency marks a strategic milestone in Range Rover’s brand evolution, reinforcing its commitment to exclusive, experience-driven engagement in a calm, contemporary setting.
