TRCSL staff donate to ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund

The Telecommunications Regulatory Commission of Sri Lanka (TRCSL) has donated to the ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund the equivalent of one day’s salary of its staff together with funds allocated for employee welfare amounting to over Rs. 12.8 million.

The contribution was handed over to the Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake, by the TRCSL Director General Air Vice Marshal (Retd.) Bandula Herath, together with Administration Director Wing Commander (Retd.) Senaka Ruwanpathirana, and the Finance Director M.K. Jayantha.
