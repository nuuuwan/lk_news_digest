CA Sri Lanka donates Rs. 10 m to ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund

The Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka (CA Sri Lanka) has contributed Rs. 10 million to the ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ fund, established to support the recovery and rehabilitation of people affected by the recent Ditwah cyclone and to rebuild the country.

The donation cheque was handed over to the Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake, by CA Sri Lanka President Tishan Subasinghe recently. Vice President Anojie De Silva, former President Heshana Kuruppu, CEO Lakmali Priyangika and Head of Marketing Udara Jayasinghe were also present.
