Fostering private sector cooperation with BRICS

BRICS Chamber of Commerce and Industry India and BRICS Chamber of Commerce Sri Lanka recently signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) in New Delhi to cooperate with each other to promote trade, services, investment, and tourism. The signatories to the MoU were BRICS Chamber of Commerce and Industry India Director General Dr. B.B.L. Madhukar and BRICS Chamber of Commerce Sri Lanka President Ishanga Mahipala.
