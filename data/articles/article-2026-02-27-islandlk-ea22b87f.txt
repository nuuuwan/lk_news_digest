Nationwide vehicle after-sales support assured as Evolution Auto joins hands with Sterling Aftercare

Evolution Auto (Pvt) Ltd recently announced a strategic partnership with Sterling Aftercare, offering customers and prospective buyers across Sri Lanka access to premium full-service support for all Evolution Auto vehicle brands.

The collaboration guarantees that every Evolution Auto customer can receive reliable, high-quality aftercare, thanks to service centers that are conveniently located in Nugegoda, Kadawatha, Rathmalana, Galle, and Minuwangoda.

To satisfy the increasing demand for easily accessible, certified vehicle maintenance, Sterling Aftercare offers its trusted expertise and nationwide presence in a variety of areas, including collision repair, auto detailing, mobile support, and vehicle repair and servicing.

Speaking on the partnership, Virann De Zoysa, CEO of Evolution Auto (Pvt) Ltd, said: “Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. This partnership with Sterling Aftercare means that Evolution Auto vehicle owners can now enjoy peace of mind, knowing that premium servicing, repairs, and aftercare are accessible to them nationwide. Our goal is to establish convenience and trust at each stage of the customer’s journey.”
