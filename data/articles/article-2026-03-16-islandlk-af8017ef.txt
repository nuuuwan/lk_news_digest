Haleon continues partnership with Sri Lanka Dental Association for second consecutive year

Haleon Sri Lanka, formerly SmithKline Beecham Private Limited, a leading consumer healthcare product company, has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Sri Lanka Dental Association (SLDA) for the second consecutive year as the Official Sponsor of World Oral Health Day, 2026. The MoU was formalised at a ceremony held on March 4.

Celebrated on March 20 each year, this year’s World Oral Health Day theme, “A happy mouth is a happy life,” highlights the essential role oral health plays in overall wellbeing at every stage of life.
