Elegant Mother’s Day brunch at Graze Kitchen

Hilton Colombo celebrates Mother’s Day with ‘Graze Her with Love,’ a thoughtfully curated brunch experience at Graze Kitchen, the hotel’s signature all-day dining restaurant. Presenting delicious cuisine, live jazz atmosphere, and a celebratory atmosphere, this exclusive Mother’s Day brunch will allow guests to treat their mothers to an indulgent occasion filled with refined, meaningful moments.

Taking place on 10 May 2026 from 12 noon to 3.00 PM, the Mother’s Day brunch at Graze Kitchen will allow guests to delight in a lavish buffet with signature highlights including Chardonnay infused salmon and lobster thermidor, an oceanic crustacean bar, a Vietnamese bánh mì live station, Hyderabadi lamb biriyani, and seafood teppanyaki. Each dish is designed to offer a diverse and delectable culinary journey perfect for a celebratory Mother’s Day get-together with family.

The Mother’s Day brunch will also feature Graze Kitchen’s assortment of international cuisines, ranging from Western Grill to Asian Kitchen, complemented by delightful native favorites and a tempting dessert selection.

Further, in partnership with Sothys Sri Lanka, a renowned premium beauty brand, guests could also participate in an exciting raffle draw where ten lucky mothers could stand a chance to win exclusive gifts and all mothers will receive a voucher courtesy of Sothys Sri Lanka, to be redeemed at their flagship store.
