FACETS 2026 to unveil new era for Sri Lanka’s gem and jewellery trade

The gem and jewellery sector is poised for a transformative start to 2026 as FACETS Sri Lanka, the country’s premier international exhibition, prepares to open its doors on January 3rd at a bold new location. Marking its 33rd edition, the event will debut at The Forum, Cinnamon Life at City of Dreams, signalling a major upgrade in scale and ambition for the industry’s flagship showcase.

Relocating to the integrated resort’s state-of-the-art convention facility, FACETS 2026 will span two floors, offering the most expansive platform in its history for buyers, traders, and connoisseurs. The modern infrastructure of Cinnamon Life is expected to provide an elevated and immersive experience, injecting fresh energy into the presentation of Sri Lanka’s celebrated gemstone heritage.

In a landmark development for the local trade, the globally revered Gübelin Gem Lab will participate for the first time as the Official Gem Lab Partner. This partnership, a first at any Sri Lankan gem exhibition, grants exhibitors and international visitors direct access to world-class on-site gem testing and certification from an institution with over a century of expertise.

The exhibition is bolstered by a robust consortium of premier corporate partners. Cinnamon Life serves as the Hotel and Venue Partner, with support from John Keells Properties as Real Estate Partner, Brinks as Official Security Partner, and LMD as Official Business Magazine. The strengthened backing underscores the event’s significant economic footprint.
