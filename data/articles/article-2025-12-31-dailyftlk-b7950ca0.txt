Tuli Cooray no more

We express our deep sorrow on the passing of former Joint Apparel Association Forum (JAAF) Secretary General Secretary General Tuli Cooray.

We shared a close, cordial, and highly professional relationship with Cooray since 2012, beginning with the launch of the JAAF Image Building Campaign, followed by the Ransalu Privilege Program two years later. He was a most helpful and principled gentleman who guided employee welfare initiatives within the apparel sector with unwavering commitment, leaving no stone unturned in his pursuit of excellence and perfection in every endeavour.

His contribution to the industry and to the wellbeing of its workforce will be remembered with respect and gratitude.
