Plans for 125th jubilee walk & carnival

Representatives of St. Bridget’s Convent, Colombo 7, invite media representatives to the official press conference for the school's 125th Jubilee Walk & Carnival, held at the Blue Hall on June 15, 2026. The event marks the launch of the landmark celebration, with organisers outlining plans for the Jubilee Walk and Carnival while acknowledging the contributions of sponsors and partners supporting the anniversary programme. The initiative aims to celebrate the school's 125-year legacy and foster continued support for the development of its students and school community. Pix by Kithsiri De Mel
