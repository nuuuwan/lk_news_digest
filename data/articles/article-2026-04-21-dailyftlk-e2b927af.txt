Israeli strikes target four towns in southern Lebanon despite ceasefire

Israeli strikes have hit several towns in Lebanon’s south in recent hours despite a 10-day ceasefire in place, Lebanon’s State-run National News Agency (NNA) reported.

A drone targeted the vicinity of the Litani River in the town of Qaqaiyat al-Jisr, NNA said, adding that there is no information on casualties yet.

An NNA correspondent in Tyre, meanwhile, reported a bombing in the town of Shamaa.

Another correspondent in Marjayoun reported that Israeli forces carried out a bombing in the town of Taybeh, as well as between the towns of Al-Qusayr and Al-Qantara.
