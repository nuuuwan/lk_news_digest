Banana hair mask …

Yes, it’s the banana scene, once again, and this time let’s work on three banana hair masks …

Mix a well-mashed banana with a tablespoon of sweet almond oil.

Apply the mixture to hair, strand by strand, and to the scalp, then cover with a towel for 30 minutes.

After 30 minutes, wash your hair as usual! Simple, isn’t it?

Mix a mashed banana with a vegetable oil of your choice (almond oil, coconut, etc.) and add an egg yolk and a teaspoon or tablespoon of honey, and mix well.

Apply to the lengths and leave on for about 30 minutes before washing your hair.

For this mask, the egg yolk and honey can be replaced with plain yoghurt and avocado oil!

A splash of lemon juice can also be added to increase the moisturising power of the mask.

Mix a mashed banana with an egg, four teaspoons of olive oil, three teaspoons of honey and a little milk to give it a semi-liquid texture.

Apply to hair and leave on for 30 minutes before rinsing in the shower!

Depending on your hair problems, the banana hair mask can be applied several times a month. If your hair is very dry, or you have a lot of itching on the scalp, you can do a mask every week. If you just need a little shot of nutrition from time to time, once every two weeks will be more than enough!
