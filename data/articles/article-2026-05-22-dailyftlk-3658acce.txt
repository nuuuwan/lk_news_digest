Fentons IT hosts exclusive CIO cocktail networking evening with IceWarp

Fentons IT (FIT), in partnership with IceWarp, recently hosted an exclusive CIO (Chief Information Officers) networking cocktail evening titled “The IceBreaker Moment,” bringing together senior IT leaders from Sri Lanka’s leading organisations.

Held on 24 April at SHARK by Port City, the event was designed as a relaxed, open-air gathering, moving away from traditional corporate formats to encourage meaningful conversations and authentic engagement. The audience included CIOs and technology decision-makers from over 40 organisations, primarily across the BFSI sector, alongside telecom, manufacturing, and diversified conglomerates.

The evening offered attendees a valuable opportunity to interact directly with IceWarp’s global leadership beyond traditional formal settings, encouraging more open and meaningful engagement, dialogue, and knowledge sharing.

The two-hour event featured interactive activities including the IceWarp Trivia Quiz, “Who’s Behind the Ice?” challenge, a scavenger hunt, and a catch dart game, creating a lively and engaging atmosphere supported by an experienced MC and light entertainment.

Key moments of the evening also included the recognition of valued customers through memento presentations, alongside the opportunity for select clients to share their experiences with IceWarp solutions, adding a personal and authentic dimension to the gathering.

Commenting on the initiative, FIT General Manager Yoosoof Ihthisham said: “‘The IceBreaker Moment’ was designed to move beyond traditional formats and create a space where industry leaders could connect more openly. By fostering a relaxed and engaging environment, we aimed to build stronger relationships and create a more meaningful experience for our partners and customers.”

Sharing his thoughts on the event, IceWarp India and Middle East CEO Pramod Sharda said: “At IceWarp, we believe meaningful partnerships are built through genuine engagement and collaboration. Events like ‘The IceBreaker Moment’ create valuable opportunities for technology leaders to exchange ideas, strengthen relationships, and explore how innovation can drive the future of digital transformation across industries.”

The event reflects FIT’s continued commitment to building meaningful industry relationships and delivering value beyond technology, aligning with its vision of enabling impactful and future-ready digital partnerships.
