PR agency Mark and Comm wins double Gold at 15th ACEF Global Customer Engagement Awards

Mark and Comm (Pvt) Ltd, Sri Lanka’s leading boutique public relations and communications agency, has won two Gold awards at the 15th ACEF Global Customer Engagement Awards held on 30 April 2026 at the Westin Garden City Hotel in Mumbai. The agency claimed Gold in both the Grand Prix category for PR Agency of the Year and the Individual Excellence Honor for PR Professional of the Year, the latter recognising managing director, Thanzyl Thajudeen.

Now in its 15th edition, the ACEF Global Customer Engagement Awards has grown into one of Asia’s most respected platforms celebrating excellence in customer engagement, brand strategy, and communications, drawing entries from agencies and brands across India, Sri Lanka, Nepal, Bangladesh, UAE, Singapore, and beyond. Competing against a strong regional field, Mark and Comm’s double Gold win marks a significant moment for Sri Lankan PR on the international stage.

The wins come at a particularly meaningful time for the agency. Mark and Comm this year celebrate 15 years in business. From its origins as a boutique consultancy, the agency has grown into an internationally connected communications practice, serving clients across insurance, mobility, financial services, technology, hospitality, energy, agriculture, healthcare, education, and sustainability among others.
