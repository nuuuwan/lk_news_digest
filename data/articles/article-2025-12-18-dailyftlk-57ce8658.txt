Indra Traders gives Rs. 100 m to ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund

Indra Traders Ltd., has made a financial contribution of Rs. 100 million to the Government’s ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund, established to provide relief to those affected by Cyclone Ditwah.

The relevant cheque was formally handed over by Indra Traders Founder and Chairman Indra Silva to Secretary to the President, Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake, at the Presidential Secretariat yesterday. Indra Traders Board of Directors Rushanka Silva and Hashindra Silva, along with General Manager Sachini Silva, were also present on the occasion.
