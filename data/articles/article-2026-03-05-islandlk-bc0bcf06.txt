Women powering South Asia’s first integrated resort

As South Asia’s first fully integrated resort, City of Dreams Sri Lanka represents a new chapter in the nation’s tourism story — one defined by scale, sophistication and global standards. Bringing together luxury hospitality, gaming, retail, entertainment and corporate spaces within one ecosystem, the integrated resort model demands complex operations, technical precision and high-level leadership.

At the centre of this ecosystem are the women helping power it every day.

Within City of Dreams Sri Lanka, women are represented not only in hospitality and guest-facing roles, but in complex, high-responsibility environments including surveillance, gaming operations, information security and security management. These are spaces that require analytical thinking, regulatory compliance, technological expertise and decisive leadership — and women are playing a critical role in shaping their success.

The integrated resort model creates a dynamic professional landscape where multidisciplinary talent thrives. For women in Sri Lanka’s tourism and entertainment sectors, this translates into broader career pathways, exposure to international best practices, and access to leadership opportunities within a world-class operation.

City of Dreams Sri Lanka’s commitment to inclusion extends beyond representation. Equal opportunity hiring practices, structured mentorship opportunities, leadership training programmes and strong maternity support policies ensure that women are empowered to grow sustainably within the organisation. A safe and transparent reporting structure reinforces a culture of trust and accountability, creating an environment where employees feel secure, supported and seen.

At the heart of this philosophy is a simple principle: performance speaks louder than stereotypes.
