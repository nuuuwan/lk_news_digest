Pink Eagle Holdings consolidates leadership in diversified services and global expansion

With bold vision and global outlook, one of Sri Lanka’s trailblazing conglomerates, Pink Eagle Holdings (Pvt) Ltd, a modern investment holding company, steps into the spotlight. Established in recent years and rooted in Sri Lankan heritage, the organisation has rapidly grown from a single founding idea and evolved into a diversified ecosystem including tea exports, logistics, warehousing, food production, and hospitality.

Pink Eagle Holdings is driven with an ambition to redefine what a Sri Lankan business group can achieve on the world stage, and is growing into a trusted name among international investors and partners, with ventures and collaborations covering Sri Lanka, Dubai, UK, France, Kenya, Ethiopia, China, and Taiwan. The Group continues to expand globally, consolidating leadership in Sri Lanka’s export and service sectors through its subsidiaries Continental Tea (Pvt) Ltd, Abanchy Ceylon (Pvt) Ltd, Continental Freight (Pvt) Ltd, T Co by Adra (Pvt) Ltd, Continental Food Products (Pvt) Ltd, and Pink Panda.
