Sunshine Holdings marks World Diabetes Day

Sunshine Holdings marked World Diabetes Day with its “Unmask Diabetes 2025” initiative. Across 10 Healthguard outlets in Colombo, the Sunshine Foundation for Good provided free blood sugar and blood pressure screenings to nearly 900 people over two days.

Participants received professional consultations on prevention, management, and lifestyle adjustments. Group CEO Shyam Sathasivam emphasized the importance of early detection and accessible preventive healthcare to address Sri Lanka’s growing diabetes challenge.

This community-based program is part of the Foundation’s ongoing healthcare pillar, Suwa Diviya, focused on raising awareness and supporting national wellbeing.
