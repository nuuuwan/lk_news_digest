Medihelp strengthens its commitment to Moratuwa community

Medihelp Hospitals, Sri Lanka’s largest primary healthcare network, has integrated the renowned Moratuwa Medical Centre into its family. This step expands affordable, quality healthcare in the region. The familiar staff and services will continue uninterrupted, with Medihelp enhancing infrastructure to meet its national standards. The integration strengthens Medihelp’s commitment to the Moratuwa community and its mission to make comprehensive healthcare accessible to all Sri Lankans.
