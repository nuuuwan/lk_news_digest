Construction sector fuels rally as CSE surges past 22,000 points

The Colombo Stock Exchange (CSE) witnessed a robust trading session yesterday, propelled notably by the construction sector. The All Share Price Index (ASPI) closed sharply higher, exceeding the 22,000-point milestone amidst healthy turnover levels. Broad-based buying interest was evident as most sectors advanced.

Market activity was dominated by Tokyo Cement Company, Access Engineering PLC, and Colombo Dockyard PLC.

Significant crossings were also recorded in several key counters, including Tokyo Cement Company (Lanka) PLC, Lanka Glass PLC, Richard Pieris & Company PLC, Sierra Cables PLC, C. T. Land Development PLC, and Hemas Holdings PLC.

Notably, trading in Tokyo Cement Company alone contributed to approximately 8% of the day’s total turnover, underscoring the intense investor focus on construction-related stocks. The session remained busy throughout largely driven by this sectoral momentum.

Consequently, the turnover stood at Rs 3.8 billion with nine crossings. Those seven crossings were reported in ACL Cables 300,000 shares crossed to the tune of Rs 75.5 million and its share price traded at Rs 255, Tokyo Cement 500,000 shares crossed to the tune of Rs 51 million and its share price traded at Rs 102, Laughs Gas 658,000 shares crossed to the tune of Rs 42.1 million and its share price traded at Rs 53.10, Richard Pieris one million shares crossed to the tune of Rs 41 million and its share price traded at Rs 40, Sierra Cables 1.2 million shares crossed to the tune of Rs 40 million and its share price traded at Rs 40, RIL Properties one million shares crossed to the tune of Rs 31.5   million and its share price traded at Rs 31.50 and Cables Solutions  1.5 million shares crossed to the tune of Rs 31.7 million and its share price traded at Rs 14.50.

In the retail market top seven companies that have mainly contributed to the turnover  Tokyo Cement (Non-Voting) Rs 283 million (3.5 million shares traded), Colombo Dockyards Rs 212 million (1.3 million shares traded), Laughs Gas Rs 151.7 million (2.8 million shares traded), ACL Cables Rs 151 million (600,000 shares traded), Sierra Cables Rs 141 million (4.3 million shares traded),  Tokyo Cement Rs 117 million shares traded) and Access Engineering Rs 92 million (1.3 million shares traded). During the day 105.7 million shares volumes changed hands in 33384 transactions.
