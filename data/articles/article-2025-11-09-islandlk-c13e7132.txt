Global Packaging Congress 2025 showcases international collaboration

The Global Packaging Congress 2025, themed “Packaged to Perfection,” concluded successfully on 30th October at the BMICH, Colombo, with over 300 participants representing more than 50 member countries of the World Packaging Organization (WPO) from 5 continents.

Organized by the Sri Lanka Institute of Packaging (SLIP) in collaboration with the World Packaging Organization, the Congress served as a key highlight of Packaging Week 2025, which also featured the Lankapak Exhibition, WPO Board Meetings, and the Sri Lanka Packaging Awards.

The event brought together global and local leaders from across the packaging, manufacturing, and sustainability sectors to discuss key themes such as circularity in packaging, innovation, education, and the path toward sustainable industrial growth.
