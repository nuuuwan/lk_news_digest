41st Colombo Shopping Festival opens at BMICH

The 41st edition of the Colombo Shopping Festival (CSF) – Avurudu Fair is now on at the BMICH Exhibition Centre, offering days of festive shopping in celebration of the Sinhala and Tamil New Year.

The ceremonial opening commenced with the traditional lighting of the oil lamp on 8 April 2026, in the presence of distinguished guests, industry leaders, and invitees. Organised by Aitken Spence Conventions and Exhibitions, the event continues to reinforce its position as one of Sri Lanka’s premier consumer exhibitions.

Running from 8–12 April 2026, from 10:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. daily, the Colombo Shopping Festival brings together over 200 Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) from across the island, offering a vibrant marketplace that supports local businesses while delivering exceptional value to shoppers.

This year’s Avurudu Fair promises an unmatched retail experience, featuring a wide range of products, including fashion, lifestyle, homeware, beauty, and more. Visitors can shop top brands such as Boss, EL Holdings, Vantage, Miniso, Governor Shirts, Edge Casual, Avirate, Cetaphil, Phoenix, Rite Shu, and many more, alongside a diverse selection of Indian stalls offering exclusive collections at significant discounts.

The festival creates an opportunity for families and shoppers to prepare for the New Year with deals, festive offers, and a lively atmosphere filled with seasonal excitement.

With its strong legacy and continued commitment to empowering local entrepreneurs, the Colombo Shopping Festival stands as a key highlight in Sri Lanka’s event calendar, bringing together commerce, culture, and celebration under one roof.
