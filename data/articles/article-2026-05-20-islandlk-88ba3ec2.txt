Wrinkles!

None of us would like to see wrinkles cropping up but as we grow older, wrinkles do remind us that we have aged (lol).

However, I would suggest that you try this out in order to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles … hopefully!

You will need 01 teaspoon each of coconut oil, honey, and fresh aloe vera gel.

Mix all the ingredients together, in a clean bowl, until smooth, and then apply evenly to your face and neck.

Leave it on for 30 minutes and then rinse with lukewarm water, and pat dry.

In a clean bowl, mix 02 tablespoons of plain yoghurt and 01 teaspoon of honey.

Wash your face and pat dry and then apply the mixture evenly to the skin.

Leave it on for 15–20 minutes, then rinse with cool water, and gently dry your face. Follow with moisturiser, if needed.

Yoghurt gently exfoliates with lactic acid, and helps remove dull surface skin, while honey deeply moisturises, softens rough skin texture, helps improve the appearance of uneven tanning, and gives skin a smoother and fresher look.
