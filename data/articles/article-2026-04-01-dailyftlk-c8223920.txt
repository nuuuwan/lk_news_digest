Lakderana Investments celebrates 20 years of growth, trust and financial resilience

Lakderana Investments Ltd., celebrated a significant milestone as it marked 20 years of empowering Sri Lankan individuals and businesses, reflecting on a journey of steady growth, financial resilience, and strong customer trust. The anniversary was commemorated at a grand ceremony held at Monarch Imperial on 7 March, attended by the Board of Directors, distinguished members of the banking and finance sector, business partners, and the company’s dedicated workforce. Founded in 2006 with a team of just six individuals, Lakderana Investments has grown into a well-established financial services provider with 32 branches across Sri Lanka. Over the past two decades, the company has served SMEs and Micro-entrepreneurs Islandwide, supporting their working capital requirements through a range of financial products. Lakderana’s services cater to multiple sectors including Agriculture, Transport, Hiring, Trading, Fisheries, Poultry, and wholesale and retail businesses, contributing to the growth of grassroots entrepreneurship and local industries.

Demonstrating strategic agility, the company has also expanded into gold loan services under the brands “Ran Saviya” and “Ran Ithiriya”. Within just three years, this segment has grown rapidly, enabling Lakderana to serve customers efficiently while maintaining a conservative gearing structure to ensure long-term financial stability. Lakderana’s progress is further supported by its BB (Stable) credit rating, reflecting strong governance, disciplined financial management, and prudent risk practices. Even during challenging economic periods, the company has maintained a consistent repayment record, strengthening its credibility among leading commercial banks and financial partners. From its modest beginnings, Lakderana Investments has grown into an organisation with a workforce of over 300 employees, driven by a strong corporate culture built on professionalism, innovation, and customer commitment. Reflecting on the milestone, the management noted that the company’s portfolio strength, expanding branch network, and loyal customer base clearly demonstrate the remarkable progress achieved during its 20-year journey. As Lakderana Investments celebrates this important milestone, the company remains committed to supporting Sri Lankan entrepreneurs and communities through responsible financial solutions for many years to come.
