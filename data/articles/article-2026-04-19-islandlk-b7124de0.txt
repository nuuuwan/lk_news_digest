Kia expands Sri Lanka footprint with landmark showroom at Altair

Kia Motors Lanka has significantly bolstered its presence in Sri Lanka with the opening of a striking new showroom at the iconic Altair high-rise. Unveiled to the media on April 7, 2026, the 2,700-square-foot facility is strategically located at Retail 3 of the development, offering maximum visibility within one of Colombo’s premier luxury lifestyle destinations.

Designed according to Kia’s global brand guidelines, the showroom provides a modern, immersive environment focused on innovation and service excellence. The opening marks a strategic milestone for the brand as it seeks to deepen customer engagement and enhance accessibility within key urban hubs.

“The opening of this new showroom signals Kia’s renewed commitment to Colombo,” stated Kia Motors Lanka Chairman Mahen Thambiah. “It represents a key milestone in our strategy to deepen customer relationships and expand the Kia footprint.”

A major highlight of the launch is the Sri Lankan debut of the all-new Kia Tasman, the brand’s first-ever double cab pickup. Alongside the Tasman, the showroom features Kia’s diverse portfolio, including popular models like the Sorento, Sportage, and Picanto, as well as its latest electric vehicle lineup (EV3, EV5, EV6, and EV9). This expansion reinforces Kia’s long-standing leadership in design and technology within the local automotive industry.
