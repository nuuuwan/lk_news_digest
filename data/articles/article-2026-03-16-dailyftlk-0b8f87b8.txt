Tankers continue to load oil on Iran’s Kharg Island: Vessel-tracking platform

A tanker has been seen loading oil on Iran’s Kharg Island, two days after the US struck military facilities there.

The vessel-tracking platform TankerTrackers said seven more tankers are seen at the anchorage. Five had already loaded fuel oil while two are waiting to load, according to satellite imagery. It was not immediately clear who the tankers belong to.
