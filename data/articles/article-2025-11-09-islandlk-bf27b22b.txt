SINGER powers ahead with TAILG Electric Motorbikes supporting green mobility

SINGER (Sri Lanka) PLC announced its entry into the electric mobility sector through the introduction of TAILG Electric Motorbikes in Sri Lanka. SINGER’s ongoing expansion into sustainable product categories and its goal of increasing access to affordable, environmentally friendly transportation solutions are reflected in this collaboration with TAILG, one of the world’s largest electric two-wheeler manufacturers.

Commenting on the launch, Mahesh Wijewardene, Group Managing Director, SINGER (Sri Lanka) PLC, stated, “At SINGER, we believe that future mobility must be useful, affordable, and environmentally friendly. An important step in fostering sustainable innovation in Sri Lanka’s transportation sector is our partnership with TAILG.”

Lin Peng, TAILG’s Marketing Director for Asia, added, “We are proud to partner with SINGER Sri Lanka, a brand that shares our vision for a sustainable and cleaner world. Combining SINGER’s local strength with TAILG’s global expertise in electric mobility, this partnership goes beyond business—it’s a shared commitment to a greener future for generations to come.”
