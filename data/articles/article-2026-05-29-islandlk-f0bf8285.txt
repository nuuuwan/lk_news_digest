Bingxue Sri Lanka opens Its first outlet in Dehiwala

Bingxue Sri Lanka officially marked a significant milestone with the grand opening of its first local outlet in Dehiwala on 16th May 2026, introducing the globally recognized dessert and beverage brand Bingxue to the Sri Lankan market for the very first time. The launch represents a key step in the brand’s regional expansion and signals the beginning of a new chapter in Sri Lanka’s rapidly evolving dessert and café culture.
