SMB Finance PLC conducts ‘Light for Learning’ to empower underprivileged children

SMB Finance PLC through one of its innovative corporate social responsibility initiatives called “Light for Learning” aimed at empowering under privileged children in Sri Lanka, donated complete set of school supplies including school bags, lunch boxes, water bottles, school books, stationery and other essential school supplies to all children studying at Sri Gunananda Vidyalaya, Colombo 13.

SMB Finance PLC embraced the spirit of sustainability by sharing its prosperity to nurture the next generation by empowering young minds through education.

Addressing the event, the Executive Director/ Chief Executive Officer of SMB Finance PLC, Supul Wijesinghe stated “As a responsible financial institution in Sri Lanka, we believe our role extends beyond business — it includes giving back to society. This initiative of our Chairman reiterates the continuous commitment of SMB Finance PLC to empower young learners and create a lasting social value”.
