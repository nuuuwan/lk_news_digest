Scheduled for three, ended up doing seven

Singer/entertainer Rajiv Sebastian was booked for three shows, in the UK, but ended up doing seven, and that’s a huge vote of confidence from UK audiences/organisers.

Usually that happens when the first gigs sell out, or get great word-of-mouth, and the organisers immediately move into action by adding extra dates to meet the demand.

Well, it certainly did happen where Rajiv is concerned and that alone is a clear indication that everything went really well for Rajiv.

Being 36 years in the music scene, with his band The Clan, he is now working on putting together a special event to celebrate the anniversary, along with his fans, right here in Colombo.

“I want to make it a unique event, incorporating classics, calypso, pop and kaffringha (baila),” said Rajiv.

Described as a versatile vocal powerhouse, Rajiv Sebastian certainly has something special up his sleeves for local music lovers!
