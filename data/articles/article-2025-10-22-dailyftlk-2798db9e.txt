Harin appointed UNP Deputy Secretary General of Political Mobilisation

The United National Party (UNP) has announced the creation of a new senior position within the party structure, appointing former Minister Harin Fernando as Deputy Secretary General of Political Mobilisation.

According to a party statement, Fernando will be responsible for fostering cooperation among political parties and promoting a unified national program. He will also oversee the coordination of meetings and political activities organised by the UNP.
