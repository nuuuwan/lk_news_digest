Last day of 'vehicle tuning dansala'

The final day of the 'vehicle tuning dansala' was held today in front of its head office in Narahenpita. The vehicle tuning dansala was announced on June 10. Motorcycles and three-wheelers, which make up the majority of Sri Lanka’s vehicle fleet, underwent emission tests aimed at reducing harmful exhaust emissions. Pix by Kithsiri De Mel
