Asriel Marketing donates Rs. 5 m to ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund

Asriel Marketing Ltd. has contributed Rs. 5 million to the Government’s ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund, established to assist communities affected by the Ditwah cyclone. The contribution was formally handed at the Presidential Secretariat by Asriel Marketing Chairman Shiran Peiris together with other executives of the company.
