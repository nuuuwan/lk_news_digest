AMAMI hotel supplies brand Ranyan Industries  wins Silver and top achiever title at CNCI Awards

Ranyan Industries Directors Harshala De Silva, and Ranuka De Silva, Manager Finance and Administration Maduranga Botheju receiving the awards

Ranyan Industries Ltd., the creators of the prestigious Amami brand, has been honored with the Silver Award and Top Achiever Award in the Small Category at the CNCI Achievers Awards 2025, organised by the Ceylon National Chamber of Industries in collaboration with the Ministry of Industry and Entrepreneurship Development.

Founded by Ranil de Silva, Ranyan Industries began as a small enterprise with a visionary goal — to pioneer Sri Lanka’s first locally manufactured hotel amenity kit. Today, the Amami brand has grown into a leading name recognised by top hotels such as Jetwing, Ramada, Galle Face Hotel, Galadari, Grand Hotel, Shangrilla, Mahaweli Reach, Anantaya and etc.. for its high-quality Ayurvedic and herbal products including shampoos, shower gels, air fresheners, glycerin soaps, detergents, Dental Kits, Shaving Kits and Shower Caps. Every Amami product is crafted using locally sourced raw materials, combining modern production standards with traditional Sri Lankan wellness values. Under the leadership of the second generation, Harshala de Silva and Ranuka de Silva, the company continues to expand its global footprint through innovation, sustainability, and a commitment to excellence. With over six decades of collective dedication, Ranyan Industries stands as a beacon of Sri Lankan craftsmanship and entrepreneurial spirit, proudly supporting the nation’s journey toward industrial and economic progress.
