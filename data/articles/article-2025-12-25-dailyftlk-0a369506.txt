Taj Samudra, Colombo hosts India’s External Affairs Minister

India's External Affairs Minister Dr. S. Jaishankar (left) received by Samrat Datta at the Taj Samudra

Taj Samudra, Colombo was honored to warmly welcome India’s External Affairs Minister, Dr. S. Jaishankar, on 22 December 2025, by Taj Maldives and Sri Lanka Area Director, and Taj Samudra General Manager Samrat Datta at the Taj Samudra, Colombo
