CCI’s 21st AGM today

The CCI will hold its 21st Annual General Meeting today at The Kingsbury Hotel.

CCI was first established in 2001 as a company limited by guarantee and later in 2019 was incorporated by an Act of Parliament as a body corporate. Presently 17 professional and trade associations related to the construction industry along with 300 companies have joined CCI as members.

At this 21st AGM, V. Manilal Fernando will formally assume the mantle of President of the Chamber.

A special feature at this event will be the signing of the MoU between this Chamber and the Indo-Sri Lanka Chamber of Commerce and Industry (ISCCI) to enhance the cooperation, economic and business ties between the member companies and state agencies of the two countries.

Also, Adovacta Institute Chairman and a leading economic analyst, Murtaza Jufferjee will deliver the keynote speech on “Future Economic Trends with the Budget 2026 and prospects for construction Industry of Sri Lanka.
