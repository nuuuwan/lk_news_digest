Amana Takaful’s Medical Claims Team recognized for excellence

Amana Takaful Insurance’s Medical Claims team has been recognized at the CPM Best Management Practices Company (BMPC) Awards 2026, highlighting excellence in management practices and operational performance.

At this year’s awards, Amana Takaful Insurance was ranked among the Top Forty Outstanding Companies and recognized as the Sector Winner in the General Insurance (Private Sector) category, reflecting the strength of its operational framework and disciplined execution.

This recognition underscores the company’s continuous efforts to enhance its claims management processes through structured workflows, process optimization, and the integration of digital capabilities. These initiatives have contributed to improved efficiency, faster turnaround times, and a more seamless customer experience.

Commenting on the achievement, Siva Karthigun, Chief Executive Officer – General, stated:

“We are encouraged by this recognition, which reflects the strength of our management practices and the consistent effort demonstrated by our Medical Claims team. Their commitment to maintaining high standards, improving efficiencies, and delivering a reliable and transparent claims experience has been instrumental in achieving this milestone.”
