LOLC Securities hosts inaugural Investor Forum on Navigating Global Uncertainty

LOLC Securities Limited, the stockbroking subsidiary of LOLC Group, successfully hosted its inaugural Investor Forum under the theme “Capitalizing on the Economic Upswing” on 6 March 2026 at the Hilton Colombo Residence, bringing together approximately 100 high-net-worth individuals including their existing and prospective investors.

Marking a significant milestone for the company, the first-ever Investor Forum brought together leading industry professionals for a meaningful panel discussion, complemented by keynote addresses and insightful presentations, emphasising LOSEC’s continued commitment to deepening client relationships beyond traditional brokerage and advisory services.

Designed as a dynamic platform for knowledge exchange and strategic engagement, the forum provided participants with actionable insights while fostering constructive dialogue on investor expectations, concerns, and long-term portfolio strategies.

The event also addressed the implications of ongoing global conflicts and economic disruptions on capital market activity, offering investors timely perspectives on how geopolitical tensions, international trade shifts, and global economic uncertainties continue to shape market sentiment and influence investment decisions.
