Janashakthi Life steps in to support rural students post-cyclone Ditwah

Janashakthi Life, a leading name in the insurance industry and a subsidiary of JXG (Janashakthi Group), recently concluded a community-focused Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiative led by its Life Operations team, extending support to students of Maho / Bakmeewewa Primary School, a rural interior school impacted by the Ditwah Cyclone.

The initiative forms part of Janashakthi Life’s broader commitment to inclusive development and equitable access to education, particularly in underserved communities. Conducted in the aftermath of the cyclone, the project was designed to address the immediate educational needs of students, helping them resume learning in a more supportive and enabling environment.
