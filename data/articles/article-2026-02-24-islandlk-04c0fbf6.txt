Retail IT hosts enterprise retail technology forum

Retail IT (Pvt) Ltd, Sri Lanka’s leading retail technology solutions provider, recently hosted “Retail Industry: Technology Trends 2026 – The Enterprise Experience” at the Cinnamon Grand Colombo, unveiling its 2026 roadmap for enterprise retail transformation. The forum explored how retailers and large organisations must evolve amid growing operational complexity, integration requirements, and heightened security expectations.

The event brought together retail business owners, operations directors, and senior enterprise decision-makers from across Sri Lanka, representing a diverse cross-section of the retail and enterprise sectors. The programme focused on real-world applications of enterprise technology, featuring live demonstrations that showcased how integrated ERP platforms, advanced POS systems, advanced loyalty and intelligent security solutions can work in unison to enhance efficiency, visibility, and scalability in complex retail environments.

A highlight of the evening was the Sri Lankan launch of Adler ERP, an integrated enterprise business management platform introduced through practical use cases illustrating how retail operations, data management & customer management can function as a single, unified ecosystem. The latest retail specific devices such as POS & Kitchen Display devices were showcased by Ms. Kelly Lee of Posiflex Taiwan.
