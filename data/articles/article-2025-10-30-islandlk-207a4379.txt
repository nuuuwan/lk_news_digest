JXG – DHACK empowers young innovators for second consecutive year

JXG (Janashakthi Group) will return as the title sponsor for DHACK 2025 for the second consecutive year, reaffirming its commitment to fostering innovation and creative problem-solving among emerging tech talent. Organized by the Student Association of Information Technology at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, the event is scheduled for 1st November 2025, from 9.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. at the University.

This year’s competition will feature seven finalists presenting their UI/UX prototypes to a panel of judges, showcasing creative solutions to real-world challenges through thoughtful design and practical execution. The event will conclude with an awards ceremony recognizing the most impactful and outstanding innovations.
