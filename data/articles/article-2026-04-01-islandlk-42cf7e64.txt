ComBank, first to enable 3D Secure authentication for LankaPay/JCB cards

The Commercial Bank of Ceylon has further strengthened its digital payments leadership by becoming the first bank in Sri Lanka to enable 3D Secure (3DS) authentication for e-commerce transactions on the LankaPay/JCB National Card Scheme, significantly strengthening the security and global usability of the card.

This milestone makes the LankaPay/JCB card a fully empowered e-commerce instrument, allowing cardholders to shop securely at millions of merchants. The latest addition to the Bank’s robust 3DS-enabled card portfolio ensures that national scheme users benefit from the same high-level authentication and fraud protection extended across Commercial Bank’s international card offerings. Commercial Bank was first bank in Sri Lanka to introduce 3DS authentication for global card brands.

The development is particularly impactful for Sri Lankan consumers who favour the LankaPay/JCB card for overseas transactions due to its highly competitive cross-border charges. The new capability combines cost efficiency with global reach and internationally accepted security standards, elevating the national card scheme to a new level of relevance in digital commerce.
