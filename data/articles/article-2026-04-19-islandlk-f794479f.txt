Global alliances drive Isuzu’s enduring growth in Sri Lanka

Sathosa Motors PLC, the authorised dealer for Isuzu vehicles in Sri Lanka, recently hosted its prestigious Dealer Convention 2026, ‘The Elite Circle’ at Monarch imperial. The event brought together the company’s island wide network of spare parts dealers for an evening of recognition, appreciation and shared success.

The event was a celebration of the outstanding achievements and success of dealers who play a pivotal role in strengthening the Isuzu Genuine Parts network across Sri Lanka. A grand and memorable occasion, the convention was designed to reflect upon the company’s deep respect for its long-standing dealer partnerships, and their continuous contribution to the growth of the brand.

The event was graced by the presence of Nobuhisa Miyashita, Managing Director of Isuzu Motors Asia, who attended as the Guest of Honour, alongside several distinguished dignitaries and industry representatives. Sumal Perera, Chairman of the Access Group of Companies; Christopher Joshua, Vice Chairman of the Access Group; Srimal Fernando, Managing Director of SATHOSA Motors PLC; Dirk Joshua, Executive Director of the company; and Ravilal Alokabandara, General Manager, Spare Parts were also present.

Representatives from Isuzu Motors Limited, Isuzu Motors International Operations, and ITOCHU Corporation also joined the celebrations, highlighting the strong international partnerships that support the continued development of the Isuzu brand in Sri Lanka.
