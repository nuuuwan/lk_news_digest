LAUGFS Rubber clinches Silver at NCPC Awards 2025 for cleaner production excellence

LAUGFS Rubber, a subsidiary of LAUGFS Holdings, has been recognised with the Silver Award in the Rubber Industry sector under the large category at the National Cleaner Production Awards (NCPC) 2025, which stands as the highest recognition awarded within the sector this year. This prestigious national honour reaffirms the company’s leadership in sustainable and environmentally responsible manufacturing in Sri Lanka, underscoring its ongoing commitment to minimising environmental impact, enhancing operational efficiency, and promoting cleaner production practices across the industry.

This honour reflects LAUGFS Rubber’s unwavering commitment to driving resource-efficient operations at its Horana manufacturing facility, where eco-friendly processes, continuous resource tracking, and energy-saving initiatives have been embedded to meet evolving sustainability standards.

LAUGFS Rubber Director – Chief Executive Officer Chinthaka Wegapitiya said, “We are truly honoured to receive the Silver Award at the NCPC Awards 2025. This recognition strengthens our commitment to sustainability and responsible manufacturing. At LAUGFS Rubber, we remain dedicated to innovating and improving our environmental performance, ensuring that our operations contribute positively to both the industry and the nation’s sustainability goals.”

This accolade further reinforces LAUGFS Rubber’s continued contribution to a greener and more sustainable industrial future for Sri Lanka. LAUGFS Rubber specialises in the production of high-quality industrial tyres, serving customers in over 50 countries worldwide, with subsidiaries in the United States (LAUGFS USA LLC) and Europe (LAUGFS Europe B.V), establishing a strong international presence. Its state-of-the-art manufacturing facility operates with a deep commitment to innovation, quality, and sustainability. The company’s commitment to environmentally responsible manufacturing and employee health and safety is further demonstrated by its globally recognised certifications, including ISO 9001:2015, ISO 50001:2018, ISO 14001:2015, and ISO 45001:2018.
