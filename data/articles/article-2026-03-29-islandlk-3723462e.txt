NSB partners with IIHS to expand healthcare education access

The National Savings Bank (NSB) has partnered with the International Institute of Health Sciences (IIHS) to improve access to global healthcare education for Sri Lankan students.

Under a new MoU, NSB will provide student loan facilities through its island-wide branch network, enabling students from diverse backgrounds to enroll in IIHS programmes. These include Nursing, Physiotherapy, Biomedical Sciences, and International Foundation courses leading to globally recognized qualifications.

“We view education financing as a vital investment in the country’s future,” said NSB Acting General Manager Devika Silva.

The initiative aims to reduce foreign exchange outflows from overseas education while strengthening Sri Lanka’s role as a regional healthcare education hub.
