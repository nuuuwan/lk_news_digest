CSE to conduct a full day of trading from Disaster Recovery site

The Colombo Stock Exchange (CSE) is to conduct a planned full day of trading from the Disaster Recovery site using Automated Trading System Disaster Recovery Solution on Friday, 10th October 2025.

The exercise will be an industry wide activity with the participation of all stakeholders, to ensure the preparedness of all participants to meet the recovery needs of the industry.
