IMM snatches victory for Sri Lanka at ASOCIO Awards 2025

Inclusive Media Monitoring (Pvt) Ltd. (IMM) has snatched a historic victory for Sri Lanka, clinching the coveted ASOCIO Outstanding Digital Transformation Organization Award at a glittering ceremony held on November 7 in Taipei, Taiwan.

The Asian-Oceanian Computing Industry Organization (ASOCIO) is the premier ICT industry body in the Asia-Pacific region. Founded in 1984 and headquartered in Tokyo, ASOCIO represents more than 24,000 technology companies across 24 member economies, including Japan, Korea, Singapore, Australia, India, Malaysia, and now proudly Sri Lanka through its member federation FITIS. Known as the “Oscars of Asian digital excellence,” the annual ASOCIO Awards recognise organisations that demonstrate exceptional leadership in digital innovation and transformation.

This triumph puts Sri Lanka firmly on the regional digital map and underscores IMM’s leadership in delivering world-class ad verification, social media monitoring, and electronic media monitoring services. In an era where data drives decisions, IMM has emerged as the homegrown champion empowering Sri Lankan businesses to compete—and win—on a level playing field with global players.

“We didn’t just attend the ASOCIO Awards – we came, we showcased Sri Lankan ingenuity, and we snatched the top honour,” said Jayantha Sittampalam, Managing Director of Inclusive Media Monitoring (Pvt) Ltd. “This victory belongs to every Sri Lankan organisation that believes local innovation can lead Asia. Our new all-in-one dashboard is proof: from Colombo to the world stage, we are transforming how businesses track performance, verify ads, and outsmart competitors.”

The centrepiece of IMM’s award-winning transformation is its revolutionary all-in-one dashboard – a powerful, unified platform that allows organisations to monitor their own performance alongside every competitor in their sector in real time.

Wilva Mudalige, Executive Director in charge of operations elaborated stating that salient features include:

• Real-time ad verification across TV and radio – pay only for what actually airs

• Comprehensive social media & electronic media tracking in Sinhala, Tamil, and English

Starting at just LKR 20,000 per month, this proudly Sri Lankan solution is now the most advanced, accessible, and cost-effective media intelligence platform in the country. (IMM)
