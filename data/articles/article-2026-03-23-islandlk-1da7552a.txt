Tough time for local entertainment scene

The entertainment scene here is certain to feel the pinch due to the Middle East conflict, and petrol rationing.

Tourism, a big part of the country’s economy, is taking a hit with fewer tourists arriving because of airspace closures and flight cancellations.

This is definitely affecting businesses, like hotels, restaurants, and entertainment venues.

The petrol rationing isn’t helping either, but it is needed, at this point in time.

With limited fuel making it tough for people to travel and enjoy entertainment activities, the industry is certain to experience a decline in revenue and bookings.

I’m told that there are organisers who are contemplating making adjustments to their programmes, fearing that the crowd turn out will be poor.

Some of the artistes have also mentioned that, the past couple of weeks, very few inquiries have come their way.

Overall, I would say it’s going to be a tough time for Sri Lanka’s entertainment scene.
