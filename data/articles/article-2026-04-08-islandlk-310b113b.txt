Altair elevates living experience with appointment of RMS as Facilities Management Partner

The Management Council of Altair, Colombo’s most iconic residential and mixed-use development, has appointed Realty Management Services Ltd (RMS) as its new facilities management partner, marking a significant step forward in enhancing operational excellence and the overall resident experience. RMS is a subsidiary of Overseas Realty Ceylon PLC, the owning company of the World Trade Centre and Havelock City.

This transition reflects a renewed commitment to delivering consistent, professional, and responsive property management aligned with Altair’s premium positioning.

RMS brings over 28 years of industry experience in managing high-rise and luxury residential and commercial developments, underpinned by a financially sound parent company, and a strong foundation in engineering, compliance, and operational governance. The company is recognized for its professional approach to facilities management, having secured several new facilities management contracts at high-end residential apartments.

Residents can expect visible enhancements in day-to-day living, including improved response times, elevated housekeeping standards, strengthened security protocols, and more structured and transparent communication channels. These improvements will be driven through clearly defined service levels, a dedicated on-site management team, and robust performance monitoring practices.

Technology integration will further enhance the resident experience, with plans to introduce digital service platforms, streamlined reporting mechanisms, and data-informed maintenance planning. Sustainability-driven initiatives and energy optimization strategies will also be progressively introduced to improve efficiency and reduce operational costs over time.

Remaz Ghouse, COO of RMS stated, “Our priority is to establish a well-structured, accountable, and service-oriented operational environment at Altair. We are committed to delivering a seamless living experience that reflects the development’s architectural distinction and premium positioning, while building long-term value for its residents.”

This transition represents an important milestone in Altair’s evolution, reinforcing its position not only as a landmark of architectural excellence but also as a professionally managed, future-ready residential address with a strong focus on quality, sustainability, and resident well-being.
