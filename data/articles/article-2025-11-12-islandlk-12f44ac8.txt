One Galle Face celebrates 6th anniversary with world-class shopping and entertainment experiences

One Galle Face, is continuing to deliver unparalleled family, shopping, dining and entertainment experiences as it celebrates its 6th anniversary in November.

During 2025, One Galle Face continued to cement its reputation as a premier lifestyle destination, earning significant recognition both locally and internationally. The mall was recently honoured with the title of ‘Most Admired Shopping Center of the Year’ at the Sri Lanka Leadership Awards 2025, and was also named ‘Sri Lanka’s Most Loved Shopping Mall for 2025’ by the leading business magazine LMD, underscoring its position as a regional leader in retail and guest experience.

With over 300 world-class brands, One Galle Face has established itself as Sri Lanka’s premier retail destination, offering a dynamic mix of global fashion labels, fine dining experiences, and family entertainment. Over the past 12 months, the mall welcomed over 25 new brands, including Carnage, Under Armour, Taco Bell, Levi’s, The Body Shop and Birkenstock, further enhancing its diverse portfolio and delivering an even wider selection of the most loved brands to its customer base. With even more exciting new openings planned in the coming months, the mall continues to evolve as a one-stop hub for shopping, leisure, and lifestyle.
