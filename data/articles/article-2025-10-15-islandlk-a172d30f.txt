SLT-MOBITEL recognised as Sri Lanka’s Favourite Telecommunications Service Brand

SLT-MOBITEL, the National ICT Solutions Provider, secured the position of No. 1 Telecommunication Service Provider in Sri Lanka in the PepperCube Tracker for August 2025.

The ranking reaffirms SLT-MOBITEL’s standing as a trusted household name. Extending a heartfelt appreciation to its loyal customers for their support, the achievement reflects the preference and trust Sri Lankan consumers place in SLT-MOBITEL’s comprehensive, reliable communication services.

This latest milestone further demonstrates the brand’s ongoing commitment to delivering innovative, high-quality solutions and strengthening its reputation as a brand of choice across the nation.
