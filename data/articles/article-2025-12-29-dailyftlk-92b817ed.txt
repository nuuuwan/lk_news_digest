Prime Lands donates Rs. 200 m to “Rebuilding Sri Lanka” Fund

A financial contribution of Rs. 200 million has been made by Prime Lands to the “Rebuilding Sri Lanka” Fund, which was established to restore the livelihoods of people affected by Cyclone Ditwah and to rebuild the country following the disaster.

The cheque was handed over last week to the Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake by Prime Lands Group Chairman Premalal Brahmanage and Co-Chairperson Sandamini Perera. Prime Lands Group CEO Ruminda Randeniya and Director Anura Pathirage were also present
