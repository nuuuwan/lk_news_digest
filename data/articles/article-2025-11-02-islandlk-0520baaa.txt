Sri Lankan business networking event in Riyadh strengthens Gulf ties

The business networking event, held in Riyadh on Friday, October 17, 2025, successfully brought together Sri Lankan entrepreneurs and investors to explore business opportunities in Saudi Arabia and the Gulf region.

The high-profile event was conceptualized by First Consultant for Construction Industry in Saudi Arabia, Palitha Rathnapala to strengthen ties and create a strong platform for Sri Lankan business growth in the Gulf.

The event featured the national anthems of Sri Lanka and the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and included the participation of the Ambassador of the Republic of Maldives. The Chief Guest, Dr. Rukshan Razak, Principal of the Sri Lankan School Riyadh, delivered a keynote emphasizing collaboration and innovation.

The presentations highlighted Sri Lanka’s global potential across diverse sectors like logistics, real estate, agriculture, technology, and cosmetics. Over 100 companies expressed interest, but only those with strong business potential were selected.

The prominent participants included: NWC Holdings, Scnawell Logistics, Home Lands (event sponsors) along with Rocell ,Pelawatte Dairy, Cinnomina Exports, Iceman, Art Décor, Ambewella Dairy, Cap Ceylon, Blue Lotus 360 Software Developers, Pintanna Oud, Harischandra, British Cosmetic, Araliya Food, Norfalk, Anods Cocoa, Avikanas Lights, Edinburgh Foods, Unifider, Green Consultant & High Tide, Crystastone, Anods Coco, Gems by Mr. Ajith and NextGen
