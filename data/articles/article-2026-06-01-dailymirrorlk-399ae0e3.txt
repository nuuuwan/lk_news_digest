55 new hostel projects for 16,000 university students

Colombo, June 1 (Daily Mirror) - Fifty-five new hostel projects will be implemented for 16,000 students in state universities across the island, the Higher Education Division of the Ministry of Education, Higher Education and Vocational Education said.

According to the Ministry, the projects are aimed at improving residential facilities for university students and will be carried out in three phases.

The initiative is expected to address accommodation shortages faced by undergraduates and enhance living conditions within state university premises.
