FireCircle by G brings first ESG Communications Workshop to Colombo

Half-day intensive designed to help organisations turn ESG compliance into brand equity

FireCircle by G, a boutique communications consultancy based in Colombo, will host its inaugural ESG Communications Workshop “From Compliance to Brand Equity” on Wednesday, 13 May 2026, at the Galle Face Hotel. The half-day session runs from 2.00 p.m. to 5.30 p.m. and is limited to 25 participants.

The workshop is designed for marketing and brand managers, sustainability and ESG leads, corporate communications teams, and founders who are doing ESG work but struggling to communicate it in ways that build real brand value. It arrives at a timely moment, ahead of World Environment Day, as Sri Lankan organisations face growing pressure from investors, regulators, and consumers to demonstrate credible, consistent sustainability narratives.

FireCircle by G Founder and workshop facilitator Gayani Punchihewa said: “Most organisations in Sri Lanka are already doing the work. But ESG sits in reports while brands stay silent. This workshop is about closing that gap, giving teams the language, the framework, and the confidence to make their impact visible.”

Participants will leave with a practical communications framework they can apply immediately, covering the distinction between marketing communications and impact communications, how to connect ESG, SDGs, and brand narrative into one coherent story, and language that works across boardrooms and social media.
