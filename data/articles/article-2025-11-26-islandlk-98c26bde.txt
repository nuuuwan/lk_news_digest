Sri Lankan pride, global glory: Solar Booze sweeps Service and Performance Excellence Awards

Solar Booze (Pvt) Ltd has emerged as one of Sri Lanka’s most recognized renewable energy companies in 2025, celebrating two prestigious awards on both international and national platforms. At the FusionSolar 4th Global Installer Summit 2025, held in Dongguan, China, the company was honored with the Excellent Performance Award, while on home soil it received the Service Excellence Award for Customer-Centric Renewable Solutions at the Clean Energy Awards 2025.

Commenting on the achievement, Ms. Nadika Herath, Managing Director of Solar Booze, said: “This achievement reflects the hard work of our dedicated team and the trust placed in us by our clients. It also reaffirms our vision of positioning Sri Lanka as a leader in renewable energy innovation.”

The success was not only about recognition but also about responsibility. As Solar Booze continues to grow, the company emphasized its role in inspiring both industry peers and local communities to adopt sustainable practices and accelerate the transition toward renewable energy solutions with the world’s number one products
