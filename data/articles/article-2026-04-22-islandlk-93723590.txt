Glowing Hair…

Yes, why not! We all look for glowing skin but what about glowing hair?

Check these out and you will find your hair shiny … and healthy!

Aloe vera hydrates the scalp and adds natural shine. Apply fresh aloe gel, leave it for 30 minutes, and then rinse with lukewarm water.

Mix 02 tablespoons of coconut oil with 01 tablespoon of honey. Apply to damp hair, leave for 20 minutes, and then wash off. This restores softness and glow.

Whisk one egg with half a cup of yoghurt, apply for 25 minutes, and then rinse with a mild shampoo. Yoghurt nourishes while eggs provide protein.

Choose the right shampoo and conditioner.Avoid harsh, sulfate-based shampoos. Opt for mild, nourishing formulas with natural ingredients, like aloe vera, argan oil, or coconut milk. Always follow up with a conditioner to lock in moisture.

Oil your hair regularly. Coconut oil, almond oil, or olive oil massages, increase blood circulation and strengthen roots. Warm the oil slightly and massage your scalp twice a week.

Protect from heat and sun. Use a heat protectant spray before styling. Cover your hair with a scarf, or hat, when stepping out in harsh sunlight, like what we are experiencing at the moment. UV rays can make hair brittle and lifeless.
