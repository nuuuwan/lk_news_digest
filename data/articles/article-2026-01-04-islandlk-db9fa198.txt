S G Actuarial Consultancy wins national award for management excellence

S G Actuarial Consultancy (Pvt) Limited has been honoured with a Bronze Award in the Support Services category at the National Management Excellence Awards (NMEA) 2025, organised by the Institute of Management of Sri Lanka. Notably, no Gold or Silver awards were presented in this segment, underscoring the significance of the achievement.

The award recognises outstanding operational reliability, innovation in service delivery, and stakeholder-driven performance. The NMEA scheme evaluates organisations based on comprehensive management standards rather than short-term financial results.

Actuaries are experts in risk management, a competency vital across all industries – not just insurance. S G Actuarial provides consultancy and training services to help organisations of any size or sector establish or enhance their risk management frameworks. The firm has expanded its reach considerably in recent years, now serving clients across five countries and delivering training to participants from 116 organisations and educational institutes in 22 countries.

In addition, the company offers introductory courses in actuarial science, designed to prepare students for employment.

“We are committed to delivering quality, customised services that ensure client satisfaction – a principle reflected in the excellent feedback we receive,” stated Saroja Gunatilleke, Managing Director and Actuary of the company. A Fellow of the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries UK, she was the only locally-based professional with this qualification in Sri Lanka when she attained it 13 years ago.
