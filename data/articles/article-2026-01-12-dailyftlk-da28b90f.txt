Book launch – Development: Not By Economics Alone

The Gamani Corea Foundation launched the monograph ‘Development: Not By Economics Alone by Dr. Nimal Sanderatne,’ Emeritus Chairperson of the Foundation, on Friday at Horton Lodge. The event was open with a welcome address by Dr. Harsha Aturupane, followed by an address by Dr. Sanderatne.
