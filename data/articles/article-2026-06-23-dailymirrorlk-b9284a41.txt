MESSI MAKES HISTORY: Argentina Legend Breaks World Cup Goalscoring Record

June.23 (Mirror Sports) - Lionel Messi has become the FIFA World Cup's all-time leading goalscorer after scoring twice in Argentina's 2-0 victory over Austria. The 38-year-old captain raised his tournament tally to 18 goals, surpassing Germany legend Miroslav Klose's long-standing record and helping La Albiceleste secure a place in the Round of 32.

Lionel Messi scores Argentina’s second goal during the World Cup Group J match between Argentina and Austria in Arlington.
