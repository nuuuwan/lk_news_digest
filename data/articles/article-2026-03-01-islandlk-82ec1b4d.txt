Lihini Sea Foods recognised for its contribution to export sector, at NCE Export Awards

Lihini Sea Foods (Pvt) Ltd was awarded the Gold Award in the Extra Large Category – Fisheries & Fishery Products Sector at the 33rd NCE Export Awards 2025, organised by the National Chamber of Exporters of Sri Lanka.

Established in 2004, the company supplies premium seafood to markets across Europe, the US, Asia, Russia, and Oceania. It specialises in high-value tuna, swordfish, and reef fish, processed to international standards.

The company has strengthened its portfolio with a focus on value-added products, including its Seafood Sausage Range, Seafood Ball Range, and breaded items like fish cutlets and rolls. It continues to offer sashimi-grade cuts and customised retail packaging.

Operating with a monthly capacity of 175 metric tons, Lihini Sea Foods holds certifications including BRC “AA” Grade, IFS, and Friend of the Sea. The award recognises its export growth and commitment to quality and sustainability.
