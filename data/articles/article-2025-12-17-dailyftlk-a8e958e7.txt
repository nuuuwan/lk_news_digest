Akbar Brothers wins top honours at Presidential Export Awards 2024/25

Akbar Brothers Director Hatim Akbarally accepting award for Net Foreign Exchange Earner of the Year

Akbar Brothers Director Mansoor Akbarally accepting award for Globally Outreached Sri Lankan Brand of the Year

Akbar Brothers Director Imran Akbarally accepting award for Best Tea Exporter 2024/25

Akbar Brothers Group Chief Financial Officer Devika Dhanapala accepting award for Best Tea Exporter 2024/25-Merit

Akbar Brothers Ltd., has secured four major titles at the 27th Presidential Export Awards 2024/2025, hosted by the Sri Lanka Export Development Board, winning the Overall Award for Net Foreign Exchange Earner of the Year. The company was also honoured with Globally Outreached Sri Lankan Brand of the Year for its “Akbar Brand,” Best Exporter Tea – (Large), and the Best Tea Exporter (Large)-Merit Award.

With this recognition, the company celebrates its 48th Presidential Export Award and maintains its position as Sri Lanka’s Largest Tea Exporter for over 30 consecutive years.

Company officials noted that the recognition underscores Akbar Brothers’ unwavering commitment to quality, Sri Lanka’s export economy and its continued efforts to elevate Ceylon Tea on the global stage. They attributed the company’s achievement to the dedication of its team and to the core values of honesty and integrity instilled by their Founders.
