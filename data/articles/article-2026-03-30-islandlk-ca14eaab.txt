Seychelles … here we come

The group Mirage left for the Seychelles last week for a three-week stint at the Lo Brizan pub/restaurant, Niva Labriz Resort.

This is their fifth trip to the Seychelles and, according to reports coming our way, the band is extremely popular at this particular venue.

“We do six nights a week and we have a very enthusiastic crowd turning up every night which makes our work so enjoyable,” says leader Donald Pieries, who has molded Mirage into a top-notch outfit.

They have two more foreign assignments cropping up, before the year is out, and they are also to the same venue in the Seychelles – Lo Brizan pub/restaurant, Niva Labriz Resort – in August and December.

There is also talk that a trip to Australia may work out for Mirage, before the year ends!

Obviously, their music will be missed by local music lovers but the scene here is a bit dicey with work, for performers, a bit hard to come by.
