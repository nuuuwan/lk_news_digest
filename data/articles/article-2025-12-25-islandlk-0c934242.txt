IMC Campus celebrates graduates at BMICH ceremony

IMC Campus held its Annual Graduation Ceremony at the BMICH on December 8, 2025, recognising over 200 graduating students. The event was graced by Chief Guest Professor Udaya Mohan Devdas from the University of Kelaniya, alongside founder Dr. Kavya Jayakody.

Students received Bachelor’s, Master’s, and Doctoral qualifications. IMC Campus, a partner of IIC University of Technology (Cambodia) and London Management Qualifications (UK), offers programs in management, education, and AI, maintaining a high success rate for working professionals.
