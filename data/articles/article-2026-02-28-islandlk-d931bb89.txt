15th Sri Lankan Textile series set to showcase global innovation

Sri Lanka’s textile and apparel industry will once again converge at the 15th Sri Lankan Edition of the Textile Series of Exhibitions, scheduled from 5–7 March at the Sri Lanka Exhibition & Convention Centre (SLECC), Colombo 10. Organized by CEMS-Global USA, the milestone edition coincides with the 25th anniversary of the group’s globally acclaimed Textile Series, reaffirming its long-standing commitment to Sri Lanka’s apparel sector.

The exhibition will present a fully integrated textile value chain through three co-located shows: the 13th Textech Sri Lanka International Expo, the 15th Colombo International Yarn & Fabric Show, and the 51st DyeChem Sri Lanka International Expo. Together, they provide manufacturers with access to global technology, sourcing alternatives, and strategic partnerships across machinery, materials, chemicals, and innovation.

The event is endorsed by the Sri Lanka Export Development Board, JAAF, SLAEA, and SLCGE, and is expected to strengthen Sri Lanka’s competitiveness and global integration in the rapidly evolving apparel export market.
