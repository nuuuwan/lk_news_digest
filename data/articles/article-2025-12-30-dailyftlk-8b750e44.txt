MAC celebrates 29 years of success and corporate excellence

MAC Holdings Ltd., celebrated its 29th anniversary recently, marking nearly three decades of purposeful growth, integrity-led leadership, and a deep commitment to every stakeholder who has been part of its journey.

The company said it has continuously evolved with changing times while remaining firmly anchored to the corporate philosophy that defines its identity, “We cannot spell success without you.” This enduring belief reflects the company’s conviction that its achievements are inseparable from the contributions of its customers, partners, service providers, employees, and the communities it serves.

“It is this collective strength that has driven MAC to uphold exceptional customer service, strong governance, and unwavering social responsibility throughout its history. Today, MAC stands as a diversified industry leader with operations across freight and logistics, aviation, shipping, hospitality and leisure, renewable energy, fisheries and agriculture, and strategic investments,” the company said in a statement.

Its regional presence extends to Bangladesh, Maldives, UAE, Singapore and agency partnerships in India and Pakistan, strengthening its footprint across key markets in Asia and the Middle East. Guided by its mission to be a multifaceted group of companies recognised for its diversified commercial interests and value-driven offerings, MAC continues to champion innovation, dynamism, and sustainable growth. The company’s values, respect, accountability, trust, integrity, and a strong ESG consciousness remain the pillars that define its corporate culture and inform every strategic decision.

Over the years, MAC has actively engaged with leading business chambers and councils, including the Ceylon Chamber of Commerce, the International Chamber of Commerce, the National Chamber of Commerce, and numerous regional and bilateral business councils. These affiliations strengthen the company’s credibility and reinforce its standing within the international business community. Its commitment to excellence has been recognised through numerous prestigious awards and certifications, including the Global Commerce Excellence Award, the Presidential Export Awards across multiple years, the National Chamber of Exporters Awards, the National Business Excellence Award, and the National Supply Chain Excellence Awards, ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and TRACE etc. Each accolade serves as both recognition and motivation, inspiring the company to raise the bar further and uphold the highest professional standards.

“The 29th anniversary celebration was a moment of gratitude and reflection, marked by religious blessings conducted by leaders representing all major faiths, symbolising unity, harmony, and the inclusive spirit that defines the MAC family,” the Company said.

Managing Director Andre Fernando emphasised the core values that will propel MAC into its next phase of growth. He reminded the team to remain honest, uphold integrity, be selfless in their service, and treat the company as their own, as values that he believes are essential for collective success. He reiterated that MAC thrives because of teamwork and the willingness of people to put the organisation first.

As the company steps confidently into its 30th year, MAC said it remains committed to empowering its people, strengthening global partnerships, embracing innovation, and contributing responsibly to society and the environment. With nearly three decades of progress and a heritage that spans over a century, MAC Holdings Ltd., moves forward with pride, clarity, and the steadfast belief that success is meaningful only when shared, because at MAC, “We cannot spell success without you.”
