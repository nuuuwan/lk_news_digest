Naturub Group of Companies donates Rs. 100 m to ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund

Naturub Group of Companies Chairman Tissa Eleperuma handing over the cheque of Rs. 100 milion to Secretary to the President, Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake, flanked by the Board of Directors of Naturub including Chief Operating Officer Yasith Eleperuma (extreme right)

The Naturub Group of Companies has donated Rs. 100 million towards the Government’s “Rebuilding Sri Lanka” Fund, established to provide relief to those affected by Cyclone Ditwah.

The cheque was handed over to the Secretary to the President, Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake, by Naturub Group of Companies Chairman Tissa Eleperuma at the Presidential Secretariat, in the presence of the board of directors of Naturub Group.

“This donation underlines our deep commitment to the people of Sri Lanka. To help drive the continued recovery, these funds are an investment in a sustainable future that benefits all communities in the country. Therefore supporting sustainable growth and revitalisation of the affected areas, would create a foundation for community prosperity,” said Eleperuma.
