Colombo Shopping Festival 40th edition officially inaugurated

The 40th edition of Colombo Shopping Festival was has inaugurated by, Aitken Spence Travels Managing Director Nalin Jayasundera with the participation of event partners and exhibitors.

Organised by Aitken Spence Conventions and Exhibitions, the Christmas Edition is now taking place at BMICH, Colombo, and will continue until 21 December from 10:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m.

The event is supported by Commercial Bank PLC as the Official Banking Partner, Sarasavi Bookshop and Weerodara Stationery Ltd., as Official Books and Stationery Partners, Ceylon Green Life Plantation Ltd., as the Official Plantation Partner, Browns EV as the Official Automobile Partner, Moose Clothing as the Official Event Partner, and Nestl√© Lanka PLC as the Official Beverage Partner.

This year, the festival invites everyone to shop for a cause. Over 250 local entrepreneurs are taking part, sharing their products and hard work with visitors. Every ticket purchased helps support families affected by recent disasters, making each visit meaningful while supporting local businesses.

The Colombo Shopping Festival offers special discounts, great deals, and exciting raffle draws throughout the event. Visitors can enjoy shopping for fashion, home items, electronics, gifts, and festive essentials, all while enjoying the festive Christmas atmosphere at BMICH.
