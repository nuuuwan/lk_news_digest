Singhagiri makes history with Sri Lanka’s first-ever Hisense 116” RGB MiniLED TV

In a landmark move for the Sri Lankan consumer electronics market, Singhagiri, the nation’s trusted home for premium appliances and electronics and the exclusive distributor of Hisense, proudly announces the pioneering launch of the revolutionary Hisense RGB Mini LED 116 television. This signifies a technological leap forward, establishing Singhagiri as the first and only retailer in Sri Lanka to bring groundbreaking RGB-Mini LED technology directly to consumers.

Beyond the typical specifications, this launch by Singhagiri paves the way for the company to redefine home entertainment by delivering meaningful innovation by Hisense. At the core of this achievement stands the mass-production of the RGB Mini LED 116, a feat first accomplished globally by Hisense. This proprietary technology utilizes individual red, green, and blue Mini-LEDs across thousands of dimming zones, which is a significant advancement over conventional single-color LED backlights.

For the Sri Lankan viewer, this translates into an experience that genuinely stands apart. The benefits are tangible: breathtaking colour purity achieving up to 95% of the cinema-grade BT.2020 colour space, an extraordinary peak brightness of 8,000 nits for dazzling highlights, and a remarkable over 20% reduction in power consumption. This means unparalleled realism, with every sunset appearing vividly true-to-life and every night scene that would appear packed with detail, all while being more energy efficient.

The massive 116-inch display of the RGB Mini-LED TV is designed to transform your living room into a personal theatre, to make you feel the roar of a stadium during a live match, or get lost in the epic sweep of a film, or dive headfirst into immersive gaming. The experience is intelligently powered by the Hi-View AI Engine X, which optimizes picture and sound in real time for any content.

Further distinguishing this masterpiece is its acoustic design, born from a unique collaboration with the iconic Opéra de Paris. The integrated 6.2.2 CineStage X Surround system is crafted to replicate the harmonic richness of a world-class opera house, ensuring the sound matches the spectacular visuals.

“Our mission at Singhagiri has always been to introduce Sri Lankans to the future of technology, not just follow trends,” says Mr. Majeed, Director at Singhagiri (Pvt) Limited. “Launching the country’s first RGB Mini LED TV is a milestone of which we are incredibly proud. It reflects our dedication to providing access to category-defining products that offer real, perceptible benefits to our customers.” He added.

Director Majeed also mentioned that architects will soon begin designing luxury living spaces to accommodate 116” TVs as an upgraded feature of modern home entertainment.

This launch firmly positions the Hisense RGB Mini LED 116 as the new benchmark for ultra-premium home entertainment in Sri Lanka. It is a statement of leadership from Singhagiri, offering an unmatched combination of technological firsts, immersive scale, and intelligent design.
