Minor flood risk warning issued for Kalu Ganga basin areas

Colombo, May 23 (Daily Mirror) - A considerable amount of rainfall has been recorded in the catchment areas of the Kalu Ganga basin as of now, the Irrigation Department said.

Based on the current rainfall situation and rising river water levels observed at hydrological stations along the Kalu Ganga, there is a possibility of a minor flood situation developing in low-lying areas of the Kalu Ganga valley.

The Department said the residents in Ingiriya, Horana, Dodangoda, Millaniya, Kalutara, Bulathsinhala, Madurawala and Palindanuwara District Secretariat Divisions have been advised to remain alert due to the potential risk.

The Department have also urged motorists travelling through these areas to exercise caution, particularly in flood-prone and low-lying sections of the road network.

Disaster management authorities have been requested to take necessary precautionary measures and remain on standby in view of the developing situation.
