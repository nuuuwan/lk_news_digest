VFS Global launches Malta Visa Centre in Sri Lanka

VFS Global and the Malta High Commission in New Delhi have opened a Malta Visa Application Centre in Colombo. Inaugurated on 3 December 2025, the centre will begin processing applications on 1 February 2026.

It offers Sri Lankan citizens a convenient local point to submit Schengen visa applications for travel to Malta, with final assessment remaining with the Malta High Commission in New Delhi.

High Commissioner Reuben Gauci noted the growing number of Sri Lankans travelling to Malta and stated the centre improves access for applicants.

VFS Global’s COO for South Asia, Yummi Talwar, highlighted the company’s trusted partnership with Malta since 2008 and the enhanced, modern facilities the new centre provides.
