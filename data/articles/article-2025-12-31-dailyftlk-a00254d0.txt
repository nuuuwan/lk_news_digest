Container Transport Owners give  Rs. 1.5 m to Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund

The Container Transport Owners Association (CTOA) recently made a financial contribution of Rs.1.5 million to Government’s ‘Rebuilding Sri Lanka’ Fund, established to provide relief and support to communities affected by Cyclone Ditwah.

The contribution was handed over to the Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika

Sanath Kumanayake by CTOA Chairman W. M. S. K. Manjula, Secretary Jayantha Karunadhipathi, and Consultant Dilip Perera in the presence of Ports Minister Aruna Karunatilake.
