SLT-MOBITEL recognized as Sri Lanka’s #1 Best Mobile Network by Ookla®

Reaffirming its commitment to offering superior network experience, SLT-MOBITEL Mobile has been crowned as Sri Lanka’s #1 Best Mobile Network by Ookla®, the global leader in internet performance testing and analytics, at the recently concluded Mobile World Congress 2025. Awarded for the period covering Q3 and Q4 of 2025, the Best Mobile Network Award reflects SLT-MOBITEL’s proven excellence in delivering the Fastest Speeds, Seamless Video Streaming, and a consistently smooth web browsing experience.

In today’s highly competitive telecom industry, independent validation from Ookla® further reaffirms SLT-MOBITEL’s position as the network of choice for Sri Lankans for a quality broadband experience. The latest recognition adds to a consistent record of Ookla-verified excellence. The company was previously honoured as Sri Lanka’s Fastest 4G Network for 2024, and as the Fastest Mobile Network for 2019, 2020, and 2021.

Ookla’s Fastest Mobile Network Award relies on its Speed Score methodology, which evaluates download speeds, upload speeds, and latency. The combined score reflects how networks perform during everyday use, offering a true measure of superior customer experience.
