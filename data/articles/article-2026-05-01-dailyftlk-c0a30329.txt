Aitken Spence Elevators and CINEC empower next generation of experts

Aitken Spence Elevators, in collaboration with CINEC Campus, recently held the Certificate Awarding Ceremony for the latest batch of trainees of the Elevator Serviceman Course at the Elevator Training Centre (ETC).

The graduates were awarded their NVQ Level 3 certifications, marking a significant milestone in their professional journey within Sri Lanka’s vertical transportation industry.

The certification program was developed in partnership with the National Apprentice and Industrial Training Authority (NAITA) and the Vocational Training and Skills Development Ministry, ensuring trainees received industry-recognised qualifications, alongside strong practical competencies. The ceremony was attended by directors of both Aitken Spence and CINEC Campus, as well as staff members, lecturers, and invited guests of the graduating students

Anuka Prashan Pieris, was recognised as the Most Outstanding Student of the batch in appreciation of his exceptional performance and commitment throughout the program. The ETC plays a vital role in addressing the growing demand for skilled elevator service technicians through structured training and hands-on learning. The facility features Sri Lanka’s first Elevator Training Tower, offering trainees practical exposure aligned with both local and international standards.

All graduates of the current batch have already commenced their careers as technicians and supervisors at Aitken Spence Elevators. Their seamless transition into the workforce reflects the program’s strong emphasis on developing job-ready professionals capable of contributing effectively from day one.

With further batches planned in the coming months, the ETC continues to strengthen the nation’s skilled workforce, supporting the long-term growth of Sri Lanka’s vertical transportation industry.

Aitken Spence Elevators is a joint venture between Aitken Spence PLC and OTIS Elevator Company, responsible for the supply, installation and maintenance of OTIS elevators and escalators in Sri Lanka and Maldives for over 35 years. Aitken Spence Elevators is the first, and only elevator company in Sri Lanka to be certified in all four key standards: technical and financial capability, quality management, environmental management, and occupational health and safety—reflecting its strong commitment to quality, safety, and sustainability.
