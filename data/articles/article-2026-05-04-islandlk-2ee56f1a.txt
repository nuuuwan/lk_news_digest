Unforgettable evening … with Rukshan

Known for his incredible range as a composer, multi-instrumentalist, recording artiste, and international performer, Rukshan Perera is working on giving music lovers an unforgettable evening with his unique blend of jazz, world music, and soul.

Yes, from masterful jazz scat to his iconic harmony whistling, his performance, on the evening of Saturday, 21st June, will be both technically brilliant and deeply moving.

The venue will be the Bishop’s College Auditorium and Rukshan will have for support several guest artistes, promising rich collaborations and musical surprises throughout the night.

This concert is being held in aid of RCU Loyalty Pledge Scholarships for students and that means while you enjoy an action-packed evening, you will also be supporting a great cause.

What’s more, those who turn up to see one of Sri Lanka’s most versatile and celebrated musicians in action will also get a chance to take home Rukshan’s CD and two Live DVDs!
