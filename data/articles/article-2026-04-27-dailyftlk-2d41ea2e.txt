EMPL leads multi-city logistics operations at Pakistan Lifestyle Exhibition in SL

Expert Maritime Ltd., (EMPL) has been appointed as the Official Logistics Partner of Pakistan Lifestyle Exhibition 2026 in Sri Lanka, strengthening bilateral trade ties.

Bringing together a diverse portfolio of Pakistani brands and manufacturers, the exhibition will  showcase products across sectors including bridal and wedding, fashion and apparel, jewellery and gems, textiles and fabrics, furniture and home décor, food and specialty products, and tiles and home solutions.

The multi-city showcase spanning key commercial regions across Sri Lanka began on 21 April, starting from Beruwala and moving to Kandy, where the show began on Saturday and will continue until 28 April at Royal Kandyan. The exercise will conclude in Kalmunai (Sainthamaruthu) from 1-4 May at the Lee Meridian Hall.

With the exhibition moving across multiple cities within a short timeframe, efficient coordination of cargo becomes critical. EMPL will oversee end-to-end logistics, including international freight forwarding, customs clearance, and timely delivery of exhibition goods to each venue.  As regional trade continues to expand, logistics plays a central role in enabling smooth cross-border business activity. EMPL’s involvement ensures that exhibitors can transport goods seamlessly from origin to exhibition floor, regardless of location.

EMPL Managing Director Shakir Azeez said: “We are proud to be the Official Logistics Partner for the Pakistan Lifestyle Exhibition 2026. Managing a multi-city exhibition of this scale requires precision, coordination, and reliability. At EMPL, we are committed to delivering seamless logistics solutions that support exhibitors at every stage, enabling them to focus on building meaningful business connections and expanding into new markets.”

By taking the exhibition across Beruwala, Kandy, and Kalmunai, the event provides exhibitors with access to diverse regional markets, strengthening visibility and engagement beyond a single location.

As the official logistics provider, EMPL will deliver a full suite of services, including air and sea freight forwarding, Customs clearance, door-to-venue delivery, temporary imports, on-site coordination, and return shipment management.

By ensuring reliable and efficient logistics throughout all three exhibition legs, EMPL plays a pivotal role in the overall success of the event, reinforcing its position as a trusted partner in exhibition and event logistics.
