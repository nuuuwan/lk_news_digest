Nations Trust Bank partners with Colombo Fashion Week’s Luxury Edit

Nations Trust Bank PLC (NTB) has renewed its partnership with Colombo Fashion Week’s (CFW) Luxury Edit: Couture + Trousseau 2025 as the Official Fashion Card, marking the ninth consecutive year of collaboration. The partnership underscores the Bank’s commitment to curating exceptional lifestyle experiences for its Cardmembers.

This year’s collaboration offers select Nations Trust Bank American Express® Cardmembers complementary access to attend the CFW event on 22nd & 23rd of November 2025. The event will feature curated exhibits of destination-wedding apparel, occasion wear, and bridal fashion offering an inspiring platform for South Asia’s leading designers to showcase their work.

Speaking on the partnership, Niluka Gunatilake, Head of Cards at Nations Trust Bank PLC said, “For nearly a decade, Nations Trust Bank has been a proud partner of Colombo Fashion Week, a platform that continues to give prominence to global fashion trends showcased in Sri Lanka. Our partnership with the Luxury Edit: Couture + Trousseau allows us to cater to the passions and lifestyles of our Cardmembers, who value the artistry and craftsmanship behind high fashion.”
