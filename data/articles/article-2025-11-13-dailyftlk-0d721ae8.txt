The 2025 celebration underscored ISMM’s ongoing mission to promote excellence, innovation, and professionalism in Sri Lanka’s supply chain ecosystem. It also reaffirmed the importance of the supply chain sector as a driving force of national competitiveness, sustainability, and economic growth.
