Nawaloka Hospitals Colombo Marks Four Decades of Responsible, Patient Centered Healthcare

Colombo , For more than four decades, Nawaloka Hospitals PLC has played a vital role in Sri Lanka’s healthcare landscape, guided by a steadfast commitment to safe, ethical, and patient-centred care. Growing in step with the nation’s evolving healthcare needs, the institution has consistently worked to uphold clinical standards aligned with international best practice anchored in professional accountability, transparency, and public trust.

Nawaloka Hospitals delivers comprehensive healthcare services through a multidisciplinary, team-based model led by experienced medical professionals across a wide range of specialties. This coordinated approach ensures continuity of care, sound clinical decision-making, and patient safety throughout every stage of treatment.

In recent years, the Hospital has strengthened its clinical capabilities through the progressive integration of advanced and AI-supported medical technologies. These systems enhance diagnostics, laboratory processes, and clinical workflows supporting healthcare professionals, improving efficiency, and ensuring greater consistency and accuracy in patient outcomes.

Commenting on the institution’s journey, Dr. Jayantha Dharmadasa, Chairman of Nawaloka Hospitals PLC, said “For over 40 years, Nawaloka Hospitals has been guided by a simple but powerful principle responsibility to our patients and to the nation’s healthcare system. Our focus has always been on ethical practice, clinical excellence, and continuous improvement. As medicine advances, we remain committed to adopting innovation responsibly, while preserving the trust placed in us by generations of Sri Lankans.”

Throughout its history, Nawaloka Hospitals has emphasised responsible growth balancing technological advancement with ethical governance and patient welfare. As Sri Lanka’s healthcare sector continues to evolve, the institution remains firmly focused on dependable service delivery and long-term sustainability.
