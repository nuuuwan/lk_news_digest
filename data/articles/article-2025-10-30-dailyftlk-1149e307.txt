Lawyer arrested for aiding ‘Ganemulla Sanjeewa’ murder suspect

Police have arrested a female lawyer on charges of aiding and abetting the murder of underworld figure ‘Ganemulla Sanjeewa’.

Police said the suspect, who is a practicing Attorney-at-Law, is accused of assisting Ishara Sewwandi, a key suspect in the killing by providing her with a forged lawyer’s identity card, the copy of the Penal Code with the pistol concealed inside to the shooter.

Investigations revealed that the fake ID was used to mislead authorities and facilitate the suspect’s movements.
