Binance launches initiative to boost blockchain education in Sri Lankan universities

Binance has launched ChainCeylon 2026, a flagship education program designed to promote blockchain literacy and responsible crypto adoption across Sri Lankan universities.

The initiative began with workshops at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, University of Kelaniya, Wayamba University, and University of Moratuwa, engaging 265 students in sessions on on-chain analysis, blockchain applications, and digital asset fundamentals. The curriculum emphasizes security, regulatory compliance, and prudent participation in the Web3 ecosystem.

“ChainCeylon 2026 reflects our commitment to cultivating a grassroots Web3 ecosystem by engaging directly with the next generation,” said a Binance representative. The program aims to develop a pipeline of knowledgeable leaders who will promote best practices in crypto adoption, aligning with Sri Lanka’s ambition to become a regional hub for blockchain innovation grounded in education and responsibility.
