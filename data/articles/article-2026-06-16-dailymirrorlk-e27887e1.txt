People’s Bank relocates Rajina Junction Service Centre

The People’s Bank Rajina Junction Service Centre was ceremoniously opened at its new and upgraded premises recently.

The relocated service centre, operating under the People’s Bank Thambuttegama branch, now offers the customers in the  area enhanced access to the latest digital banking products, facilities and services.

The opening ceremony was attended by Regional Manager Anuradhapura Tissa Tennakoon, Assistant Regional Managers I.S. Kirindegedara, S.P.K. Ekanayake and T.M.M.H. Thennakoon, Thambuttegama Branch Manager S. Akilan and Rajina Junction Service Centre Manager B.M.A.S. Bandara, together with the members of the bank staff and valued customers.
