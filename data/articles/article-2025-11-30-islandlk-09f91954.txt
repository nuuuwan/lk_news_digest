Sri Lanka Insurance honoured at National Supply Chain Excellence Awards 2025

Sri Lanka Insurance (SLIC) has been honoured with a Commendation Award for ‘Best Supply Chain Practicing Organization – Service Category’ at the National Supply Chain Excellence Awards 2025.

The prestigious award, organized by the Institute of Supply and Materials Management (ISMM), recognises SLIC’s exemplary performance in procurement management. The company was selected from 24 nominated organisations for its strong governance, transparency, and adherence to national procurement guidelines.

Chairman Nusith Kumaratunga commented, “This recognition underscores our commitment to the highest standards of integrity and efficiency. We continue to strengthen our procurement processes through accountability and digitisation, ensuring public trust remains at the heart of what we do.”

As the nation’s largest insurer, SLIC has embedded procurement excellence into its corporate strategy, modernising processes with digital tools to promote value creation and sustainability. This commendation reinforces SLIC’s standing as a benchmark for responsible corporate governance in Sri Lanka.
