Royal Nursing Home’s LKR 3 million investment powers major community camp

With an investment of nearly LKR 3 million, Royal Nursing Home (Pvt) Ltd. demonstrated a powerful commitment to community welfare as the Platinum Sponsor of a large-scale medical and legal service camp held on March 28 at Dharmasoka College, Ambalangoda.

The initiative, organized by the Ceylon Excellence Lions Club of Colombo in partnership with the STEMI Forum, Sri Lanka Medical Association, and the Dharmasoka College Old Boys’ Association, served over 500 individuals. A team of consultant physicians and surgeons provided medical care, while President’s Counsel Kaushalya Navaratne, former President of the Bar Association of Sri Lanka, led legal aid services.

Beyond the day’s consultations, the project also distributed wheelchairs and sports equipment.

Manuja Hewawasam, Chairman of Royal Nursing Home and Senior Vice President of the Club, attended as Guest of Honour alongside District Governor Mahesh Boralgoda. The event was chaired by Club President Ayesh de Silva.
