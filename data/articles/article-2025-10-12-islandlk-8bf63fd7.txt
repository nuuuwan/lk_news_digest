DSI Tyres’ e-commerce wins four awards at SLIM DIGIS 2.5

Sri Lanka’s pioneering tyre e-commerce platform, dsityreshop.com, backed by the trusted strength of DSI Tyres, was honored with four awards at the prestigious SLIM DIGIS 2.5 Awards. These recognitions spotlight the platform’s innovative Pick-Up from Dealer model and data-driven, performance-focused campaigns.

At SLIM DIGIS 2.5, dsityreshop.com proudly secured two Category Best Awards (Silver):

• Best use of Digital in a Marketing Campaign for Automotive

Through this dual fulfilment system, customers can purchase genuine DSI Tyres and Hankook tyres online, with the choice of secure home delivery or pick-up from dealer via DSI Tyres’ trusted dealer network.

Commenting on this success, Kavinda Rajapaksa, Managing Director of Samson Rubber Industries, said:

“We are proud to see dsityreshop.com recognized with four awards at SLIM DIGIS 2.5. This achievement reflects our vision of transforming the tyre-buying journey in Sri Lanka through digital-first innovation.”
