Centrum Marine facilitates high value ALAM scholarships

Centrum Marine Consultancy (Pvt) Ltd., a leader in Marine HR solutions in Sri Lanka, announced a major milestone in the country’s maritime education landscape as five Sri Lankan students have secured the prestigious fully funded scholarship from Akademi Laut Malaysia (ALAM), Southeast Asia’s leading maritime academy operated under the MISC Group a global leader in energy-related (oil & gas) maritime solutions with Its main shareholder being Petroliam Nasional Berhad (Petronas). This achievement reflects a breakthrough collaboration between Sri Lanka and Malaysia and marks an important step in strengthening the future of Sri Lankan youth aspiring to join the global maritime workforce.

The accomplishment follows Centrum Marine’s appointment as the official student recruitment partner for ALAM in Sri Lanka, positioning the company at the forefront of facilitating international opportunities for aspiring seafarers.
