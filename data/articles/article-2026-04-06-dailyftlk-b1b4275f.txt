Vauxhall DSTRCT by John Keells Properties sets new benchmark

Colombo’s real estate market witnessed a remarkable milestone as John Keells Properties (JKP) announced that its latest residential development, Vauxhall DSTRCT, achieved over 200 apartment sales within just two weeks of launch, setting a new benchmark for demand in the metropolitan residential segment.

Strategically located in the heart of Colombo along Vauxhall Street, Vauxhall DSTRCT has captured strong interest from both local and international investors, reaffirming growing confidence in Sri Lanka’s urban residential market and the long-term value of centrally located developments, while also reflecting the strong trust placed in JKP as the country’s premier real estate developer.

John Keells Holdings PLC Vice President and Head of Sales and Marketing – Property Group Nadeem Shums said: “The response to Vauxhall DSTRCT has been exceptional and clearly reflects a shift in buyer preference towards well-located, thoughtfully designed developments that offer both lifestyle convenience and strong investment potential. This momentum is a strong validation of the product, pricing, and overall proposition we have brought to market.”

Spanning approximately 1.5 acres in one of Colombo’s most connected locations, the development will feature a striking 60-storey residential tower comprising 749 apartments, offering a mix of one-, two-, three-, and four-bedroom units. Designed to deliver the most attractively priced apartments in the heart of Colombo, Vauxhall DSTRCT combines accessibility, smart living, and a comprehensive range of lifestyle amenities.

Building on the success of its previous high-rise developments in the metropolitan segment, JKP continues to lead innovation in the sector through efficient design, convenient locations, and smart technology-enabled living. Residents at Vauxhall DSTRCT will benefit from the seamless integration of digital solutions.

With over 200 apartments now sold, Vauxhall DSTRCT continues to gain momentum as one of Colombo’s most in-demand new residential developments, reinforcing JKP’s track record of delivering projects that combine strong location fundamentals with long-term value.

Over the past two decades, JKP has played a defining role in transforming Colombo’s skyline and shaping Sri Lanka’s modern residential landscape, delivering some of the country’s most iconic developments including Emperor and Monarch at Crescat City, OnThree20, 7th Sense, TRI-ZEN, City of Dreams Sri Lanka – Cinnamon Life, and VIMAN, alongside exclusive real estate offerings at the Victoria Golf Resort in Kandy.

Through these landmark projects, the company has consistently introduced new benchmarks in design, lifestyle integration and contemporary urban living, setting the standard for premium residential developments in Sri Lanka.
