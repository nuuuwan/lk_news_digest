Access Motors unveils new Range Rover Boutique Residency in Colombo

The opening of the exclusive Range Rover pop-up at ITC Ratnadipa Colombo One Workplaces (from left): Access Motors Managing Director Theo Fernando, British High Commissioner Andrew Patrick, Access Group Chairman Sumal Perera, Jaguar Land Rover Managing Director – Asia Pacific Region Garth Turnbull, and former All Blacks Captain Kieran Read – Pic by Daminda Harsha Perera

Strategically located in Colombo at ITC Ratnadipa Colombo One Workplaces

Marks a milestone in Range Rover’s brand strategy and luxury client engagement

Access Motors has unveiled a new Range Rover Boutique Residency at ITC Ratnadipa Colombo One Workplaces — a carefully composed, immersive environment designed as a physical expression of the brand’s modern luxury ethos. Defined by reduction and materiality, the space brings clients closer to the world of Range Rover through considered architecture, curated design elements and a calm, contemporary spatial language.

Building on this philosophy, the boutique offers a more intimate, highly personalised client journey. The latest Range Rover vehicles are presented within a refined, contemporary setting, where curated configurations, bespoke options and a dedicated lifestyle selection are explored through a consultative, onetoone approach led by trained specialists.

Range Rover is one of the world’s leading British luxury brands, sold in 121 countries. It belongs to the JLR house of brands together with Defender, Discovery and Jaguar.
