ComBank Cards offer up to 70% Avurudu discounts across the widest range of outlets island-wide

The Commercial Bank of Ceylon has launched its much-anticipated seasonal promotion for the Sinhala and Tamil New Year, offering credit and debit cardholders discounts of up to 70% across an extensive network of merchant outlets island-wide in the run-up to the Avurudu celebrations.

Running up to 30th April 2026, the annual promotion, one of the biggest highlights of the Bank’s cards calendar, brings together more than 579 merchant partners representing over 4,000 retail outlets, providing Commercial Bank cardholders with a wide array of savings across multiple lifestyle categories during the festive season. As one of the Bank’s most significant and welcomed annual campaigns, this promotion embodies Commercial Bank’s commitment to spreading the spirit of Avurudu by bringing families and communities closer together, enabling customers to celebrate the season with greater joy, ease and generosity, while sharing in the happiness of meaningful savings across all aspects of their festive preparations.
