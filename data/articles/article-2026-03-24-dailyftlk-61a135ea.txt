Cenmetrix achieves ISO 9001:2015 certification

From left: Chittananda Kulatilake, Imran Ismail, Roshani Perera, Dewmi Gamage, Deshika Kushan and Vipula Chaminda

Cenmetrix recently achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification, the world’s most widely recognised standard for Quality Management Systems (QMS). This significant milestone reflects the company’s strong commitment to quality assurance, continuous improvement, and operational excellence across its technology solutions and services.

Following the successful completion of all certification requirements and full conformity with ISO 9001:2015 standards, the official certificate was formally awarded to Cenmetrix in February 2026, marking an important step in the company’s long-term quality and governance strategy.

The company’s ISO journey began with a strategic decision to engage expert guidance to strengthen and transform its internal processes. Win Win Consultants Ltd. was appointed to introduce ISO best practices, define organisational quality objectives, and support process improvements, while TQCS International (Group) Ltd. was selected as the certification body to facilitate a structured and comprehensive certification process.

As part of the implementation process, organisation-wide awareness programmes were conducted covering ISO fundamentals, quality management principles, and workplace safety practices. These included fire safety training, first aid training, and occupational health and safety awareness sessions to reinforce a culture of quality, responsibility, and continuous improvement.

Under the leadership of the ISO implementation team, headed by Dewmi Gamage and Deshika Kushan, Cenmetrix established key QMS foundations, including clearly defined organisational structures, process maps, workflow documentation, standard operating procedures, and performance monitoring mechanisms.

“Achieving ISO 9001:2015 certification is a significant milestone that strengthens the trust our clients place in us. This certification demonstrates our commitment to delivering consistent, high-quality technology solutions through well-defined processes, strong governance, and a culture of continuous improvement. It also reinforces our readiness to scale internationally while maintaining the highest quality standards,” said Cenmetrix Assistant Accountant Dewmi Gamage.

This achievement further strengthens Cenmetrix’s position as a trusted technology solutions provider and reflects its ongoing investment in process excellence, customer satisfaction, and sustainable business growth.
