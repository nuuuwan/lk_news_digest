Ganesh Vinayagasundaram, who is a well-known and popular entrepreneur & businessmen in the Colombo imports and trade market was sworn in as the president of the Essential Food Commodities, Importers and Traders Association recently at Kingsbury Hotel, Galle Face.

This occasion was graced by Wasantha Samarasinghe, Minister of Trade, Commerce, Food Security & Corporative Development as Chief Guest.

Ganesh has a wealth of knowledge and experience in imports and trade. He has assured to improve and promote food commodity imports and trade with the assistance of his committee which has been elected for the ensuing year.

Ganesh is the Vice President of the Automobile Association of Ceylon and also holds a responsible portfolio as Sectional Chairman, Accounts & Revenue Management.
