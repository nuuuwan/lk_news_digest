Singer expands luxury footprint with grand unveiling of ‘Signature by Singer’ at One Galle Face

Singer Sri Lanka PLC has announced the launch of Signature by Singer, a premium home and living experience at the Singer Flagship Store, One Galle Face Mall. The new showroom marks a significant expansion of Singer’s mission to deliver global-standard, design-driven home solutions to Sri Lankan consumers.

Developed in partnership with the Signature Group, Malaysia’s largest kitchen cabinet and wardrobe manufacturer with nearly three decades of industry leadership, the showroom brings together contemporary design, luxury craftsmanship and complete lifestyle concepts under one roof. The space has been curated to offer an immersive environment where customers can experience the look and feel of refined living, enhanced by Singer’s trusted service excellence and longstanding reputation for quality.

Singer Sri Lanka PLC Group Managing Director Mahesh Wijewardene said, “At Singer, our vision is to enhance the quality of home living for every Sri Lankan. Our partnership with Signature marks another important step in that journey. The new showroom at One Galle Face is designed to inspire; offering complete living concepts that reflect luxury, elegance and modern functionality. We are proud to bring international-standard craftsmanship to Sri Lanka, backed by Singer’s trusted service, nationwide reach and long-term commitment to our customers.”

Set within one of Colombo’s most prestigious retail destinations, the Signature by Singer showroom presents a seamless journey through modern kitchens, sophisticated wardrobe systems, elegant bedroom settings, integrated home-office solutions and refined bathroom vanities. Each space has been designed to harmonise functionality with aesthetic appeal, offering homeowners, architects and interior designers a clear vision of elevated living. Complementing these environments are thoughtfully selected sofas and seating solutions that blend effortlessly into contemporary interiors, enabling a cohesive and luxurious living experience.

The opening ceremony brought together the senior management from Singer and the Signature Group, alongside interior designers, industry professionals and distinguished guests. Their presence underscored the growing demand for curated, international-standard home concepts within the country.

With the unveiling of this exclusive showroom, Singer strengthens its position as a total home-solutions provider, making premium global design more accessible to discerning Sri Lankan customers. The Signature by Singer showroom at the Singer Flagship Store, One Galle Face Mall, is now open daily during mall hours.
