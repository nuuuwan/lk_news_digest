Global Biz Connect 2026: Positioning Sri Lanka at centre of international business

Colombo will host one of the country’s most anticipated international business gatherings on 3 March 2026, as Global Biz Connect 2026 takes place at Cinnamon Life from 7 p.m. onwards.

Jointly organised by JCI Colombo Mid Town and the Old Nalandians’ Association of Australia, this landmark initiative aligns with the broader vision of “Rebuild Sri Lanka” — a strategic movement focused on driving economic revival, strengthening global partnerships, and positioning Sri Lanka as a competitive international business destination.

Global Biz Connect 2026 is designed as a high-level platform bringing together diplomatic missions, trade bodies, Government representatives, multinational corporations, investors, SME leaders, and entrepreneurs. The objective is clear: to create direct access between credible Sri Lankan enterprises and international markets, enabling cross-border trade, investment, and long-term commercial partnerships.

The evening will feature a commendable and diverse panel representing a wide spectrum of industries including value-added tea, confectionery, hospitality, finance and capital markets, the Sri Lankan diaspora ecosystem, medical tourism, human resources, spirits and beer, fashion, entertainment, and more. These conversations are curated to provide insight, market intelligence, and practical perspectives on scaling businesses beyond borders.

Beyond the panel discussions, the event promises refined fellowship and curated entertainment, creating an engaging atmosphere where the most meaningful networking naturally unfolds. With over 100 distinguished business leaders and professionals from Sri Lanka, Australia, and beyond expected to attend, the platform offers direct access to decision-makers, investors, and industry influencers under one roof.

For businesses seeking partnerships, investors exploring emerging opportunities, and leaders looking to expand their global footprint, Global Biz Connect 2026 presents a timely and strategic opportunity to connect, collaborate, and capitalise.
