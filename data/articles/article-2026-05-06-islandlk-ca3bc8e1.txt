Exfoliation with Natural Scrubs

Thought of giving my readers something different this week – exfoliation with natural scrubs.

Gently massage in circular motions for 02–03 minutes, and then rinse with lukewarm water.

Massage hair and scalp with argan, olive, or black seed oil.

Leave for 30 minutes, or overnight, and then wash with a gentle shampoo.

You can do it with Honey and Yoghurt: Moisturizing and brightening, and also with Turmeric and Milk: Reduces blemishes and inflammation.

Mix the ingredients (either honey and yoghurt or turmeric and milk) to a paste and apply evenly.

Leave for 10–15 minutes, and then rinse gently with warm water.

Include dates, almonds, honey, figs, and olive oil in your diet.

Drink plenty of water and herbal teas (like chamomile or sage).
