Cap Snap Lanka secures six international certifications in six months

Cap Snap Lanka Pvt. Ltd., a member of the CSL Group, has been awarded six internationally recognised certifications within a six-month period, including the Food Safety System Certification (FSSC 22000).

The certifications, presented by Bureau Veritas Sri Lanka, include ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, GMP, HACCP, ISO 22000:2018, and FSSC 22000 Version 6. The achievement underscores the company’s commitment to quality, safety, and operational excellence.

At a ceremony in Colombo, Appreciation Awards were presented to 14 departments, while Internal Auditor Excellence Awards recognised staff for ISO-FSSC compliance.

Established in 2003, Cap Snap Lanka is a leading plastic packaging manufacturer with two production plants employing over 1,000 people. The company supports more than 250 brands across Sri Lanka, producing PET bottles, caps, preforms, and other rigid packaging solutions for industries including pharmaceuticals, dairy, and beverages.
