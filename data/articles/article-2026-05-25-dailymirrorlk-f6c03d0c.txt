Kandy Myst by Cinnamon partners with Kapruka to bring its signature cakes across the Kandy region

Kandy’s evolving hospitality and lifestyle landscape is set to become even more convenient and connected as Kandy Myst by Cinnamon officially partners with Kapruka to offer signature cake varieties from Terra Lounge through Kapruka’s e-commerce platform across the Kandy region.

Under the partnership, Kapruka will enable Kandy Myst by Cinnamon to list and showcase its signature cakes online, making premium cake creations more accessible for celebrations, gifting occasions, and special moments.

The delivery service will initially be available across Kandy, Katugastota, Kundasale, Digana, Peradeniya, Akurana, and Matale, extending the hotel’s culinary offerings to a wider audience across the region, particularly during festive seasons and celebratory occasions.

Kapruka, recognized as one of Sri Lanka’s pioneering e-commerce and online gifting platforms, will facilitate the ordering and delivery process, ensuring a seamless and convenient customer experience.

The collaboration further reinforces Kandy Myst by Cinnamon’s positioning as a contemporary lifestyle-driven hotel experience in Kandy, blending modern hospitality with accessible culinary experiences tailored for today’s digitally connected consumers.

Complementing this vision, Terra Lounge is set to evolve as a vibrant social hub for the Kandy crowd to connect, unwind, and lounge, offering a modern and inviting space within the city.
