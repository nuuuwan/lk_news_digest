HNB launches ‘Happiness Cards’ campaign with Avurudu Savings

HNB PLC has unveiled its ‘Happiness Cards’ campaign, offering seasonal savings and flexible payment options just ahead of the festive season.

The initiative provides cardholders with up to 70% savings, zero-interest instalment plans spanning up to 48 months, and benefits across over 300 merchant partners in categories including shopping, travel, dining, and electronics.

HNB Vice President Upul Adikari said the card is positioned as an enabler of experiences, while Vice President Gauthami Niranjan noted the offering is tailored to seasonal spending patterns. Premium Visa cardholders also stand a chance to win a tour to the FIFA World Cup Finals 2026.
