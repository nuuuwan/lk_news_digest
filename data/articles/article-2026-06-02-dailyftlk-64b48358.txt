Porsche Club of Sri Lanka hosts exclusive movie night for members, families

The Porsche Club of Sri Lanka recently organised an exclusive movie night for its members and their families, bringing together Porsche enthusiasts for an evening of entertainment, fellowship, and camaraderie.

The event featured a private screening of the highly anticipated movie Michael at the luxurious Luxe Cinema at One Galle Face, Colombo. In a unique experience reserved exclusively for Club members and their guests, the entire cinema was booked for the evening, creating a memorable and intimate setting for Porsche owners and their families.

Following the movie screening, members gathered for cocktails, fellowship, and dinner at Central, Shangri-La Colombo. The evening provided an excellent opportunity for members to strengthen friendships, share experiences, and celebrate their common passion for the Porsche brand in a relaxed and elegant atmosphere.

Speaking on the occasion, Porsche Club of Sri Lanka President Prithiv S. Dorai expressed appreciation for all members and families who participated, making the event a tremendous success. The evening reflected the Club’s ongoing commitment to creating meaningful social experiences and fostering a vibrant community of like-minded individuals.

The event was generously hosted and sponsored by Softlogic Stockbrokers (SSB).

The Porsche Club of Sri Lanka extends its sincere gratitude to SSB for their generous sponsorship and continued support in bringing members together through memorable experiences and exceptional events.
