People’s Bank launches “People’s Remittance – Kalin Avurudu Vaasi Draw 2026’’

People’s Bank is offering valuable gifts through People’s Bank “People’s Remittance – Kalin Avurudu Vaasi Draw 2026”. This special opportunity allows customers who receive foreign remittances into their People’s Bank account before 31 March 2026 to win valuable gift packs in celebration of the upcoming Sinhala and Tamil New Year.

For this draw, all account holders who receive worker’s remittances into their People’s Bank account via People’s Remittance from 1 January to 31 March 2026 will be eligible. Fifty lucky winners will be selected, each receiving a gift packs worth Rs. 25,000 containing essential and valuable goods.

In addition, customers who receive worker’s remittances through People’s Remittance will have the chance to win weekly cash prizes worth Rs. 100,000 through the “People’s Remittance Vaasi Kotiyai 2026” draw. Furthermore, the “Dolosmahe Vaasi Chakraya” program offers multiple chances to win prizes every month. This initiative aims not only to strengthen the country’s economy but also to recognise and encourage Sri Lankan foreign workers who, with unwavering courage and dedication, support and sustain the well-being of themselves and their families, the bank said in a statement.
