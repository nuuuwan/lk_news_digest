Rocking scene … with Chitral

It’s certainly going to be a rocking scene, in Negombo, on Saturday, 22nd November.

In action at ‘Santana Bloom,’ to be held at Grandeeza Hotel, will be the rock star himself, Chitral Somapala, and one can be assured of star-class entertainment, with vocals to match.

Along with Chitral will be the band Hot Chocolate and singer/actress Raini.

‘Santana Bloom’ is a fund-raiser for the renovation of St. Anne’s Church Kurana, in Negombo.

Music lovers could obtain more details by calling Nimesh on 0717505197 or Shenal on 0771230967.

‘Santana Bloom’ will boom into action from 8.00 pm onwards, so make sure you are there to take in this awesome scene.
