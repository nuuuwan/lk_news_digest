Hasitha Leanage and Hugh Terry as Independent Non Executive Directors

Janashakthi Insurance PLC has appointed Hasitha Leanage and Hugh Terry as Independent Non‑Executive Directors to the Board, with effect from 20th April 2026.

Hasitha Leanage is a corporate finance professional with an actuarial background and more than 15 years of experience in life insurance, strategic advisory and capital markets.

Hugh Terry is a senior insurance professional with over 30 years of global industry experience, specialising in digital transformation, innovation, and business development. He is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of The Digital Insurer, a global platform supporting insurance professionals and organisations in navigating technology‑driven change.
