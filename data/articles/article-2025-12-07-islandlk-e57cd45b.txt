Three Sinha Industries awarded Gold at CNCI Awards 2025

Three Sinha Industries (Pvt) Ltd., has been awarded the Gold Award in the Extra-Large Category at the CNCI Achievers Awards 2025. Organized by the Ceylon National Chamber of Industries (CNCI) in collaboration with the Ministry of Industry and Entrepreneurship Development, the ceremony was held at Cinnamon Life, Colombo, recognizing outstanding industrial excellence across Sri Lanka.

Founded as a small-scale enterprise, Three Sinha Industries has grown into one of the country’s most trusted and recognized names in the construction and engineering sector. Under the leadership of Manjula Ariyakumara, the company has achieved both local and international recognition for its strong commitment to quality, innovation, and reliability. Over the years, it has earned multiple certifications for compliance with globally accepted standards and has received numerous awards for its contribution to advancing Sri Lanka’s modern infrastructure landscape.
