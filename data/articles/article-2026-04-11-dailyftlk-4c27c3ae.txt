SLIA award for Sri Lanka’s first Three Sinha Clean Room Door, turning a new page in healthcare and laboratory sectors

Three Sinha Industries Ltd. Managing Director Manjula Ariyakumara (left) receiving the award

Three Sinha Industries Ltd. has been honoured with the Product Award of Merit at the Sri Lanka Institute of Architects (SLIA) Annual Sessions 2026, recognising the company’s excellence in locally manufactured construction products. The award was presented under the category of “Locally Made Products and Materials using Local and Imported Materials and Equipment” for the company’s innovative Clean Room Door solution. This recognition highlights Three Sinha Industries’ commitment to quality manufacturing, technical precision, and contribution to Sri Lanka’s architectural and construction sectors. The SLIA Product Awards are regarded as one of the most prestigious recognitions in the built environment industry, celebrating innovation, sustainability, and excellence in materials and product development. Receiving this award places Three Sinha Industries among a select group of manufacturers that meet the highest standards of performance and design.

Three Sinha Industries’ Clean Room Door has been designed to meet stringent industry requirements, ensuring durability, hygiene control, and efficiency in specialised environments such as healthcare facilities, laboratories, and industrial clean rooms. The product reflects the company’s ability to integrate both locally sourced and imported materials with advanced manufacturing techniques. Speaking on the achievement, the company expressed pride in contributing to the growth of Sri Lanka’s local manufacturing sector while maintaining international standards. The recognition further reinforces its mission to deliver reliable, high-quality solutions tailored to modern construction needs. The award was officially presented on 27 February 2026, marking another significant milestone in the company’s journey of innovation and excellence.
