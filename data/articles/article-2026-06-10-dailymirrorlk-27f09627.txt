First Capital named SL’s Leading Investment Banking Firm by World Economic Magazine Awards 2026

First Capital Holdings PLC has been recognised as the Leading Investment Banking Firm in Sri Lanka – 2026 at the World Economic Magazine Awards.

Presented by the World Economic Magazine, the award honours organisations that demonstrate outstanding strategic impact while contributing meaningfully to economic progress and industry advancement. The awards programme recognises distinguished institutions across sectors including banking, financial services, investment, fintech, governance and sustainability.

Recipients are selected through an independent evaluation conducted by the magazine’s research and editorial team, based on key criteria such as company performance and growth indicators, market presence and industry impact, as well as leadership, innovation and strategic initiatives. This rigorous assessment ensures that recognised organisations exemplify excellence, sustainable growth and industry leadership.

This latest international accolade reflects First Capital’s continued leadership in Sri Lanka’s capital markets and its commitment to delivering innovative financial solutions, trusted advisory services and exceptional value to clients. The recognition further reinforces the company’s position as one of the most decorated investment firms in the country, with a proven track record of excellence, transparency and performance across its diverse business operations.

Commenting on the achievement, Dilshan Wirasekara, Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer of First Capital Holdings PLC, said, “Being recognised as the Leading Investment Banking Firm in Sri Lanka by the World Economic Magazine Awards is a significant milestone for First Capital and a testament to the strength of our business model, the trust placed in us by our clients and the dedication of our team. This recognition reflects our commitment to delivering market-leading financial solutions, driving innovation across our operations and contributing meaningfully to the development of Sri Lanka’s capital markets. As we continue our growth journey, we remain focused on creating sustainable value for all stakeholders while supporting the broader economic progress of the nation.”

Over the years, First Capital has consistently played a pivotal role in shaping Sri Lanka’s financial landscape, offering a comprehensive suite of services spanning investment banking, wealth management, fixed income and stockbroking. The company has earned a reputation for combining deep market expertise with a client-centric approach, enabling individuals, corporates and institutions to navigate evolving market opportunities with confidence.

The international recognition comes at a time when First Capital continues to strengthen its market presence through innovation, strategic execution and a relentless focus on excellence. By maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and governance, the company remains well-positioned to support investors and businesses while contributing to the advancement of Sri Lanka’s financial sector.
