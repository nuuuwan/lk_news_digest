Likuid Spaces at One Galle Face  redefining premium workspaces in Colombo

Perched high above the city within Shangri-La’s One Galle Face Office Tower, Likuid Spaces stands as a reflection of Colombo’s new era of work  one defined by creativity, connection, technology, and design excellence.

First unveiled in mid-2023 with its exclusive Executive Wing spanning 10,000 square feet, Likuid Spaces at One Galle Face has become a sought-after destination for professionals and teams seeking more than just an office  a place where ideas grow and collaborations thrive. Likuid Spaces has now added a further 6,000 square feet in its newly completed Phase 2, bringing the total footprint to over 16,000 square feet of thoughtfully curated workspace.

Recognized as one of the most automated workspaces in Sri Lanka, Likuid Spaces integrates intelligent access systems, digital booking technology, and seamless service management to create a workspace experience that is as effortless as it is inspiring.

Located within Colombo’s most prestigious business and lifestyle address, Likuid Spaces fosters a vibrant professional community through curated events, creative partnerships, and a dedicated community management team  connecting founders, creators, and global businesses in one inspiring ecosystem.

Founded by Shuaib Yusoof, Likuid Spaces has been instrumental in shaping Sri Lanka’s modern workspace culture offering elevated environments that blend design, technology, and community for startups, creators, and multinational teams alike.

“Likuid Spaces has always been about more than just a workspace,” said Shuaib Yusoof, Founder of Likuid Spaces. “It’s about creating places where ideas grow, people connect, and businesses thrive. Our expansion at One Galle Face is another step toward that vision  a space that balances sophistication with creativity, right in the heart of Colombo.”

Designed across expansive ocean-view floors, Likuid Spaces at One Galle Face offers a mix of private suites, collaborative lounges, meeting rooms, and breakout zones  all enhanced by integrated technology for an elevated user experience.
