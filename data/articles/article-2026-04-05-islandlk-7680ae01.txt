Singer Sri Lanka partners with Kapruka for online expansion

Singer Sri Lanka PLC has formed a strategic partnership with Kapruka Holdings PLC to list its consumer electronics and homeware on the Kapruka Partner Central platform.

The collaboration ensures price parity, with all Singer products sold at the same retail prices as physical showrooms. Kapruka’s network will serve over one million expatriate customers and local shoppers, offering reliable delivery and service.

Singer COO Vajira Tennakoon cited shifting consumer behavior online, while Kapruka’s Himali Silva said the platform helps established brands reach digital audiences. The move enhances online access to genuine Singer products across Sri Lanka.
