You will be given several Sri Lankan news articles from the past week. Write a concise, cohesive editorial digest in the style of The Economist, output as a JSON list. Each list element must be a dict of the form:

```json
{
  "title": "...",
  "body": "..."
}
```

**Titles** must read like headlines from a reputable newspaper (think *Financial Times* or *The Economist*) — precise, active, and newsy — but when read in sequence, they should form a narrative arc that shows how the week's events connect and build on one another. Titles must contain only verifiable facts, no opinion or editorialising.

**Body** paragraphs must add context and nuance beyond the headline. Do not use bullet points. Explain any non-obvious acronyms or jargon on first use. Use emojis purposefully and sparingly where they aid scannability (e.g. 💰 for fiscal matters, ⚡ for energy). Stick strictly to verifiable facts; do not speculate or editorialize.

The digest as a whole should read as a coherent account of the week, not a list of unrelated summaries.
